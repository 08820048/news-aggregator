Asia-Pacific markets reversed gains on Thursday as investors assessed [U.S. President Donald Trump's](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/01/trump-address-nation-iran-live-updates.html) address to the nation on the Iran war.

During his speech, Trump reiterated that the U.S. objectives in Iran were almost met and said that "we have all the cards" in the conflict. He also said that Washington will hit Iran "very hard" over the next two to three weeks.

Early Wednesday stateside, Trump [claimed](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/01/trump-iran-war-ceasefire.html) that Iran's "New Regime President" had asked the U.S. for a [ceasefire](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/04/01/iran-irgc-nvidia-appple-attack-threat.html), a claim that Tehran has denied.

Trump added that the U.S. will "consider" the offer only once the [Strait of Hormuz](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/27/trump-strait-of-hormuz-iran-war.html) was "open, free, and clear," he said on [Truth Social](https://truthsocial.com/@realDonaldTrump/posts/116329512466946656).

Trump previously said he was willing to end the U.S. military campaign against Iran even if the Strait of Hormuz remained closed, according to [a report](https://www.wsj.com/world/middle-east/trump-iran-war-strait-of-hormuz-ee950ad4) by the Wall Street Journal.

South Korea's [Kospi](https://www.cnbc.com/quotes/.KS11/) dropped 4.25%, leading Asian losses, and the small-cap Kosdaq was down 4.71%, both the indexes opened more than 1% higher.

Japan's [Nikkei 225](https://www.cnbc.com/quotes/.N225/) was down 2.3% after Trump's address, while the Topix fell 1.5%.

Australia's [S&P/ASX 200](https://www.cnbc.com/quotes/.AXJO/) started the day in positive territory, but was also down 1.11%.

Hong Kong's Hang Seng index fell 1.08% after the speech, while the CSI 300 index on mainland China lost 0.77%.

U.S. stock futures fell, with S&P 500 futures and Nasdaq-100 futures down over 1%. Dow futures were down 439 points, or 0.94%.

Overnight in the U.S., the [S&P 500](https://www.cnbc.com/quotes/.SPX/) advanced 0.72%, and the [Nasdaq Composite](https://www.cnbc.com/quotes/.IXIC/) gained 1.16%. The [Dow Jones Industrial Average](https://www.cnbc.com/quotes/.DJI/) added 0.48%.

_—CNBC's Lisa Kailai Han and Sean Conlon contributed to this report._
