The [Pentagon](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/04/pentagon-blacklist-anthropic-defense-tech-claude.html) on Monday identified the seventh U.S. service member to have died in the [Iran war](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/09/the-us-iran-war-is-the-biggest-oil-supply-disruption-in-history.html) as 26-year-old Army Sgt. Benjamin Pennington.

Pennington, a resident of Glendale, [Kentucky](https://www.cnbc.com/id/10000570), was seriously wounded March 1 during an Iranian strike at [Prince Sultan Air Base in Saudi Arabia](https://www.afcent.af.mil/News/Photos/?igtag=Prince%20Sultan%20Air%20Base).

He died on Sunday from his wounds, according to the Defense Department.
