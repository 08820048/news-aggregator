The Aaoyun power bank has three outputs: one 2.2ft 22.5W USB-C cable, one 22.5 USB-C port and one USB-A port. A simple LED meter lets you know approximately how much battery you have left. The built-in USB cable loops back into the body and plugs into the available USB-C port when not in use. Aaoyun claims that it is strong enough to serves as a mini-lanyard.
