## Capcom Spotlight: All the big news from Capcom's new games showcase

   ![Rudy from Monster Hunter Stories 3: Twisted Reflection wearing the Capcom ](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/mhst3_catcomworks.jpg?&fit=crop&w=1600&h=900)Image: Capcom

Capcom's latest games showcase, [Capcom Spotlight](https://www.polygon.com/capcom-spotlight-games-showcase-march-2026-how-to-watch/), had some great news for fans looking forward to the developer-publisher's 2026 lineup. That starts with the promising [_Pragmata_](https://www.polygon.com/game/pragmata/40733/), which got a new release date on Thursday — Capcom moved the game up a week from its previously planned April 24 launch.

But that's not all; Capcom revealed new details on its upcoming slate, including another look at next year's [_Mega Man: Dual Override_](https://www.polygon.com/mega-man-dual-override-capcom-game-awards-tga/), what's next for _Street Fighter 6_, and new trailers for _Onimusha: Way of the Sword_ and _Monster Hunter Stories 3: Twisted Reflection_.

Read on for the biggest announcements from the latest Capcom Spotlight.

## Pragmata release date

In a surprise twist, Capcom changed the release date of _Pragmata_, first announced in 2020, one more time. The game is now coming one week earlier than previously expected. Capcom showed off new footage of the action-adventure third-person puzzle-shooter, as well as fancy new pre-order skins, during its Capcom Spotlight.

_Pragmata_ is coming to Nintendo Switch 2, PlayStation 5, Windows PC, and Xbox Series X on April 17. For the Nintendo Switch 2 version, Capcom is also releasing an amiibo based on Diana.

## Mega Man: Dual Override

Mega Man franchise producer Shingo Azumi and _Mega Man: Dual Override_ producer Hiroyuki Minamitani revealed one of the Robot Masters that will appear in next year's mainline Mega Man game. The twist is that this particular Robot Master was designed by fans, and the Mega Man development team has to choose from six designs (out of some 10,000) that qualified as finalists. Up for contention as a Mega Man: Dual Override boss are Valve Man, Juggle Man, Recycle Man, Cactus Man, Cleanser Man, and Sweeper Woman. You can see concept art of each (and silhouettes for the other Robot Master bosses) in the gallery above.

## Street Fighter 6

Alex, the pro wrestling-flavored fighter who first appeared in _Street Fighter 3_, comes to _Street Fighter 6_ on March 17 as part of the Year 3 character pass. Also coming soon are the revealing, swimsuit-themed Outfit 4 costumes for the _Street Fighter 6_ cast. On Thursday, Capcom gave fans a peek at new costumes for Elena and Dee Jay that show ample amounts of skin.

## Onimusha: Way of the Sword

    ![Onimusha_Key_Art_-_16×9](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/onimusha_key_art_-_16x9.jpg?q=49&fit=crop&w=825&dpr=2)Image: Capcom

Capcom released a new overview trailer for _Onimusha: Way of the Sword_ on Thursday, which you can watch on [YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=foOtNKru5_0) for a look at the game's early Edo-period Kyoto setting, and the intense swordplay action. _Onimusha: Way of the Sword_ will be released sometime in 2026 for PlayStation 5, Windows PC, and Xbox Series X.

## Monster Hunter Stories 3: Twisted Reflection

Ryozo Tsujimoto, producer of the Monster Hunter series, revealed a launch trailer and cosmetics for the upcoming _Monster Hunter Stories 3: Twisted Reflection_. Linking save data from previous Monster Hunter titles will unlock layered armor bonuses, including the Hakum Rider Outfit, Mahana Rider Outfit, Kamura Garb, and the Hope Layered Armor set for the protagonist. Connecting a Capcom ID unlocks the Catcom Work outfit for Rudy.

_Monster Hunter Stories 3: Twisted Reflection_ will be released on March 13 for Switch 2, PS5, Windows PC, and Xbox Series X.

## Mega Man Star Force Legacy Collection

Producer Shingo Azumi detailed online features in the _Mega Man Star Force Legacy Collection_, which will include casual, friend, and ranked matches. Players will be able to matchmake across multiple games included in the collection, and even “rent” decks that will give players access to cards they may not have acquired in the main game.

_Mega Man Star Force Legacy Collection_ arrives on March 27 for Nintendo Switch, PlayStation 4, PlayStation 5, Windows PC, Xbox One, and Xbox Series X.
