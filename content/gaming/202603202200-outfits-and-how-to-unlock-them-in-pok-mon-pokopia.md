If you want to **customize** your Ditto in [_Pokémon Pokopia_](https://www.polygon.com/pokemon-pokopia/), you can find **outfits** and **hairstyles** around the deserted island to swap in to. Lots of these outfits will look familiar, as they're based off of gym leaders and trainers from previous games.

All cosmetics are found as some of the "human records" around the island. They may appear as **magazines**, **tablets**, **books**, or **newspapers** that have a yellow sparkle on the ground. Interact with them and you'll get these outfits added to your collection.

Once you find and unlock an outfit, you can use the pieces separately, too. This means you can mix-and-match the Team Rocket hat with the Super Nerd button-up, if you'd like. Just **interact with a mirror**, and you'll be able to swap your look to whatever you want.

Notably, there are some cosmetics you can find in set locations on the main island in _Pokopia_, but some can only be found on the randomized [Dream Islands](https://polygon.com/pokemon-pokopia-dream-islands-drifloon-dolls/). We've separated the two into sections below.

Here's **where to find the various outfits and hairstyles in _Pokémon Pokopia_**.

## Dream Island outfits and hairstyles in Pokopia

There are also outfits and hairstyles you can unlock on **Dream Islands**. However, Dream Islands are random, and since you can only access one per day, you'll just have to test your luck to unlock them. You can time travel to get faster access to more islands, if you want to hunt for cosmetics faster.

We usually find the cosmetics behind a breakable rock wall inside of caves, in an area with a bookshelf and boxes, so keep your eye out for those **breakable, brown rock blocks**.

    ![A bookshelf, box, and book in a cave in Pokopia](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/obs64_2026-03-20_17-53-21.jpg?q=49&fit=crop&w=825&dpr=2)Image: Koei Tecmo, Game Freak/Nintendo, The Pokémon Company via Polygon

These seem to be **somewhat rare**, as we've traveled to dozens of islands at this point, but we've only found a few cosmetics. (We've literally found all the [legendary Pokémon](https://www.polygon.com/pokemon-pokopia-legendary-mythical-how-to-find-unlock/) on these islands, but only a handful of cosmetics.) We'll update this guide as we find more.
