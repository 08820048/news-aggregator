## Blizzard teases first look at Overwatch's next hero

   ![Overwatch lineup season 2 hero 51 teaser artwork](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/ow_2026_lineup_season2.jpg?&fit=crop&w=1600&h=900)Image: Blizzard Entertainment

The next hero coming to the [_Overwatch_](https://www.polygon.com/overwatch/) roster will be fully revealed on April 8 at 9 a.m. PDT, Blizzard Entertainment [announced](https://x.com/PlayOverwatch/status/2039009850277912908) Tuesday. The developer teased a first look at the game's next Damage-role character, currently known only as "Hero 51," hinting at her in-game abilities.

We've been able to see Hero 51 in silhouette for a couple of months now, but the character's full art offers a much clearer look at her kit. She's armed with a pronged weapon that looks like an energy-based sniper rifle, and she's paired with a drone that resembles a flying fish. She sports extremely long pink and brown braids, and her military-style uniform makes her look not unlike [Widowmaker dressed as Cammy from Street Fighter](https://www.polygon.com/trailer/599851/overwatch-2-street-fighter-6-collab-skins-price/) — or simply [Cammy with pants](https://www.polygon.com/2018/11/20/18105376/street-fighter-5-resident-evil-costumes-cammy/).

A logo on her vest indicates that she's a member of [Helix Security International](https://overwatch.blizzard.com/en-gb/news/20877886/talon-involvement-confirmed-in-previous-attack-on-helix-facility/), a private security firm funded by the United Nations. Helix ran the maximum security prison that once housed Akande Ogundimu, aka Doomfist, who was freed by Reaper. Pharah is a former member of Helix.

Hero 51 is slated to join _Overwatch_ with the launch of season 2, which is expected to kick off on April 15.

Blizzard relaunched _Overwatch_ in February, dropping the 2 from the game's title and adding five new heroes to the roster: Domina (Tank), Anran (Damage), Emre (Damage), Jetpack Cat (Support), and Mizuki (Support). Blizzard plans to add a total of [10 new heroes to the game in 2026](https://www.polygon.com/overwatch-2026-new-heroes/).

The teaser for Hero 51 follows an announcement from Blizzard and webcomic company Webtoon on a new expansion of _Overwatch_'s lore, which will continue the game's current Reign of Talon storyline.
