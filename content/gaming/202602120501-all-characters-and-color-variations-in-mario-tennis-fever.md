[_Mario Tennis Fever_](https://www.polygon.com/mario-tennis-fever/) boasts one of the largest rosters ever seen in a Mario Tennis title with **38 playable characters** and **20 color variations**. Like in other Mario sports games, you'll start out with a set of characters that are available as soon as you launch the game, but there will be another set that you'll need to unlock through a series of challenges. _Mario Tennis Fever_ has streamlined the unlocking process, but there are a few unlocks that are a bit tricky.

Here's **how to unlock every character and color variation in _Mario Tennis Fever_**, along with each character's play style and description.
