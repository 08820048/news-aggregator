Prep your wallets for the latest seasonal Steam sale

## Steam's spring sale starts this week, with discounts on some of the biggest indies and AAA games

   ![A ship in No Man's Sky](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/steam-spring-sale-2026.jpg?&fit=crop&w=1600&h=900)Image: Hello Games

Valve announced the Steam Spring Sale start date with a brief trailer that teases some of the hundreds of discounts planned. The 2026 Steam Spring Sale begins on Thursday, March 19 at 10 a.m. PDT/1 p.m. EDT and runs through Thursday, March 26 at the same time.

Valve's sale teaser picks out a dozen or so [PC games](https://www.polygon.com/pc/) that will headline the sale, along with a quick look at what to expect from the deep discounts section, a selection of popular games at prices much lower than usual. The trailer goes heavy on ["friendslop" games](https://www.polygon.com/friendslop-games-are-good-actually/) — popular co-op and multiplayer games like _RV There Yet_, _Raft_, _Sons of the Forest_, and _Yapyap_ — but there's a broad range of indies and AAA games as well. Some standouts are:

-   _Unbeatable_
-   [_Dave the Diver_](https://www.polygon.com/reviews/23777236/dave-the-diver-review-management-sim-steam-pc/)
-   _Avatar: Frontiers of Pandora_
-   _No Man's Sky_
-   [_Pathologic 3_](https://www.polygon.com/pathologic-3-is-the-best-in-the-series/)
-   _Manor Lords_
-   _Phasmophobia_
-   [_Strange Antiquities_](https://www.polygon.com/strange-antiquities-hands-on-impressions/)

And the deep discount section will include:

-   _Resident Evil 3 Remake_
-   _Fallout: New Vegas_
-   _Star Wars: Jedi — Survivor_
-   _Vampyr_
-   _Metro: Last Light Redux_
-   _Danganronpa 2: Goodbye Despair_

Along with a lot more. Steam's seasonal sales feature discounts on literally thousands of games from hundreds of developers and publishers, and this one will be no different. Just keep your expectations in check for Steam Deck discounts, as Valve is still [struggling with shortages](https://www.polygon.com/steam-deck-out-of-stock-ram-shortages/) thanks to the [RAM crisis](https://www.polygon.com/ram-crisis-gdc-2026-analysis/) affecting supply.
