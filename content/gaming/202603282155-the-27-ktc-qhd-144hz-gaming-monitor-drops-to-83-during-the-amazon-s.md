### 27" KTC H27S5C 2560x1440 (QHD) Curved 144Hz Gaming Monitor

First off, the KTC H27S5C is a 27" curved monitor featuring a 2560x1440 (QHD) resolution. Nearly every other monitor within this price range has a lower 1080p resolution. A 27" 1440p monitor averages out to a pixel density of 109ppi, which is great for both gaming and general workstation use where you need to look at a lot of text. In addition to that, the monitor features a 144Hz refresh rate. Again, you'd be lucky to find a sub-$100 monitor with higher than 60Hz, maybe 100Hz-120Hz if you score a good deal. The VA panel has 129% sRGB and 110% DCI-P3 color gamut coverage for accurate color reproduction. Connectivity options include DisplayPort and HDMI.

If you're considering a budget monitor, chances are you're not pairing it with a super high-end graphics card. If that's the case, you definitely don't want to get a 4K monitor because it will overly tax your PC. A 1440p monitor is much less demanding on your GPU. It can be paired with a gaming PC that's equipped with an RTX 5060 Ti (16GB) or AMD Radeon RX 9060 XT (16GB) to still achieve a consistent 60fps-144fps frame rate in most games.
