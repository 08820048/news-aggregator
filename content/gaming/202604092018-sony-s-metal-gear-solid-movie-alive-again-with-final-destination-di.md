## The Metal Gear Solid movie is back on with a hot horror directing duo in charge

   ![Artwork of Solid Snake and Gray Fox from the original Metal Gear Solid from Yoji Shinkawa.](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/chorus/uploads/chorus_asset/file/13619889/mgs.jpg?&fit=crop&w=1600&h=900)Image: Konami

Sony Pictures is giving a [_Metal Gear Solid_](https://www.polygon.com/game/metal-gear-solid/7341/) movie another run, with [_Final Destination: Bloodlines_ directing duo Zach Lipovsky and Adam B. Stein](https://www.polygon.com/horror/602031/final-destination-bloodlines-ending-explained-directors-spoilers-erik-death/) tackling a new live-action adaptation of the tactical stealth action game franchise. According to [The Hollywood Reporter](https://www.hollywoodreporter.com/movies/movie-news/metal-gear-solid-movie-works-1236560289/), Lipovsky and Stein landed the gig directing _Metal Gear Solid_ thanks to a fresh first-look deal at Sony, where they're also working on an animated movie based on Spider-Man anti-hero Venom.

"_Metal Gear Solid_ was nothing short of a groundbreaking cinematic masterpiece that forever revolutionized video games," Lipovsky and Stein said in a statement published by THR. "We are thrilled and honored to bring Hideo Kojima’s iconic characters and unforgettable world to life."

Lipovsky and Stein's filmography to date is eclectic. In addition to last year's franchise-reviving [_Final Destination: Bloodlines_](https://www.polygon.com/horror/599421/final-destination-bloodlines-directors-interview-special-imax-version/) and the [2018 sci-fi thriller _Freaks_](https://www.polygon.com/recommends/599522/freaks-2018-stranger-things-final-destination-bloodlines-connections/), the pair have worked on kid-friendly fare like _Kim Possible_ for Disney and _Fraggle Rock_ for Apple. Lipovsky does have a video game adaptation under his belt already, though. He directed the 2015 straight-to-Crackle [_Dead Rising: Watchtower_](https://www.polygon.com/2015/1/22/7872235/dead-rising-movie-trailer-watchtower/), an adaptation of Capcom's tongue-in-cheek zombie horror franchise that starred Jesse Metcalfe and featured [Rob Riggle as Frank West](https://www.polygon.com/2014/10/20/7021957/dead-rising-watchtower-movie-frank-west-rob-riggle/).

The directing duo has also reportedly written for Chris Columbus' [_Gremlins 3_](https://www.polygon.com/gremlins-3-movie-release-date-2027/), where they'll split the difference between gruesome horror and kid appeal.

The father-son team of Avi Arad and Ari Arad are producing the new _Metal Gear Solid_ movie. They've been trying for more than a decade to get a _Metal Gear Solid_ flick made, including [a notable previous attempt with director Jordan Vogt-Roberts (_Kong: Skull Island_) attached to direct](https://www.polygon.com/2014/6/3/5777494/metal-gear-solid-movie-director-jordan-vogt-roberts/) and [Oscar Isaac eyed to star](https://www.polygon.com/2020/12/4/22153421/metal-gear-solid-movie-solid-snake-actor-oscar-isaac-jordan-vogt-roberts/). That version isn't happening anymore, but it wouldn't be the first failed attempt to adapt MGS; Hideo Kojima himself announced plans for a live-action _Metal Gear Solid_ movie as far back as 2006.

Kojima hasn't been involved with the Metal Gear franchise for more than a decade now. He [left Konami in 2015](https://www.polygon.com/2015/12/15/10220372/hideo-kojima-new-studio-leaves-konami/) to form a new studio, Kojima Productions, which is best known for the [Death Stranding](https://www.polygon.com/game/death-stranding/39186/) games. There's a good chance that Kojima's _Death Stranding_ movie will beat the latest attempt at _Metal Gear Solid_ to theaters. A24 is deep into producing its _Death Stranding_ adaptation, with _A Quiet Place: Day One_ and _Pig_ director [Michael Sarnoski at the helm](https://www.polygon.com/movies/554015/death-stranding-movie-director-plot-hideo-kojima/).

There's no release date, writer, or casting details for _Metal Gear Solid_ yet. But with more and more video game-to-movie adaptations in the works, and many [netting big box office returns](https://www.polygon.com/the-super-mario-galaxy-movie/), maybe Sony Pictures can actually make _Metal Gear Solid_ happen this time. After all, it's the company that [finally made the Legend of Zelda movie a reality](https://www.polygon.com/23951355/zelda-movie-live-action-nintendo-sony/).
