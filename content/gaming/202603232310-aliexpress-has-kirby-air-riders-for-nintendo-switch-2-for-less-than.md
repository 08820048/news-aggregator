### Kirby Air Riders (Switch 2)

On the surface, Kirby Air Riders is a kart racer similar to Mario Kart World but with a different cast of characters including Kirby, Waddle Dee, King Dedede, and Meta Knight. However, they're actually quite different. While both are racing games, Mario Kart World focuses more on the racing mechanics whereas Kirby Air Riders puts more focus on the battle mechanics. Kirby Air Riders has a dedicated single player mode with an actual story called Road Trip. It also has a really fun City Trial mode where you get to explore a big city to collect power-ups and customize your vehicle across a total of 150 different challenges. My opinion is that you should own both games.
