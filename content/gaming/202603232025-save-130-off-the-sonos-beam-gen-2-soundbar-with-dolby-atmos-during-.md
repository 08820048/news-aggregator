### Sonos Beam Gen 2

The Sonos Beam Gen 2 soundbar is a 26-inch long, 5.0-channel soundbar that houses one tweeter and four mid-woofers paired with five Class-D digital amplifiers. There's no space for a woofer, but there are three passive radiators that do a good job of enhancing the lower frequencies for natural, balanced sound. The Sonos Beam doesn't have physical up-firing speakers but it does support Dolby Atmos. According to Sonos, "advanced processing creates phantom height channels for Dolby Atmos content to immerse you in a multidimensional soundstage."

Some other nifty features include built-in microphones for smart functionality with voice control, room calibration using your mobile device via Trueplay, a "Speech Enhancement" setting to boost voice frequences and clarify dialogue, and both 802.11n (2.4GHz and 5GHz) and HDMI eARC connectivity.
