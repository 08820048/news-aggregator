"**Mad Ellie and the Vault of the Damned**," a [_Borderlands 4_](https://www.polygon.com/game/borderlands-4/443248/) **DLC** and **story pack**, is releasing soon with a long list of additions including a new playable character. Launched last year, the [fourth entry](http://www.polygon.com/borderlands-4-review/) in the series has already received a few updates, like the premium Bounty Packs, but this story pack will be the first of its kind.

Although vault hunters must always be prepared to improvise, you should consider preparing yourself and setting an alarm for **when** **the _Borderlands 4_ DLC** **will be released in your time zone**.

* * *

## Borderlands 4 DLC release time in your time zone

_Borderlands 4_ DLC, "Mad Ellie and the Vault of the Damned," will be released on **Thursday, March 26 at 4 p.m. EDT**, as announced by the [official X account](https://x.com/Borderlands/status/2034657459168796899). Below, see when that is in your time zone:

-   **1:00 p.m. PDT on Thursday, March 26** for the west coast of North America
-   **4:00 p.m. EDT on Thursday, March 26** for the east coast of North America
-   **5:00 p.m. BRT on Thursday, March 26** for Brazil
-   **8:00 p.m. GMT on Thursday, March 26** for the U.K.
-   **9:00 p.m. CET on Thursday, March 26** for western Europe
-   **5:00 a.m. JST on Friday, March 27** for Japan
-   **7:00 a.m. AEDT on Friday, March 27** for the east coast of Australia

## What to expect from 'Mad Ellie and the Vault of the Damned'

_Borderlands 4_'s first story pack update is a massive update. Players who have acquired either the **Super Deluxe Edition** or the **Vault Hunter Pack** will immediately have access to "Mad Ellie and the Vault of the Damned," but others will have to purchase the DLC separately.

    ![A Borderlands 4 "Mand Ellie and the Vault of the Damned" DLC content map](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/b4_servicepost_releasetime_dlc_content.jpg?q=49&fit=crop&w=825&dpr=2)Image: Gearbox Software/2K Games

The star of this DLC is the new vault hunter **C4SH**. Classified as a rogue, this brand-new playable character comes with an intricate set of abilities that reflects the robot's gambler nature. When it uses its Action Skill "Sleight of Hand," C4SH draws random cards from its deck to cast spells, for example.

The "Mad Ellie and the Vault of the Damned" update is also adding a new map zone called "**The Whispering Glacier**," which will be available after you complete the "A Lot to Process" main mission in the Fadefields. However, [2K](https://borderlands.2k.com/borderlands-4/news/story-pack-1/) recommends reaching level 13 or higher before heading here.

Since this is a Borderlands game, no update would be a real one without new enemies to shoot and new items to loot. Story Pack 1 adds **18 pieces of Legendary Gear**, **three Pearlescent weapons**, **two boss fights,** and **16 minibosses** for you. Lastly, there are a ton of new cosmetics for weapons, vehicles, and the vault hunters themselves.
