## Disney Believe will be the fourth Wish-class ship following the Wish, Treasure, and Destiny.

![Adam Bankhurst Avatar](https://assets-prd.ignimgs.com/avatars/4ec7dfcfe4b0ca04b5bca47a/wanu8zLq_400x400-1588309801243.jpg?crop=1%3A1&width=21&format=jpg&auto=webp&quality=80)

Updated

: Mar 18, 2026 9:14pm UTC

Disney Cruise Line has revealed that its next ship is officially called the Disney Believe. Furthermore, it confirmed it will set sail in late 2027 with experiences inspired by Encanto, Frozen, Snow White and the Seven Dwarfs, Moana, and The Little Mermaid.

The news was revealed during [Disney's annual shareholders meeting,](https://thewaltdisneycompany.com/news/2026-annual-shareholders-meeting-remarks/) where new CEO Josh D'Amaro shared that the Disney Believe will honor the "dreamers and doers who dare to pursue their own happily ever after."

[

](https://assets-prd.ignimgs.com/2026/03/18/believe-1773868088339.png)

The Disney Believe, which is all about promise and possibilities, is the ninth Disney Cruise Line ship and the fourth Wish-class ship. It also follows the enchantment-themed Disney Wish, the adventure-themed Disney Treasure, and the heroes and villains-themed Disney Destiny.

Last year, [we had the honor of attending the Steel Cutting ceremony for the Disney Believe](https://www.ign.com/articles/construction-has-officially-begun-on-disney-cruise-lines-fourth-wish-class-ship-as-disney-celebrates-with-its-steel-cutting-ceremony) in Papenburg, Germany, which marks the special moment when construction officially begins on the vessel. It was a surprise addition to our journey [to learn how the Disney Destiny and the other Disney Cruise ships are built](https://www.ign.com/articles/disney-destiny-float-out-meyer-werft-cruise-ship-heroes-villains-marvel) by an incredible team.

The Disney Believe, and the Disney Adventure that just had its Maiden Voyage out of Singapore on March 10, are part of a huge expansion by Disney Cruise Line. By 2031, there are plans to have the fleet grow to 13 ships, including a brand-new class of ships in 2029 and an agreement with Disney and Oriental Land Co. Ltd. (OLC) to introduce Disney cruise vacations to Japan in the same year.
