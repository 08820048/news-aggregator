Two-day event includes exclusive reveals, trailers and playable games on show floor

         ![IGN Live logo](https://assetsio.gnwcdn.com/ign-live_ugyEV7X.jpg?width=570&quality=85&format=jpg&dpr=3&auto=webp)

Image credit: IGN Entertainment

IGN Entertainment, the parent company of _GamesIndustry.biz_, has put tickets on sale for consumer event IGN Live, which returns to Los Angeles on 6-7 June.

The event includes two days of livestream showcases including exclusive game reveals, new trailers and celebrity interviews, and a showfloor featuring hundreds of playable games.

Playable titles include THQ Nordic's RPG reboot Gothic Remake, and celebrity guests will include voice talent Roger Craig Smith (Sonic the Hedgehog, Batman, and more) and Patricia Summersett (Princess Zelda, Zyssyk from Star Wars Outlaws, and others).

Further announcements will be made closer to the event.

Producers are still accepting submissions for the livestream inclusion, developers and publishers [can apply here](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSe__1fWg7mdsCyXWCXcRpuB1grYhyuGFdM9reqh5FxSvo0deg/viewform).

Audience figures for [the 2025 event](https://www.gamesindustry.biz/ign-live-reveals-full-schedule-ahead-of-this-weekends-event) included 119 million video views, 258 million social impressions, and a reach of more than 204 million, alongside thousands of attendees to the live event.

Industry participants included Xbox, Sega, Ubisoft, and 2K Games, while entertainment guests included [Last of Us TV show lead Craig Mazin, Euan MacGregor, and the cast of Critical Role](https://www.ign.com/articles/the-biggest-reveals-from-ign-live-2025).

IGN Live 2026 will be streamed live across IGN platforms including the IGN homepage, Twitch and YouTube.

The event itself takes place from June 6 to 7 at the Magic Box @ The Reef; public tickets are [are on sale here](https://www.ign.com/live/#tickets-section).
