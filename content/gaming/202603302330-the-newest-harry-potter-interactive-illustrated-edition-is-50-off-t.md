The fourth Harry Potter Interactive Illustrated edition was just released in October of 2025 but it's already 50% off. The latest clippable coupon takes an additional $5 off, dropping the price down to $24.10 for a limited time.

This is the lowest price we've ever seen on this book, but it's far from the first time it's been discounted. I started [tracking the price of this book last year](https://www.ign.com/articles/new-harry-potter-book-illustrated-edition-where-to-buy) when preorders first went live and it was already on sale back then. With the Amazon coupon, it's now priced closer to the other three books in the series.

### Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire (Interactive Illustrated Edition)

This particular illustrated edition of the books is part of the 'interactive' series originally created by MinaLima. After the MinaLima editions were officially discontinued, the project was taken up by a new duo with Karl James Mountford illustrating and Jess Tice-Gilbert doing the papercraft. Presumably this duo will be taking over the final books in the series as well, but we haven't heard any news about book five just yet.

There are also various [different Amazon coupons](https://www.ign.com/wikis/amazon-prime/How_to_Find_Amazon_Coupons) available on the previous three books, but the ['3 for 2' sale officially ended over the weekend](https://www.ign.com/articles/books-are-included-in-a-new-buy-2-get-1-free-deal-amazon-spring-sale). I'd suggest picking up a copy of this book right now if you're just adding this book to your existing collection, but waiting for the '3 for 2' sale to return if you're looking to buy the full set in 2026. Those types of promotions are usually the [best time to buy illustrated collections](https://www.ign.com/articles/best-time-to-buy-books-and-comics) with the biggest overall discount.

### Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince: The Illustrated Edition

Although we don't have any sort of release date for the next interactive illustrated edition, we _do_ know that a [new illustrated edition is releasing in 2026](https://www.ign.com/articles/harry-potter-and-the-half-blood-prince-illustrated-edition-where-to-buy). This one doesn't include papercraft, but it is a big deal for anyone who's been collecting the Jim Kay illustrated editions over the years. The new book features illustrations from Levi Pinfold, who took over the project years after Jim Kay stepped away from it.
