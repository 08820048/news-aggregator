The **"Fragmented Logs" quest** in [_Arc Raiders_](http://www.polygon.com/game/arc-raiders/443837/) takes place entirely on Stella Montis, and while it doesn't need to all be completed in a single run, it's definitely possible if you steer clear of Shredders. Each location you need to visit for this quest is teeming with them though, from the Robotic Sandboxes to the Cultural Archives, so you should come prepared.

Here's a step-by-step guide to completing "Fragmented Logs" in _Arc Raiders,_ including where to find the out-of-service terminal, the conduit backrooms, and the main frame data terminal in the service backrooms.

* * *

## 'Fragmented Logs' quest steps in Arc Raiders

Here are all three steps you must complete for "Fragmented Logs" in _Arc Raiders_ in any order:

-   In either of the Robotic Sandbox control rooms, repair the out-of-service terminal with one electrical component.
-   Enable the power in any of the conduit backrooms near the Robotic Sandboxes.
-   Boot the main frame data terminal in the Cultural Archives service backrooms.

Since the step to repair the out-of-service terminal requires an electrical component, I'd strongly recommend you do not attempt this quest with a free loadout, lest you get stuck searching for an electrical component for too long. Instead, you should **bring an electrical component in from your stash**.

* * *

## Where to repair the out-of-service terminal in the Robotic Sandbox control room

    ![The left-hand side of the image shows the player in Arc Raiders standing in front of the out-of-service terminal that needs repairs while the right-hand side of the image shows the location marked on the in-game map with a star.](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/arc-raiders-fragmented-logs-out-of-service-terminal.jpg?q=49&fit=crop&w=825&dpr=2)Graphic: Ford James/Polygon |Source images: Embark Studios via Polygon

Stella Montis is a maze, that's no secret. As a result, it's easy to get hung up searching the Robotic Sandboxes for the out-of-service terminal, when instead you need to specifically look for the control room, which is between the two of them on the map. It's easy to miss the fact that this is actually a named location.

Head inside the control room and you should find a terminal to repair with your electrical component. The one I used was on the south side, facing Robotic Sandbox B.

* * *

## Where to enable the power in any of the conduit backrooms

    ![The left-hand side of the image shows the player in Arc Raiders standing in front of the objective while the right-hand side of the image shows the location marked on the in-game map with a star.](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/arc-raiders-fragmented-logs-conduit-backrooms.jpg?q=49&fit=crop&w=825&dpr=2)Graphic: Ford James/Polygon | Source images: Embark Studios via Polygon

The conduit backrooms are also near the Robotic Sandboxes, and the one I used is east of Robotic Sandbox A. You're looking for a small room off to the side, and inside you'll find a power switch with yellow electrical tape on the terminal. Interact with it to enable the power.

* * *

## Where to boot the main frame data terminal in the Cultural Archives service backrooms

    ![The left-hand side of the image shows the player in Arc Raiders standing in front of the objective while the right-hand side of the image shows the location marked on the in-game map with a star.](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/arc-raiders-fragmented-logs-cultural-archives.jpg?q=49&fit=crop&w=825&dpr=2)Graphic: Ford James/Polygon | Source images: Embark Studios via Polygon

Finally, head to Cultural Archives. This location is on the far east side of Stella Montis, and the service backrooms are near the middle. On the map, you'll see a small section that juts out, away from the archives themselves. Head in here and you can find a terminal with a prompt to bring it online at the back.

That's all you need to do! It sounds simple, but Stella Montis truly is a hellscape of Shredders and PvP, so completing this quest is easier said than done.
