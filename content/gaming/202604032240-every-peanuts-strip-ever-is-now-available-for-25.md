## Every Peanuts strip ever is available for $25 via Humble Bundle

   ![The cover of Fantagraphics Books' The Complete Peanuts: Volume 15, featuring Charlie Brown with a huge smile on his face](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/04/cp3-1.jpeg?&fit=crop&w=1600&h=900)Image: Fantagraphic Books

Sign in to your Polygon.com account

As part of [Fantagraphics’](https://www.fantagraphics.com/) ongoing [50th-anniversary celebration](https://comicon.com/2026/03/24/fantagraphics-celebrates-50-years-and-announces-new-titles/), the comics publisher is teaming up with Humble Bundle to provide Charlie Brown fans with access to every _Peanuts_ comic book and strip published between 1953 and 2000. Given that there are [43 books in this bundle](https://www.humblebundle.com/books/collected-peanuts-fantagraphics-books) (though eight-book and four-book bundles are also available), that’s an astonishingly extensive catalog.

    ![A Peanuts strip depicts Lucy making a large snowball. Charlie Brown and three other boys mock her for its size, so she uses it as a bowling ball to knock them all down.](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/04/screenshot-2026-04-03-at-5-23-57-pm.png?q=49&fit=crop&w=825&dpr=2)Image: Fantagraphics

In the full bundle, you’ll find Peanuts Every Sunday strip collections organized by the year they were published, as well as classic themed collections like _Snoopy vs. the Red Baron_ and _Waiting for the Great Pumpkin_. As with previous bundles, these are digital books, available for download on your computer, smartphone, e-reader, or other reading device.

    ![Three Peanuts comic strips, with Snoopy writing on a typewriter, catching a stray soccer ball, and sharing a blanket with Linus](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/04/screenshot-2026-04-03-at-5-29-10-pm.png?q=70&fit=crop&w=825&dpr=1)Image: Fantagraphics

As is tradition with Humble Bundle, a portion of each sale benefits a non-profit. In this case, proceeds from sales will go to [Canine Companions](https://canine.org/), which works to enhance the lives of people with disabilities by providing them with trained service dogs, free of charge — a worthy cause that Snoopy himself would likely approve of.

The Collected Peanuts Book Bundle will be available until April 16, 2026.
