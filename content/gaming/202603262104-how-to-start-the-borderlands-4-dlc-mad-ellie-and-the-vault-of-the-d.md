Get your guns! It's time to start [_Borderlands 4_](https://www.polygon.com/game/borderlands-4/443248/)'s **DLC "Mad Ellie and the Vault of the Damned."** This is BL4's first story pack, and it brings not only a new character, but more quests to complete, enemies to kill, and guns to loot in a brand-new zone.

However, if you are as lost as Claptrap, it might be hard to even find your way to the DLC. Below, you'll find a complete rundown on **how to start** **_Borderlands 4_****'s DLC "Mad Ellie and the Vault of the Damned."**

* * *

## How to start the 'Mad Ellie and the Vault of the Damned' DLC in Borderlands 4

Before you start _Borderlands 4_'s "Mad Ellie and the Vault of the Damned" DLC, you must, of course, own the DLC, and, **for characters created before the DLC's release**, complete the "**A Lot of Process**" mission, which is the fourth main quest.

    ![A montage showing where players can create a new character at level 13 in Borderlands 4 to start the DLC](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/borderlands4_guide_dlc_howtostart_mainmenu.jpg?q=49&fit=crop&w=825&dpr=2)Graphic: Paulo Kawanishi/Polygon | Source images: Gearbox Software/2K Games via Polygon

Alternatively, you can create a new character that will start at level 13. To do so, start a new game and select the "Mad Ellie and the Vault of the Damned" option. After you choose your desired character, a cutscene will play and you'll find yourself in the Whispering Glacier. As this character has skipped story missions and levels, you'll start with 12 skill points, four guns, a grenade, and a repkit.

    ![A montage using two Borderlands 4 images showing where players must go to start the game's new DLC](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/borderlands4_guide_dlc_howtostart_quest_11.jpg?q=49&fit=crop&w=825&dpr=2)Graphic: Paulo Kawanishi/Polygon | Source images: Gearbox Software/2K Games via Polygon

For those looking to start the DLC on an old character, it's recommended that you're at least level 13. Head to **Zadra's Backup Plan** in **Dissected Plateau area** to start the **"An Invitation" quest**.

Interact with the **alien pillar** inside to watch DLC's initial cutscene where you'll see Moxxi receiving a mysterious letter. Then, you'll be teleported to the **Whispering Glacier**, the new zone, where you'll receive the quest **"On Icebound Shores Abandoned**" and start the Mad Ellie and the Vault of the Damned DLC.
