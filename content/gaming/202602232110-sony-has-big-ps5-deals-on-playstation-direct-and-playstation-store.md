If you've been waiting for a reason to fill up your [PlayStation 5](https://www.polygon.com/playstation/) storage (or even finally snag a [PlayStation VR 2 headset](https://www.polygon.com/reviews/23601086/playstation-vr-2-review-ps5-release-date-price-horizon-vr-call-of-the-mountain/)), Sony Interactive Entertainment has made it a perfect time to strike. There are solid deals at the digital PlayStation Store and on physical PS5 games through the PlayStation Direct website. PS5 owners can also get big savings on PS5 accessories, including on the DualSense controller, Pulse wireless earbuds, and console covers.

In terms of physical games, basically the best of the best of the PS5 software library is on sale through [PlayStation Direct](https://direct.playstation.com/en-us/deals), including, but not limited to, the following titles:

-   _Marvel's Spider-Man 2_ - $29.99 (was $69.99)
-   _Helldivers 2_ - $29.99 (was $39.99)
-   _Stellar Blade_ - $39.99 (was $69.99)
-   _Death Stranding 2: On the Beach_ - $49.99 (was $69.99)
-   _God of War Ragnarök_ - $19.99 (was $69.99)
-   _The Last of Us Part 1_ - $29.99 (was $69.99)
-   _The Last of Us Part 2 Remastered_ - $29.99 (was $49.99)
-   _Demon's Souls_ - $29.99 (was $69.99)
