[_Crimson Desert_](https://www.polygon.com/game/crimson-desert/40719/) is a vast open world game that takes you and your Greymane buddies through Pywel, a medieval realm full of knights, bandits, witches, and even cats. You'll start off as Kliff, who is looking to get the gang back together, but you'll need to take a pit stop in Hernand first. As you meet up with your friends, you'll encounter numerous puzzles both on land and in the Abyss, take on difficult battles, and perhaps pet a dog or two.

## Who needs a map? Actually...

Pywel is _huge_ and extremely easy to get lost in. Everyone will have a different playthrough due to the world's sheer size and options, but that can also be daunting. To help you get started, check out our [beginner's guide](http://www.polygon.com/crimson-desert-beginner-tips-tricks/) and [interactive map](https://www.polygon.com/map/crimson-desert-interactive-pywel-hernand-demeniss-pailune-delesyia/) to help you get accustomed to the world. If you want a sense of direction, we've got you covered on [where you should go first](http://www.polygon.com/crimson-desert-where-to-go-first/).

## Prepare for a quest

_Crimson Desert_ is fairly hands-off with its tutorials, which can make its quests and puzzles a little difficult to solve. Right from the get-go, you'll be thrown into the [Abyss](http://www.polygon.com/crimson-desert-mine-ore-where-to-find-iron-copper/) and [unraveling ancient ruins](https://www.polygon.com/crimson-desert-reunion-quest-walkthrough-ancient-ruins-puzzle-unravel-secrets/), but they're no easy task. Before you set off, you should [gear up](http://www.polygon.com/crimson-desert-craft-how-to-weapons-armor/), stock up on [food](http://www.polygon.com/crimson-desert-how-to-cook-improvise-recipe/), and pick the [best skills](http://www.polygon.com/crimson-desert-best-skills-level-up-first-stamina-flight-axiom-force-stab-keen-sense/) for you. If you'd rather explore, make sure to look out for [abyss cressets and abyss nexuses](http://www.polygon.com/crimson-desert-fast-travel-how-to/), so you can traverse the world quickly.
