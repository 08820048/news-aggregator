One of the last big box-marts of its kind, Target has a dedicated LEGO section. If you're ever looking for a recently retired set that's been taken off the LEGO Store, your local Target is a possible bet. Target is also great for its clearance sales, where they'll reduce the prices by 40% or even 50% just to clear inventory.

But too many LEGO fans sleep on Target's online retail, which currently has some great deals to compete with [Amazon's Big Spring Sale](https://www.ign.com/articles/amazon-spring-sale-2026-best-deals). Here are the best ones I found. But hurry, because the sale ends tomorrow (Tuesday, March 31)!
