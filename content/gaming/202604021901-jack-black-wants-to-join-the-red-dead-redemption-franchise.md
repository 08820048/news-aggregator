Jack Black has found a surprising acting niche over the past decade. Starring in [_A Minecraft Movie_](https://www.polygon.com/review/551004/minecraft-movie-jack-black-jason-momoa/), [_The Super Mario Bros. Movie_](https://www.polygon.com/reviews/23669990/super-mario-bros-movie-review-references/), and a modern take on [Jumanji](https://www.polygon.com/2019/12/11/21011044/jumanji-the-next-level-review-dwayne-johnson-danny-devito-kevin-hart-jack-black-awkwafina/) — oh, and [_Borderlands_](https://www.polygon.com/review/24216309/borderlands-movie-review-cate-blanchett/) — Black has become the go-to guy if you want to adapt your video game into a movie. With [_The Super Mario Galaxy Movie_](https://www.polygon.com/super-mario-galaxy-movie/) now in theaters, a third Jumanji entry coming this Christmas, and [a Minecraft movie sequel in the works](https://www.polygon.com/minecraft-movie-sequel-release-date/), Black shows no signs of slowing down in his particular lane.

So what does Jack Black want to do next, specifically when it comes to future video game movie work? Well, he wouldn't say no to getting a role in a Red Dead Redemption adaptation, he told Polygon.

Speaking with Black during a press event for _The Super Mario Galaxy Movie_, I asked Black where he wants to go next in his video game movie career arc.

"It's no secret that [I love _Red Dead Redemption_ and _Red Dead Redemption 2_](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7wwWTLtpAM4)," Black said, "but I think those guys are on record — the Houser brothers — as having zero interest in turning any of their games into movies. Which is a shame, because of all the video games out there, theirs are the most cinematic. It would be, like, _too_ easy to make them into great movies. But yeah, that's where I'd like to party if they ever wanna party with me."

Added Black, "Hey Housers? Dan, Sam, I'm waiting for your call."

While [Dan Houser is no longer with Rockstar Games](https://www.polygon.com/2020/2/4/21123170/dan-houser-departs-rockstar-games-red-dead-gta/), the company he co-founded with brother Sam, Terry Donovan, Gary Foreman, and Jamie King in 1998, maybe Sam Houser will be open to the suggestion, once he gets [_Grand Theft Auto 6_](https://www.polygon.com/game/grand-theft-auto-6/40846/) out the door. But Black has another Red Dead role in mind, should _Red Dead Redemption: The Motion Picture_ never come to pass.

"_Red Dead Redemption 2_ takes place around the turn of the century, right?" Black said, "There was a character around that time named Jack Black, who wrote an autobiography called _You Can't Win_. I would kinda like to play Jack Black in _Red Dead Redemption 3_."

While Rockstar hasn't announced plans for a third Red Dead Redemption game, it never hurts to ask, as Black's _The Super Mario Galaxy Movie_ costar [Donald Glover can attest](https://www.polygon.com/super-mario-galaxy-movie-donald-glover-yoshi/). In fact, we have a Jack Black ask of our own: [Put the man in a Yakuza movie](https://www.polygon.com/jack-black-yakuza-diaper-fetishist/).
