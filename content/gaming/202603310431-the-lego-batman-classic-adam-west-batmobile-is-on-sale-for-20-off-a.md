If you're a fan of Batman, camp humor, terrible puns, or all three, the LEGO Batmobile (the one from the the 1966 Adam West-led television show) is currently on sale at Target. It normally costs $150, but until the end of Tuesday (3/31), it only costs $120 – a 20% discount off the sticker price.

### LEGO Batman: The Classic TV Series Batmobile

Decked with its signature red-trimmed fins and molded windshield, the Batmobile comes with a placard perfect for display and a LEGO minifigure of Batman with a Batarang, which can fit inside in the car or be posed alongside it. Opening the trunk will reveal the Bat-Computer.

The Adam West TV show ran for only three seasons, but it's become a beloved classic in the decades since. Despite its ridiculous premises, the actors took the proceedings seriously, never letting on that they were in on the joke. The Neal Hefti theme song, multiple catchphrases ("Holy \[insert descriptive\], Batman!" "Same Bat-time, same Bat-channel!" and meme-able gifs keep the show alive to the current day.

**Act fast you want to buy; the Target sale ends on Tuesday, March 31st!**
