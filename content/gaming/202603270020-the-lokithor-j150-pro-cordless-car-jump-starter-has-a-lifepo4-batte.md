### Lokithor J1350 Pro 1,350-Amp LiFePO4 Cordless Car Jump Starter

Let's talk about the best feature about the Lokithor J150 Pro. Unlike every other cordless jump starter in this price range, it has a lithium iron phosphate (LiFePO4) battery. LiFePO4 batteries have safer chemistry; their thermal stability gives them a much smaller chance of catching on fire than a lithium-ion battery. They will also retain more charge when left alone for long periods of time, and their total lifespan is longer than lithium batteries (usually at least 10 years). Finally, if you're in cold weather country, LiFePO4 batteries operate at lower temperatures more efficiently than lithium-ion.

If you're worried about the "low" 1,350-amp rating, don't be. A 1,000-amp jump starter is good enough for the vast majority of cars here in the US, which have anywhere between 1.6L and 3L gas engines. This jump starter is perfectly fine for most trucks as well, unless maybe if you're rocking a souped up beast with a V8 or higher engine.

The 7,000mAh battery capacity means you can get by with several jump starts before needing a recharge, although I would still recommend keeping your battery topped up. You can also use it as a battery backup for your smartphone in a pinch, thanks to its USB-C port with 30W of Power Delivery. USB cables are also included.
