In [_Crimson Desert_](https://www.polygon.com/game/crimson-desert/40719/), the **Fallen Kingdom armor** is the perfect set if you want a fierce look combined with good lightning resistance. It features vibrant red and green tones along with an imposing helm. While in the early stages of the game, this is a great armor to chase since all of its parts are located within Hernand. You just need to know where to look.

Here's **how to get the Fallen Kingdom** **armor in** **_Crimson Desert_**, showing the exact location of each piece.

* * *

## How to get the Fallen Kingdom armor in Crimson Desert

The journey to obtain all five pieces of the Fallen Kingdom armor is a long one, and it will take you to the ice peaks of Everfrost in Hernand. Because of that, prepare yourself with either ice-resistant gear like the [Frostcursed Plate](https://www.polygon.com/crimson-desert-frostcursed-plate-armor-locations/) set, or food that can protect you from the cold.

You should also unlock the **Stab** skill from the blue section skill tree. When you use it, your character performs a thrust attack, moving forward and being able to **pass through waterfalls**.

Below we've listed where you can find each piece of the Fallen Kingdom armor in _Crimson Desert_.

* * *

### Fallen Kingdom gloves location in Crimson Desert

The Fallen Kingdom gloves are located inside the **Starlight Storm cave**, **west of the city of Hernand**. The cave can be found below the **"NA" in the "Hernand" text** on the map.

    ![A montage showing the location of the Fallen Kingdom gloves](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/04/crimsondesert_guide_fallenkingdom_gloves.jpg?q=49&fit=crop&w=825&dpr=2)Graphic: Paulo Kawanishi/Polygon | Source images: Pearl Abyss via Polygon

Since the entrance is blocked by a waterfall, hold the Stab skill until your sword starts to glow, and release it to pass through the waterfall. Inside, you'll find a chest with the Fallen Kingdom gloves.

* * *

### Fallen Kingdom boots location in Crimson Desert

Like the gloves, the Fallen Kingdom boots are hidden inside the **Nightstorm cave**. To find it, head **north from the Starlight Storm cave**, and just a bit above the **"N" of the Hernand text** on the map. Here, you'll find a river. Follow it upward to get to the Nightstorm Cave.

    ![A montage showing the location of the Fallen Kingdom boots](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/04/crimsondesert_guide_fallenkingdom_boots.jpg?q=49&fit=crop&w=825&dpr=2)Graphic: Paulo Kawanishi/Polygon | Source images: Pearl Abyss via Polygon

The entrance is hidden behind another waterfall, and you'll need to use Stab to get through it. Once inside, open the chest containing the boots.

* * *

### Fallen Kingdom leather cloak location in Crimson Desert

    ![A montage showing the location of the Fallen Kingdom cloak](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/04/crimsondesert_guide_fallenkingdom_cloak.jpg?q=49&fit=crop&w=825&dpr=2)Graphic: Paulo Kawanishi/Polygon | Source images: Pearl Abyss via Polygon

The Fallen Kingdom leather cloak is hidden inside the **Icemoor Castle Ruins**, which is located to the **west of the Nightstorm Cave**. You will find it above the **"RN" of the "Hernand" text** on the map.

    ![A montage showing the location of the Fallen Kingdom cloak](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/04/crimsondesert_guide_fallenkingdom_cloak_2.jpg?q=49&fit=crop&w=825&dpr=2)Graphic: Paulo Kawanishi/Polygon | Source images: Pearl Abyss via Polygon

Once you arrive, climb the stairs **heading north**. At the highest northern room, **turn right** to find a path blocked by a large stone. Climb it and **look south** to find an **opening in the wall**. Crouch to enter and use **Force Palm** to break the wood blocking the path. Enter the next room to find a chest with the leather cloak.

* * *

### Fallen Kingdom leather helm location in Crimson Desert

    ![A montage showing the location of the Fallen Kingdom helm](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/04/crimsondesert_guide_fallenkingdom_helm.jpg?q=49&fit=crop&w=825&dpr=2)Graphic: Paulo Kawanishi/Polygon | Source images: Pearl Abyss via Polygon

The Fallen Kingdom leather helm can be found at the **Sanctum of Benediction**, which is **southwest of the Icemoor Castle Ruins** and j**ust southwest of the "E" in the Hernand text** on the map. However, to access the sanctum, you may need to unlock **Elowen**, the second witch, in Chapter 5. Additionally, the sanctum is overrun with members of the **Antumbra Order** and you need to defeat enough of them to clear the area before you can try to obtain the helm.

    ![A montage showing the location of the Fallen Kingdom helm](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/04/crimsondesert_guide_fallenkingdom_helm_1.jpg?q=49&fit=crop&w=825&dpr=2)Graphic: Paulo Kawanishi/Polygon | Source images: Pearl Abyss via Polygon

After you've defeated enough enemies, head north and **climb the stairs to reach the upper level** where you'll find the sanctum's large mechanism. **Head west** then **turn left twice** to find yourself in front of a **closed door at the top of a flight of stairs**.

    ![A montage showing the location of the Fallen Kingdom helm](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/04/crimsondesert_guide_fallenkingdom_helm_2.jpg?q=49&fit=crop&w=825&dpr=2)Graphic: Paulo Kawanishi/Polygon | Source images: Pearl Abyss via Polygon

Light the lantern to the right of the stairs to open the door and find the chest with the Fallen Kingdom leather helm inside.

* * *

### Fallen Kingdom leather armor location

The armor piece of the Fallen Kingdom set is hidden inside the **Frozen Soul Cavern**, **southeast of the Sanctum of Benediction**. The cave can be found **south of the "ER" in the "Hernand" text** on the map.

    ![A montage showing the location of the Fallen Kingdom armor](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/04/crimsondesert_guide_fallenkingdom_armor.jpg?q=49&fit=crop&w=825&dpr=2)Graphic: Paulo Kawanishi/Polygon | Source images: Pearl Abyss via Polygon

To enter the cave, Stab through the waterfall. Inside, there's a chest containing the Fallen Kingdom leather armor.
