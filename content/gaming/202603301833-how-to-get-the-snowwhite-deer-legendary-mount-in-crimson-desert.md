With the **Snowwhite Deer** legendary mount [_Crimson Desert_](https://www.polygon.com/game/crimson-desert/40719/), you can travel long distances or climb steep hills without too much effort.

Just like Kliff and the other two protagonists, mounts have stats, and actions like running consume stamina. As a legendary mount, the Snowwhite Deer has better attributes than the regular horses, running faster and jumping higher than them. However, the real reason you might want to use this mount is its large stamina pool, which allows for longer periods of sprinting.

Obtaining this mount involves finding a mystic creature in the ice peaks of Pailune. If you're up for the challenge, here's **how to get the** **Snowwhite Deer mount in** **_Crimson Desert_****.**

## How to get the Snowwhite Deer mount in Crimson Desert

Before you hunt down the Snowwhite Deer mount in _Crimson Desert_, it's important to know that you'll need to have access to any [witch](http://www.polygon.com/crimson-desert-witch-where-to-find-location-abyss-gear-equip-hermit/) beyond Sylvia. You'll unlock Elowen after completing the "Demenissian Delegation" quest in Chapter 5, so make sure to progress in the story if you haven't met the Witch of Wisdom yet.

**To unlock the Snowwhite Deer mount** in _Crimson Desert_, you need the **Snowwhite Deer Antlers**, which is obtainable by slaying and skinning the Snowwhite Deer.

    ![A montage of two Crimson Desert images showing the location of the Snowwhite Deer legendary creature](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/crimsondesert_snowwhitedeer_map.jpg?q=49&fit=crop&w=825&dpr=2)Graphic: Paulo Kawanishi/Polygon | Source images: Pearl Abyss via Polygon

The Snowwhite Deer is located in **Pailune in the Silver Wolf Mountain area.** You can find it west of the **Sanctum of Solace**, near the **"A" in the Pailune text on the map**. The Snowwhite Deer is usually grazing along with other deer, and it will run as soon as you get near it. One way to prevent it from running away is to stagger it for a couple of seconds by hitting it with arrows. Keep shooting until you're close enough to use other weapons.

Once you've defeated the Snowwhite Deer, **skin it** to receive the Snowwhite Deer Antlers. You also get the "Throne of the White Night," which you can place in your house.

    ![A Crimson Desert screenshot showing the Snowwhite Deer Antlers item](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/crimsondesert_snowwhitedeer_antler.jpg?q=49&fit=crop&w=825&dpr=2)Image: Pearl Abyss via Polygon

Next, visit any witch, except for Sylvia in Hernand Witch's Lair, and select the "Craft Abyss Gear" option. Move to the "Special Items" tab by pressing C on the keyboard or R2/RT on the controller. The **Sigil of Solidarity (Snowwhite Deer)** will appear as a craftable item.

    ![A Crimson Desert screenshot showing a Witch crafting the Sigil of Solidarity](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/crimsondesert_snowwhitedeer_sigil.jpg?q=49&fit=crop&w=825&dpr=2)Image: Pearl Abyss via Polygon

After crafting the sigil, it will move to your inventory. Use it to unlock the Snowwhite Deer legendary mount. You can now call your new mount by selecting it on the character selection wheel (F1 on the keyboard or holding down the D-pad on controllers).
