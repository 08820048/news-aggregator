Finding the **unreachable village** in _[Crimson Desert](https://www.polygon.com/game/crimson-desert/40719/2/)_ is no mean feat, as the local fairy-like kids known as the Shai would rather drag your unconscious body through the forest than admit entry. Luckily, one of them will let you enter their mysterious village if you grant them a favor; to retrieve the Relic of Pororin from the "big oaf" who's stolen it.

Here's how to trigger "The Unreachable Village" quest and **how to obtain the Relic of Pororin** in _Crimson Desert_.

* * *

## How to find Pororin, the Unreachable Village, in Crimson Desert

To start "The Unreachable Village" quest in _Crimson Desert_, you must **approach Pororin's approximate location**, which, as seen on an unexplored map, is just south of the Goldleaf Guildhouse:

    ![The location of Pororin, the unreachable vilage, in Crimson Desert.](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/crimson-desert-unreachable-village-2.jpg?q=49&fit=crop&w=825&dpr=2)Graphic: Marloes Valentina Stella/Polygon | Source image: Pearl Abyss via Polygon

If you see colorful floating flower pots, you'll know you're close by.

Once there, the Shai will **knock you unconscious**, drag you away from the village, and leave you behind, but don't let that put you off; **approach Pororin two more times**. After you've regained consciousness the third time, a Shai called **Arkin** will call you over from a nearby tree. If you help her, she'll grant you access to the mysterious village.

Go to Arkin's next location to continue "The Unreachable Village," which is much further east, just across the Demeniss border. She's waiting on a lower cliff in the **Trembling Gorge**. While traveling east, don't get too close to Pororin or you'll get knocked unconscious again.

    ![The Trembling Gorge in Crimson Desert, where players must find the Relic of Pororin.](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/crimson-desert-unreachable-village-3.jpg?q=49&fit=crop&w=825&dpr=2)Graphic: Marloes Valentina Stella/Polygon | Source image: Pearl Abyss via Polygon

* * *

## How to retrieve the Relic of Pororin in Crimson Desert

Arkin will ask you to **retrieve the Relic of Pororin** from a "stubborn oaf." The only other hint you'll get is that this mystery creature is "really tall and passes through here all the time." It's an important remark, though, as it explains why you won't find anything if you simply visit the quest marker; the person who carries the Relic of Pororin isn't stationary.

In fact, he's a **walking tree**; an Ent, if you will. You can wait for him at the quest marker location, but it's faster and arguably more fun to track his route; he circles the Trembling Gorge in a clockwise direction. The picture below shows a rough sketch of this route. If you go counterclockwise, following the broken tree trunks that mark his path, you'll find him.

    ![The walking route of the tree who carries the Relic of Pororin in Crimson Desert.](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/crimson-desert-unreachable-village-6.jpg?q=49&fit=crop&w=825&dpr=2)Graphic: Marloes Valentina Stella/Polygon | Source image: Pearl Abyss via Polygon

To get the Relic of Pororin from the Ent, you must **climb on top of his head**. You can either literally climb them, glide from a nearby cliff, or use an upgraded Axiom Force to leap to the top.

    ![Kliff collects the Relic of Pororin in Crimson Desert to complete The Unreachable Village quest.](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/crimson-desert-unreachable-village-5.jpg?q=49&fit=crop&w=825&dpr=2)Graphic: Marloes Valentina Stella/Polygon | Source image: Pearl Abyss via Polygon

Once you've got the Relic of Pororin, return to Arkin (same location as before) to complete "The Unreachable Village." From now on, you can visit _Crimson Desert_'s village of Pororin anytime you like.
