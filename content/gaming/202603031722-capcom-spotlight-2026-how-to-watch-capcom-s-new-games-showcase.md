## A new Capcom Spotlight games showcase is coming this Thursday

   ![Pragmata](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/pragmata_lunar_cityscape_ss_16.jpg?&fit=crop&w=1600&h=900)Image: Capcom

Capcom, the publisher and developer behind [_Resident Evil Requiem_](https://www.polygon.com/resident-evil-requiem/), has announced it will hold a new Capcom Spotlight showcase on March 5. Now that Capcom's latest [survival-horror blockbuster is out in the wild](https://www.polygon.com/resident-evil-requiem-review/), the company can focus on its other upcoming games, including sci-fi puzzle-shooter [_Pragmata_](https://www.polygon.com/pragmata-game-preview-capcom-gamescom/) and role-playing game [_Monster Hunter Stories 3: Twisted Reflection_](https://www.polygon.com/monster-hunter-stories-3-preview/).

The first Capcom Spotlight of 2026 will stream on Thursday, March 5, starting at 2 p.m. PST/5 p.m. EST/10 p.m. UTC. The showcase is planned to run for 30 minutes.

In addition to _Monster Hunter Stories 3_ (out March 13) and _Pragmata_ (out April 24), Capcom promises updates on _Street Fighter 6_, _Mega Man Star Force Legacy Collection_, and _Mega Man: Dual Override_.

[_Street Fighter 6_](https://www.polygon.com/game/street-fighter-6/40838/)'s newest fighter, Alex, arrives on March 17. The one-time star of _Street Fighter 3: New Generation_ is the third character to be released as part of _Street Fighter 6_'s Year 3 Character Pass, which also includes Sagat, C. Viper, and the upcoming Ingrid, originally from _Capcom Fighting Evolution_. [Alex was motion-captured by professional wrestler Kenny Omega](https://www.polygon.com/street-fighter-6-alex-aew-kenny-omega-release-date-trailer/) — a fun fact.

_Mega Man Star Force Legacy Collection_, coming on March 27, bundles seven games from the action RPG Mega Man spinoff series:

-   _Mega Man Star Force Pegasus_
-   _Mega Man Star Force Leo_
-   _Mega Man Star Force Dragon_
-   _Mega Man Star Force 2 Zerker x Ninja_
-   _Mega Man Star Force 2 Zerker x Saurian_
-   _Mega Man Star Force 3 Black Ace_
-   _Mega Man Star Force 3 Red Joker_

Finally, [_Mega Man: Dual Override_ was announced](https://www.polygon.com/mega-man-dual-override-capcom-game-awards-tga/) at [The Game Awards 2025](https://www.polygon.com/game-awards-2025-reveals-news-winners) as the next mainline entry in the Mega Man franchise. The 12th Mega Man game will arrive in 2027 — the Blue Bomber's 40th anniversary — on Nintendo Switch, Nintendo Switch 2, PlayStation 4, PlayStation 5, Windows PC via Steam, Xbox One, and Xbox Series X. Hopefully, Capcom Spotlight will give us more concrete details on what's next for Mega Man.
