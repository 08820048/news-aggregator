## Epic is giving this D&D game a free Dragonlance DLC for a limited time

   ![Idle Champions Raistlin fron the Dragonlance novels in a red robe wielding a glowing magic staff](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/idle-champions-raistlin.jpg?&fit=crop&w=1600&h=900)Image: Codename Entertainment

A bounty of free DLC for an idle Dungeons & Dragons game is being made free on the Epic Games Store for a limited time starting today. Also on offer is a hilarious top-down shooter for the low price of $0.

[_Idle Champions of the Forgotten Realms_](https://www.polygon.com/videos/2018/5/9/17335332/idle-champions-forgotten-realms-free-good-cheap-games/) is an idle strategy management sim. As an auto-battler, your champions will fight on your behalf while you're not actively in the game. Free at Epic is [Raistlin's Renown Pack](https://store.epicgames.com/en-US/p/idle-champions-of-the-forgotten-realms), spotlighting the character from the [Dragonlance](https://www.polygon.com/dnd-dungeons-dragons-dragonlance-manganiello-hickman-weis/) novels. It contains over $100 in add-ons, including "Raistlin, Gale, Spurt, Briv, and Ellywick, along with 32 Platinum Chests each, two week-long potions, and an exclusive Baaz Draconian familiar." Raistlin's basic attack is the iconic Magic Missile, while his ultimate is Wheel of Flame, which circles enemies in a ring of fire, dealing an ultimate hit each second to trapped foes.

Also on offer is [_Turnip Boy Robs a Bank_](https://store.epicgames.com/en-US/p/turnip-boy-robs-a-bank-3fae0e). It follows up on the comedic and criminal adventures Turnip Boy got up to in _Turnip Boy Commits Tax Evasion_. While the first game's gameplay centered around puzzles, _Robs a Bank_ goes for something more high-octane; it's a roguelite top-down shooter. You'll team up with the Pickled Gang to relieve the vaults of the Botanical Bank of all they contain. Both Turnip Boy games are rated "Overwhelmingly Positive" based on thousands of Steam reviews.

If free content for _Idle Champions of the Forgotten Realms_ or the hilarious _Turnip Boy Robs a Bank_ catches your eye, all you need to do is log in to the [Epic Games Store](https://store.epicgames.com/en-US/) and claim them. They'll be free until Thursday, March 12 at 11 a.m. EST.
