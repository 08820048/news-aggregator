Writer-director John Krasinski's A Quiet Place returns in 2027 for _Part 3_, with stars Emily Blunt, Millicent Simmonds, Noah Jupe, and Cillian Murphy from [the second entry](https://www.polygon.com/movies/22459067/a-quiet-place-part-ii-review/) set to return, Krasinski revealed Monday in a surprise announcement.

Joining the returning cast are newcomers Jack O'Connell (_Sinners, 28 Years Later_), Jason Clarke (_Zero Dark Thirty_, _Pet Sematary_), and Katy O'Brian (_Love Lies Bleeding_, _The Mandalorian_), Krasinski also revealed. "So proud to be a part of this \[A Quiet Place\] family… old and new! Here we go!" Krasinski said in a post on [his Instagram account](https://www.instagram.com/p/DV83JgcjjoF/) that showed each actor's personalized movie scripts.

Like the previous mainline entries in the sci-fi horror thriller series, Krasinski will direct based on his script. He starred in the first _A Quiet Place_, which he co-wrote with Bryan Woods and Scott Beck. _A Quiet Place Part 3_ will be the fourth entry in the franchise, following 2024's pretty good prequel [_A Quiet Place: Day One_](https://www.polygon.com/reviews/24187509/quiet-place-day-one-review-prequel/). The franchise has grossed $900 million at the box office over the past eight years, and spawned a [so-so video game spinoff](https://opencritic.com/game/17489/a-quiet-place-the-road-ahead), _A Quiet Place: The Road Ahead_.

_A Quiet Place Part 3_ arrives in movie theaters on July 30, 2027. Production on the film is reportedly set to begin in New York this spring, according to [Variety](https://variety.com/2026/film/news/a-quiet-place-3-cast-emily-blunt-cillian-murphy-jack-oconnell-1236690148/).
