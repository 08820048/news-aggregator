## 2 more Studio Ghibli IMAX remasters are on the way

GKIDS is about to make U.S. and Canadian animation fans very happy. The distribution company is about to bring two more movies from [Studio Ghibli](https://www.polygon.com/23841933/studio-ghibli-gifts-toys-plushies-decor-music/), the globally revered animation house that gave viewers _My Neighbor Totoro_ and _Kiki's Delivery Service_, to IMAX theaters this year.

Hot on the heels of GKIDS' announcement that _Kiki's Delivery Service_ (set to debut on Mar. 13) would also be coming to US and Canadian IMAX theaters this year, _Whisper of the Heart_ and _The Secret World of Arrietty_ have both been announced for IMAX screenings on Apr. 21 and May 19, respectively. This means audiences will be able to get lost in the world of Ghibli in crisp 4K resolution, which, considering the extraordinary visuals Studio Ghibli always provides, makes this Brit very jealous of my U.S. and Canadian friends.

_Whisper of the Heart_ is a beautiful, feel-good coming-of-age classic from director Yoshifumi Kondo based on the original manga by Aoi Hiiragi and a screenplay and storyboards by Studio Ghibli co-founder Hayao Miyazaki. It follows the young girl Shizuku, who, after becoming enchanted by the Baron, an antique cat figurine, sets out on a life-changing adventure to pursue her true talent for storytelling. Tickets for _Whisper of the Heart's_ IMAX debut are [available now](http://www.fandango.com/whisper-of-the-heart-4k-2026-244728/movie-overview).

If you're seeking a smaller-scale adventure, _The Secret World of Arrietty_ is also a must-watch. Based on Mary Norton's _The Borrowers_, directed by Hiromasa Yonebayashi with a screenplay by Hayao Miyazaki, the film introduces a hidden world beneath the floorboards. In this tiny universe, Borrowers, including a brave young girl named Arrietty, live quietly. But when Shawn, a human boy, discovers her, a new story of friendship and adventure unfolds. The film features the voices of Moises Arias, Bridgit Mendler, David Henrie, Will Arnett, Carol Burnett, and Amy Poehler. You can [grab tickets](http://www.fandango.com/the-secret-world-of-arrietty-4k-2026-244745/movie-overview) for the IMAX debut of _The Secret World of Arrietty_ now.

Showings of both films include the original Japanese-language version with subtitles and the English-language dubbed versions.

IMAX and GKIDS announced in [early December](http://www.imax.com/pr/gkids-and-imaxr-announce-new-studio-ghibli-4k-restorations-debut) last year that their partnership would continue. _The Boy and the Heron_ was the first Studio Ghibli film to screen in IMAX format in 2023. Since then, [_Princess Mononoke_](https://www.polygon.com/new-4k-studio-ghibli-movies-2026-imax/) received a 4K IMAX release, and we already know there are even more Ghibli films on the way.

While it's unknown whether these IMAX debuts will reach European audiences, we would be surprised if that weren't the case. With a name as beloved as Studio Ghibli, it seems likely that it's only a matter of time before fans in more countries get the chance to watch these movies in the same 4K treatment.
