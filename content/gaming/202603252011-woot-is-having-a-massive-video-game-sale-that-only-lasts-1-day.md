### What's the Deal With Woot?

Woot is a legit retailer, in case you were wondering. I’ve ordered from it many times, and it often has excellent sale prices on video games and other items. As mentioned above, Woot is owned by Amazon, and you can actually log in and check out using your Amazon credentials, which is what I always do. If you have an Amazon Prime account, you’ll usually get free shipping from Woot as well.

Whatever you do, **don't forget to enter promo code SAVETWENTY at checkout**. That's a top-priority thing, because it saves you 20% on top of the sale price. That's what pushes these deals into extraordinary territory.
