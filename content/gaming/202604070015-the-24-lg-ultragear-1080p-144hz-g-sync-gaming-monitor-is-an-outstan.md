### 27" LG UltraGear 27G411A-B 1080p 144Hz Gaming Monitor

The LG UltraGear 27G411A-B is a 24" monitor with a 1920x1080 FHD resolution. That averages out to a pixel densitiy of 92ppi, which is good for games and decent for general workstation use. The monitor also features a native 120Hz refresh rate that can be overclocked to 144Hz and G-Sync compatibility. The IPS panel boasts wide viewing angles, 1ms response time, and 99% sRGB color gamut. Connectivity options include both DisplayPort and HDMI.

If you're considering a budget monitor, chances are you're not pairing it with a super high-end graphics card. If that's the case, you definitely _don't_ want to get a 4K monitor because it will overly tax your PC.

A 1080p monitor is much less demanding on your GPU. It can be paired with a gaming PC that's equipped with something as inexpensive as an RTX 5060 or AMD Radeon RX 9060 XT (8GB) and still achieve a consistent 60fps-144fps frame rate in most games.
