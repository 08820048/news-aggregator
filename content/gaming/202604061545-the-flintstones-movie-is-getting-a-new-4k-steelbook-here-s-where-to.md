The 1994 movie [The Flintstones](https://www.ign.com/movies/the-flintstones) – which starred a variety of famous faces, from John Goodman to Rick Moranis to Elizabeth Perkins and more – is finally making its way to 4K. For those interested in adding it to their physical media library, a new limited edition 4K steelbook (stylized here as "stonebook") is now available to preorder for as low as $32.96 at a few different retailers ([see it at Amazon](https://zdcs.link/aN3EGy?object_uuid=dee7eab1-e712-49e1-b2b6-7f2ce421cd9f&t=article)). This price is even a 34% discount from its MSRP of $49.98, which is a nice bonus.

There's still a couple of months to go before you can watch this ultra hi-def transfer at home, with a set release date of June 2, but that's not too far away. Head to the links below to preorder a copy for your collection. Be quick as well, just in case it sells out.

Alongside the film, this steelbook also comes with plenty of bonus features for fans to check out, which we've broken down below.
