### Sonos Era 100 Smart Speaker

The Era 100 is Sonos' most popular and most versatile speaker. Despite its compact size, the Era 100 houses two tweeters, a mid-woofer, and three class-D digital amplifiers to produce precise, distortion-free audio even at high volumes. The omnidirectional design allows it to project sound in all directions, which also makes positioning it much easier. The Era 100 also has a built-in microphone for smart functionality. At this price, it's cheaper getting this than the recently released Era 100 SL which takes out the mic. Because of its flexibility, the Era 100 can be used solo, as a multi-channel system with other Era speakers, or as satellites for a soundbar like an Arc or Arc Ultra.
