## Let the countdown begin to May 19.

![Hannah Hoolihan Avatar](https://assets-prd.ignimgs.com/avatars/5dacb56f933e880b27420a50/Enlight830-1629333774500.JPG?crop=1%3A1&width=21&format=jpg&auto=webp&quality=80)

Fans of James Cameron's Avatar films, it's now time to make a little extra space in your film library: [Avatar: Fire and Ash](https://www.ign.com/movies/avatar-fire-and-ash) is making its way to physical formats. There's quite a lot of options to choose from as well, including a [limited edition 4K steelbook](https://zdcs.link/Qm8Bv6?object_uuid=803a1047-d5cd-4a83-89cc-9474041f6788&t=article), [standard 4K](https://zdcs.link/zE86ln?object_uuid=803a1047-d5cd-4a83-89cc-9474041f6788&t=article), [Blu-ray](https://zdcs.link/98A8J1?object_uuid=803a1047-d5cd-4a83-89cc-9474041f6788&t=article), a [three-movie collector's edition on 4K](https://zdcs.link/QV53rg?object_uuid=803a1047-d5cd-4a83-89cc-9474041f6788&t=article), and a [three-movie Blu-ray collection](https://zdcs.link/9gXme0?object_uuid=803a1047-d5cd-4a83-89cc-9474041f6788&t=article).

The steelbook, 4K, Blu-ray, and Blu-ray collection are all set to be released on May 19 this year, but the 4K three-movie collector's edition appears to be coming out just a little bit later on June 16. Below, you can learn more about where each of these are available to preorder right now, alongside information on the many, many bonus features you'll get to watch, too.

## Preorder Avatar 3-Movie Collector's Edition

[![The Avatar 3-Movie Collector’s Edition (4K UHD + Blu-ray + Digital)](https://assets-prd.ignimgs.com/2026/04/07/the-avatar-3-movie-collectors-edition-4k-uhd-blu-ray-digital-1775565330843.jpg?width=220&format=jpg&auto=webp&quality=80)](https://zdcs.link/QV53rg?object_uuid=803a1047-d5cd-4a83-89cc-9474041f6788&t=article "The Avatar 3-Movie Collector’s Edition (4K UHD + Blu-ray + Digital)")

Out June 16, 2026

### The Avatar 3-Movie Collector’s Edition (4K UHD + Blu-ray + Digital)

If you're a big fan of these films and want to go all-out with a 4K purchase, this three-movie collector's edition sets you up with Avatar, Avatar: The Way of Water, and Avatar: Fire and Ash on 4K, Blu-ray, and digital. That's not all, though: it also comes with three Blu-ray bonus discs for each film, a collectible book, and a special letter from James Cameron that's only available in this collector's edition.

This is set to be released a little bit later than the other versions, on June 16.
