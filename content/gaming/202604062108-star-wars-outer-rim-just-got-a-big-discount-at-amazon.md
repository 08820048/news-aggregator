    ![Star Wars board game](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/04/star-wars-board-game.jpg?q=70&fit=crop&w=825&dpr=1)Image: Asmodee via Amazon

If you’ve ever dreamed of playing as morally gray characters like Han Solo, Lando Calrissian, the death-dealing droid IG-88 or even Boba Fett and Jyn Erso, then _Outer Rim_ will be right up your alley. The game eschews Jedi philosophizing and large-scale battles in favor of a quest to gain notoriety by any means necessary, whether you want to be a cheeky ne'er-do-well or a downright psychopathic bounty hunter. _Outer Rim_ lets you build your legend and upgrade your ship as you go.

Each session takes between two and three hours, and the game can accommodate anywhere from one to four players. With more than 1,600 reviews on Amazon, _Outer Rim_ is sitting pretty at an impressive rating of 4.8/5 stars. No one hates Star Wars as passionately as disgruntled Star Wars fans, so this narrative-driven strategy game is clearly striking the right notes with players.

If you’re a fan of _stuff,_ you’ll no doubt enjoy the volume of cards, boards, and everything else contained in here, too. (There's nothing quite like a board game full of pieces, is there?) _Outer Rim_ has an optional expansion, too, called _Unfinished Business,_ which popped up in our [recommended board game list back in 2022](https://www.polygon.com/23502142/best-board-games-2022/).
