Whether you're looking for fishing spots or dungeon entrances, we've got your back. Our **_No Rest for the Wicked_ interactive maps** will help you find fire pits, quests, chests, keys, and other helpful points-of-interest around the beginning areas (Shallows, Mariner's Keep, and Orban Glades), as well as The Nameless Pass and Marin Woods.
