How to solve the large golden disc puzzle

   ![A Crimson Desert screenshot showing Kliff facing the Trembling Woods puzzle](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/crimsondesert_guide_tremblingwoodspuzzle_header.jpg?&fit=crop&w=1600&h=900)Image: Pearl Abyss via Polygon

**The Trembling Woods puzzle** in [_Crimson Desert_](https://www.polygon.com/game/crimson-desert/40719/) is one of the many mysteries you'll find in the Ironwood and also one of the **ancient ruins puzzles**. Known as the **"Thread in the Forest"** challenges, these puzzles vary in form and complexity. Some, like the [Azure Moon Labyrinth](https://www.polygon.com/crimson-desert-azure-moon-labyrinth-puzzle-location-question-mark-witchwoods/), require you to explore the game's map to find the clues you need. Others will require you to strain your brain.

If you're having trouble figuring out the solution for this specific "Thread in the Forest" challenge, we can help. Here's **how to solve the Trembling Woods puzzle in** **_Crimson Desert_**.

* * *

## Trembling Woods puzzle location in Crimson Desert

    ![A montage of two Crimson Desert images showing the location of the Trembling Woods puzzle](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/crimsondesert_guide_tremblingwoodspuzzle_map.jpg?q=49&fit=crop&w=825&dpr=2)Graphic: Paulo Kawanishi/Polygon | Source images: Pearl Abyss via Polygon

The Trembling Woods is a region **southwest of The Ironwood** and **north of the Willow River**. Just south of the Trembling Gorge and Galepully, you'll find a mysterious energy, which is the question mark found on the map. Here, you'll encounter a **large golden disc** and **three turning wheels** in front of it. The right and the middle wheels have handles you can use to rotate them. The left wheel will have no handles, making it seemingly impossible to turn. However, it's possible if you have the **Stab skill** unlocked.

By completing this puzzle, a door will open to an **abyss cresset**, unlocking a new [fast travel station](https://www.polygon.com/crimson-desert-fast-travel-how-to/).

    ![A Crimson Desert screenshot showing Kliff in front of the Trembling Woods puzzle](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/crimsondesert_guide_tremblingwoodspuzzle_tip.jpg?q=49&fit=crop&w=825&dpr=2)Image: Pearl Abyss via Polygon

**If you just want an initial tip**: the golden disc is split into three layers. On one of them, there are **white symbols** that look a bit incomplete.

* * *

## How to solve the Trembling Woods puzzle in Crimson Desert

Finding the answer to the Trembling Woods puzzle requires testing and spending time rotating the wooden wheels. If you want to complete it as quickly as possible, follow the steps below.

While facing the disc:

1.  Move the **right mechanism clockwise** so the **white icons are aligned** with the inner circle, completing the images.
2.  Move the **middle mechanism counterclockwise** until the largest gold circle in the middle layer aligns with the star-like symbol.
3.  Use **Stab** (R1/RB and Y/Triangle or left shift and left-click) on the **left wheel** to stick your blade into it and move it clockwise for the top layer's two circles to align with the other symbols.

With all the layers in their correct positions, a short animation will play and the water in front of you will drain, revealing a **door on the lower level**. Drop down to collect the abyss cresset and unlock the fast travel station.
