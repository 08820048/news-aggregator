Nintendo drops prices on some must-play Mario games

## Super Mario games are on sale for Mario Day, including Nintendo's first Switch 2 discount

   ![Super Mario's head on a red background](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/02/mario-sports.jpg?&fit=crop&w=1600&h=900)Image: Nintendo

[Nintendo](https://www.polygon.com/nintendo/) is celebrating "Mario Day" — otherwise known as March 10 — with more than just [new Virtual Boy games for Nintendo Switch Online](https://www.polygon.com/nintendo-classics-mario-mar10-day-virtual-boy/). Select Mario games for Nintendo Switch and Switch 2 will be discounted at the Nintendo eShop and at select retailers as part of Mario Day festivities.

The list includes Nintendo Switch games _Super Mario 3D World + Bowser's Fury_ and _Super Mario Odyssey_, two games that are [highly influential on Illumination's upcoming _The Super Mario Galaxy Movie_](https://www.polygon.com/super-mario-galaxy-movie-trailer-easter-eggs/). Nintendo's Mario Day sales also include the first Switch 2 game from Nintendo to go on sale: _Super Mario Party Jamboree – Nintendo Switch 2 Edition + Jamboree TV_.

Physical versions of those games and more will be on sale from March 8-14 at select retailers, including Nintendo's U.S. stores and the [My Nintendo Store](https://www.nintendo.com/us/store/sales-and-deals/mar10-day-sale/). Deals on Mario games are already live at the My Nintendo Store and Nintendo eShop, if you're looking for digital games. (Savings on digital games come to an end on March 15 at 11:59 p.m. PST.)

While the Super Mario games on offer may not be the latest — [_Donkey Kong Bananza_](https://www.polygon.com/review/616250/donkey-kong-bananza-nintendo-switch-2/) and [_Super Mario Bros. Wonder_](https://www.polygon.com/reviews/23920680/super-mario-bros-wonder-review-nintendo-switch/) aren't included as part of the sales — the titles included are some of the greatest. The spectacular [_Super Mario Odyssey_](https://www.polygon.com/2017/10/26/16551724/super-mario-odyssey-review/), for example, landed at No. 3 on [Polygon's Games of the Year in 2017](https://www.polygon.com/2017-best-games/2017/12/14/16776852/super-mario-odyssey-best-games-2017-goty/). [_Super Mario 3D World + Bowser's Fury_](https://www.polygon.com/reviews/22273180/super-mario-3d-world-bowsers-fury-review-switch/) is a polished version of a Wii U co-op platformer with a brilliant open-world take on 3D Mario action. [_Super Mario RPG_](https://www.polygon.com/reviews/23960636/super-mario-rpg-2023-switch-remake-review/) and [_Mario vs. Donkey Kong_](https://www.polygon.com/reviews/24072246/mario-vs-donkey-kong-switch-review/) are great opportunities to play classic games, one from the Super NES era, one from the Game Boy Advance era, on modern platforms.
