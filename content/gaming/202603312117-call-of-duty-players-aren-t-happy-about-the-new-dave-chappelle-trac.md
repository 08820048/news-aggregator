_**Update (3/31 at 3:54pm PT) -**_ The [Call of Duty Studio Community Teams](https://x.com/CallofDutyCM/status/2039099135325012455?s=20) took to X/Twitter to share a bit more about its plans for 4/20, confirming there will be no events or limited-time modes and that the Black Ops 7 Half Baked Tracer Pack featuring Dave Chapelle will be the only celebration for the day.

"We know a lot of players enjoy them, but we've scaled back the 4/20 celebrations this year to a single bundle for fans of the Half Baked movie," the teams wrote. "No events or LTMs this time."

In 2025, [Call of Duty: Black Ops 6 featured such festivities for 4/20](https://www.ign.com/articles/call-of-dutys-ongoing-420-obsession-sparks-debate-among-fans-some-have-had-enough-others-really-want-that-seth-rogen-skin) as The Joint Operations Limited Time Mode, which added a +420 Score Bonus, 3rd person, Visual Impairment, Double Health, Hardcore, Paranoia Sounds, Low Gravity, Double Jump, and Increased Movement Speed.

Warzone also had a similar mode called High Trip Resurgence that challenged players to "smoke" the competition. This was all in addition to a High Art event with a Seth Rogen skin for the event pass, a Blaze of Glory event, and even a transformation of Nuketown into Blazetown.

_The original story follows._

Call of Duty fans are up in arms after Activision announced a first look at its upcoming [Black Ops 7](https://www.ign.com/games/call-of-duty-black-ops-7) Half Baked Tracer Pack featuring Dave Chappelle.

Details on the latest cosmetic bundle for the newest entry in the ongoing FPS franchise were revealed in a [Season 3 blog post](https://www.callofduty.com/blog/2026/03/call-of-duty-black-ops-7-season-03-battle-pass-blackcell-store-bundles) today. Its ties to the shooter’s [annual weed-inspired 4/20 content drop](https://www.ign.com/articles/call-of-dutys-ongoing-420-obsession-sparks-debate-among-fans-some-have-had-enough-others-really-want-that-seth-rogen-skin) have done little to ward off criticisms from those unhappy with its inclusion and the timing of its announcement.

[](https://assets-prd.ignimgs.com/2026/03/31/half-baked-tracer-pack-call-of-duty-1774989766722.png)

Call of Duty: Black Ops 7 Season 3 Half Baked Tracer Pack. Image courtesy of Activision.

Call of Duty has remained infamous for its use of out-there [crossover outfits and goofy skins](https://www.ign.com/articles/nicki-minaj-is-call-of-duty-season-5-snoop-dogg) for years, but talk around how Operator cosmetics have been handled throughout the franchise really heated up as post-launch support for Black Ops 6 arrived throughout 2025. As a result of fan feedback and as part of a commitment to stick to gritty realism, Activision confirmed last August that skins from previous entries would [no longer carry forward](https://www.ign.com/articles/amid-battlefield-6s-commitment-to-gritty-realism-activision-makes-shock-call-of-duty-change-for-black-ops-7-we-hear-the-feedback) to new ones.

“Black Ops 7 needs to feel authentic to Call of Duty and its setting,” it [said](https://www.callofduty.com/blog/2025/08/call-of-duty-community-update-august-2025) at the time. “That is why Black Ops 6 Operator and Weapon content will not carry forward to Black Ops 7.”

Although Activision almost immediately dipped its toes back into the water with [a Fallout collaboration](https://www.ign.com/articles/yes-its-true-fallout-is-coming-to-call-of-duty-black-ops-7) with Black Ops 7 Season 1, the Half Baked Tracer Pack takes a more comedic approach with content based on [Chappelle’s ‘90s stoner comedy of the same name](https://www.ign.com/movies/half-baked). Included in the bundle is a new Operator with two Thurgood-inspired skins, three themed weapon blueprints, an emote, finishing move, and more pot cosmetics. For many, it’s a step too far, as players have been especially quick to express their disappointment on social media.

"I don’t think a singular person on this earth was praying that the comedian Dave Chappelle would be added to Black Ops 7," one [X/Twitter user](https://x.com/itsHunterTV/status/2039036156869038148?s=20) replied.

"Ok so when is carry forward coming back now that we're doing silly skins again," [another](https://x.com/RexPowerCo1t/status/2039026510586150999?s=20) added.

The issue for some lies with the appearance of a comedic bundle less than one year after Activision expressed a desire to lean more into the grounded visuals that originally made Call of Duty popular. A [message](https://x.com/CallofDuty/status/2029969934730809611?s=20) posted by the series’ official account March 6, which stated fans would only see new collaboration bundles if “they are the right fit,” has only added insult to injury.

Meanwhile, Chappelle has remained a controversial figure for years as viewers have called out transphobic comments he’s made during specials like The Closer and The Dreamer. His joining Call of Duty would no doubt face criticism on any day, but the fact that the announcement of his inclusion arrives on International Transgender Day of Visibility has only added to the confusion and disappointment.

"Announcing a dave chappelle collab bundle, despite saying that you wouldn't make specifically that, on trans day of visibility of all days, sure is a choice," one [player](https://x.com/AnimuuStew/status/2039047270616662305?s=20) said.

"Why Dave Chapelle? And to announce it on Trans Visibility Day of all days? Even as a BO7 defender, I can’t defend this," another [commented](https://x.com/nndn010/status/2039050674588311922?s=20).
