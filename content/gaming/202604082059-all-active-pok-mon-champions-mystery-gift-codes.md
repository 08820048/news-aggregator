Redeeming [_Pokémon Champions_](https://www.polygon.com/pokemon-champions/)' **Mystery Gift codes** lets you get useful freebies to make your road to the top a bit easier. That sometimes includes [new Pokémon](https://www.polygon.com/pokemon-champions-how-to-get-more-pokemon/) without having to rely on [transferring from Pokémon Home](https://www.polygon.com/pokemon-champions-home-transfer-visit-use/) or random [recruitment](https://www.polygon.com/pokemon-champions-how-to-get-more-pokemon/), too. The Pokémon Works hasn't indicated how often it'll make _Pokémon Champions_ codes available, but the game did launch with one that has no expiration date.

Below, we list **all active _Pokémon Champions_ Mystery Gift** codes and explain how to redeem them.

* * *

## All active Pokémon Champions codes

    ![The Machamp you obtain via Mystery Gift code in Pokemon Champions](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/04/pokemon-champions-mystery-gift-codes.jpg?q=49&fit=crop&w=825&dpr=2)Image: The Pokémon Works/Nintendo, The Pokémon Company via Polygon

_Pokémon Champions_ launched with one Mystery Gift code.

-   **CHAMP10N** (Machamp)

The Machamp has the same stats, moves, and abilities for every player. It knows:

-   Dynamic Punch
-   Stone Edge
-   Ice Punch
-   Bullet Punch

Its ability is No Guard, which guarantees all of its moves will hit and all moves against it will hit. Once you collect your Mystery Gift Machamp, it'll automatically move to your box.

Note that you can't send Pokémon that you first obtained in _Champions_ to Pokémon Home. If you want a Machamp to use in another Pokémon game, you'll have to obtain it the usual way in that game.

## How to get Mystery Gifts in Pokémon Champions

    ![The Mystery Gift menu in Pokemon Champions](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/04/pokemon-champions-mystery-gift-codes-2.jpg?q=49&fit=crop&w=825&dpr=2)Image: The Pokémon Works/Nintendo, The Pokémon Company via Polygon

You'll gain access to _Pokémon Champions_' Mystery Gift menu after you complete the tutorial. From the main menu:

1.  Select "Submenu" (located on the bottom right of the screen).
2.  Scroll down to the Mystery Gift option.

As of _Pokemon Champions_' first season, The Pokémon Company is distributing gifts via serial codes. That may change in later seasons, but for now, your next steps are:

1.  Input your Mystery Gift code.
2.  Retrieve your reward from the in-game mailbox.

And then you're good to go.
