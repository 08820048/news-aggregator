[_Crimson Desert_](https://www.polygon.com/game/crimson-desert/40719/)'s continent of Pywel is a big ol' place, and it's easy to miss a lot of the best things the game has to offer if you don't know where to look. One of _Crimson Desert_'s favorite things is to not tell you anything about how the game works, like how to find [fast travel points](https://www.polygon.com/crimson-desert-fast-travel-how-to) and where to look for special abyss artifacts. Some of it is fine to find out on your own, but if you start lagging behind on abyss artifacts and [weapon upgrades](https://www,polygon.com/crimson-desert-craft-how-to-weapons-armor), you'll feel the effects sooner rather than later.

Our **_Crimson Desert_ interactive maps** show you where to find every important thing, and we mean _every_thing: faction quests, inns and vendors, including sketchy back-alley vendors for buying less-than-legal goods, bounty boards, dungeons, caves, resource nodes, like where to find [iron ore](https://www.polygon.com/crimson-desert-mine-ore-where-to-find-iron-copper), abyss cressets, and even the best spots to [cast your fishing line](https://www,polygon.com/crimson-desert-fish-how-to-fishing-rod-vendor).

Just starting out in _Crimson Desert_? We have guides to help you learn the [basics](http://www.polygon.com/crimson-desert-beginner-tips-tricks/) and refresh your memory on how some of its systems work. The world of Pywel is _huge_, so you might have a hard time deciding [where to go first](http://www.polygon.com/crimson-desert-where-to-go-first/). Along the way, make sure to learn a few life skills like [fishing](http://www.polygon.com/crimson-desert-fish-how-to-fishing-rod-vendor/), [logging](http://www.polygon.com/crimson-desert-trees-how-to-cut-timber/), [cooking](http://www.polygon.com/crimson-desert-how-to-cook-improvise-recipe/), and [mining](http://www.polygon.com/crimson-desert-mine-ore-where-to-find-iron-copper/), all of which can help you [make money](http://www.polygon.com/crimson-desert-how-to-make-money-fast-bank/).

We also have walkthroughs for main story quests like "[Abyss Without Balance](http://www.polygon.com/crimson-desert-abyss-without-balance-quest-walkthrough-abyss-puzzle-ethereal-pathway/)" and "[Familiar Curses](http://www.polygon.com/crimson-desert-familiar-curses-quest-house-of-healing-how-to-get-in/)," and puzzles in the Abyss like the [Root's End](http://www.polygon.com/crimson-desert-roots-end-abyss-puzzle-walkthrough/).
