The [Triple-i Initiative Showcase is back](https://www.polygon.com/triple-i-iniative-2026-announcement-interview/) for its third year in a row, taking place on Thursday, April 9, and will feature announcements for 40 games, including eight world premieres. Last year's showcase included announcements of hugely successful games including [_The Alters_](https://www.polygon.com/impressions/608350/alters-review-11-bit-studios/), [_Rematch_](https://www.polygon.com/guides/607583/rematch-beginners-tips-before-you-start/), [_Deep Rock Galactic: Survivor_](https://www.polygon.com/24074194/deep-rock-galactic-survivor-review-impressions/), and [_Vampire Survivors_](https://www.polygon.com/reviews/22927447/vampire-survivors-review-roguelike-bullet-hell-steam-pc-browser-8-bit-characters-power-ups/), which isn't too shabby of a lineup at all.

The 2026 Triple-i Initiative Showcase is 45 minutes long and will take place at **12 p.m. EDT** on Thursday. Here's how that looks in your time zone:

-   **9 a.m. PDT** for the west coast of North America
-   **12 p.m. EDT** for the east coast of North America
-   **1 p.m. BRT** for Brazil
-   **5 p.m. BST** for the United Kingdom
-   **6 p.m. CEST** for Western Europe
-   **1 a.m. JST on Friday, April 10** for Japan
-   **2 a.m. AET on Friday, April 10** for the east coast of Australia

As for what to expect this year, we know the following games will have announcements:

-   _Risk of Rain 2_
-   _Windrose_
-   _Solarpunk_
-   _Over the Hill_
-   _Dead as Disco_
-   _Far Far West_
-   _Castlevania: Belmont's Curse_

We also know there will be announcements from the developers behind both _[1000xResist](https://www.polygon.com/1000xresist-ps5-xbox-game-pass-impressions/)_ and [_Buckshot Roulette_](https://www.polygon.com/24030565/buckshot-roulette-indie-game-itch-mike-klubnika/), alongside _Cairn_ and _Shift at Midnight_. Keep your eyes peeled on the [official site](https://iii-initiative.com/) for more information.
