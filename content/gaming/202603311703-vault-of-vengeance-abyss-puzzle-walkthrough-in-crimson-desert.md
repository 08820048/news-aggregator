The **Vault of Vengeance Abyss puzzle** in [_Crimson Desert_](https://www.polygon.com/game/crimson-desert/40719/) contains too many lasers for comfort. Luckily, it also contains more loot than you could possibly carry, including a particularly valuable golden sword. Besides the lasers, the main obstacle between Kliff and his gold is the marksman targets, which require a specific skill and weapon to clear.

Here's **how to restore the Vault of Vengeance Abyss** in _Crimson Desert_.

* * *

## How to unlock the Vault of Vengeance in Crimson Desert

Before you head to this Abyss, beware of a few **puzzle requirements**: You must bring a bow with at least six arrows and unlock the **Focused Shot skill**. The skill requires Marksmanship level 2 and Focus level 2, and costs one abyss artifact.

To reach the Vault of Vengeance, you must pass through the skybridge that becomes available after you've restored the **[Secret Garden](https://www.polygon.com/crimson-desert-secret-garden-abyss-puzzle-walkthrough/)**.

    ![The path to the Vault of Vengeance Abyss in Crimson Desert.](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/crimson-desert-vault-of-vengeance-abyss-2.jpg?q=49&fit=crop&w=825&dpr=2)Graphic: Marloes Valentina Stella/Polygon | Source image: Pearl Abyss via Polygon

If you haven't reached the **end of Chapter 4** in the main story yet, which involves the [Scholastone Institute puzzle](https://www.polygon.com/crimson-desert-scholastone-institute-puzzle-gate-otherworld-how-to-solve-quest/), defeating [Tenebrum](https://www.polygon.com/crimson-desert-tenebrum-boss-how-to-beat-scholastone/), and solving the [Spire of the Stars puzzle](https://www.polygon.com/crimson-desert-spire-of-the-stars-puzzle-walkthrough-how-to-solve/), you must do so first. Then, following the [Obsession and Madness quest](https://www.polygon.com/crimson-desert-obsession-and-madness-abyss-puzzle-walkthrough-sanctorum-insight/) at the Sanctorum of Insight Abyss, you can take the skybridge to the Secret Garden Abyss.

* * *

## Vault of Vengeance Abyss puzzle walkthrough in Crimson Desert

To enter the Vault of Vengeance in _Crimson Desert_, **grab the two floating blue cubes** using Axiom Force and place them on the stone pillars near the large doorway. As shown in the picture below, the first blue cube is to the left, a bit further into the garden, and the second is on a ledge to the right, very close to the doorway.

    ![Arrows point out the puzzle solution to open the Vault of Vengeance door in Crimson Desert.](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/crimson-desert-vault-of-vengeance-abyss-3.jpg?q=49&fit=crop&w=825&dpr=2)Graphic: Marloes Valentina Stella/Polygon | Source image: Pearl Abyss via Polygon

Next, **shoot the two targets** adjacent to the large doors. Once you hit a target, it'll light up, and a timer will start; the objective is to **hit the other target before time runs out**. Although you _could_ hit these two with single shots in quick succession, **it's easier to use Focused Shot**; aim the bow, activate "Focused Shot" (press X on a keyboard or press both sticks on a controller), and select both targets before releasing the arrows.

After activating the targets, use **Nature's Grasp** to lift the door and enter the Vault of Vengeance.

    ![Arrows point to the door puzzle solution at the Vault of Vengeance Abyss in Crimson Desert.](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/crimson-desert-vault-of-vengeance-abyss-4.jpg?q=49&fit=crop&w=825&dpr=2)Graphic: Marloes Valentina Stella/Polygon | Source image: Pearl Abyss via Polygon

To deactivate the lasers in the room ahead, **shoot the four targets** on the wall using **Focused Shot**. Due to the extremely brief timer, you can't activate all four targets quickly enough with normal shots.

This room is stacked with loot, so be sure to grab some before moving on. When you're ready, **take the golden sword** in the center of the room (this will net you a big sack of silver) and take the elevator down.

    ![Arrows point to the targets and the golden sword inside the Vault of Vengeance Abyss in Crimson Desert.](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/crimson-desert-vault-of-vengeance-abyss-5.jpg?q=49&fit=crop&w=825&dpr=2)Graphic: Marloes Valentina Stella/Polygon | Source image: Pearl Abyss via Polygon

In the hallway with the moving laser beams, wait for a gap, then run, jump, and crawl through. In the picture below, for example, you could easily pass through the large gaps on the left.

At the end of the hallway, use **Axiom Force** to grab the blue stone and place it against the square slot on the wall. Use **Force Palm** to fasten it.

    ![Lasers and a floating blue stone in the Crimson Desert Vault of Vengeance Abyss.](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/crimson-desert-vault-of-vengeance-abyss-6.jpg?q=49&fit=crop&w=825&dpr=2)Graphic: Marloes Valentina Stella/Polygon | Source image: Pearl Abyss via Polygon

Step on the circular teleporter to reach the top of the Vault of Vengeance. See the massive circular pressure plate ahead? Your final objective at the Vault of Vengeance is to activate it.

Use **Axiom Force** to place the blue stone right in front of the empty socket on the right, then use **Force Palm** to push it into the socket. Now, use Force Palm on the large circular device.

    ![Kliff completes the Vault of Vengeance Abyss puzzle in Crimson Desert.](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/crimson-desert-vault-of-vengeance-abyss-7.jpg?q=49&fit=crop&w=825&dpr=2)Graphic: Marloes Valentina Stella/Polygon | Source image: Pearl Abyss via Polygon

Climb to the higher ledge, above the circular device, to collect the abyss artifact. From here, you can take the skybridge to the Courtyard of Precision.
