During a new Nintendo Direct on Monday, [Nintendo](https://www.polygon.com/nintendo/) and Illumination revealed the final trailer for [_The Super Mario Galaxy Movie_](https://www.polygon.com/super-mario-galaxy-movie-trailer-nintendo-direct/). While that new look at Mario and Luigi's next adventure looks impressively larger in scope than 2023's [_The Super Mario Bros. Movie_](https://www.polygon.com/reviews/23669990/super-mario-bros-movie-review-references/), it was a handful of casting reveals that stole the show.

That includes the surprise reveal of Donald Glover (_Atlanta_, _Community_) as [Yoshi the dinosaur](https://www.polygon.com/super-mario-galaxy-movie-nintendo-direct-new-trailer/). Yoshi has traditionally been voiced by [Kazumi Totaka](https://www.polygon.com/2015/1/12/7534637/mario-kart-8-totakas-song-nintendo-secrets/), the veteran musician and composer of dozens of Nintendo games. Glover is stepping into Yoshi's weird shoes that are somehow attached to Yoshis at birth, giving _The Super Mario Galaxy Movie_ extra star power. Illumination CEO Chris Meledandri says that Glover is a "Nintendo super-fan."

Joining Glover in _The Super Mario Galaxy Movie_ are Luis Guzmán (_Wednesday_, _Boogie Nights_) as the [recently revealed Wart](https://www.polygon.com/super-mario-galaxy-movie-nintendo-direct-wart-poster/), and Issa Rae (_Insecure_, _Barbie_) as Honey Queen, the "imposing ruler of the Honeyhive Galaxy," as described by Meledandri.

The latest trailer for _The Super Mario Galaxy Movie_ also confirms the return of Lumalee, the [beloved nihilistic Luma](https://www.polygon.com/23673058/super-mario-bros-luma-wants-to-die/) who was a breakout hit from _The Super Mario Bros. Movie_. This time, Lumalee is flexing her cruelty as a... prison guard?

In the follow-up to 2023's _The Super Mario Bros. Movie_, Mario, Luigi, and Princess Peach face a new threat in Bowser Jr and his father, who is now back to normal size. Our heroes are joined by new newcomers Rosalina and Yoshi in a story that expands the Super Mario cinematic universe well beyond Brooklyn and the Mushroom Kingdom. According to Meledandri, there will be more characters from the Super Mario universe that have not yet been revealed, "some big surprises and some very small."

_The Super Mario Bros. Movie_ actors Chris Pratt (Mario), Anya Taylor-Joy (Peach), Charlie Day (Luigi), Jack Black (Bowser), Keegan-Michael Key (Toad), and Kevin Michael Richardson (Kamek) reprise their respective roles in _The Super Mario Galaxy Movie_. New to the franchise are Brie Larson as Rosalina and Benny Safdie as Bowser Jr.

_The Super Mario Galaxy Movie_ opens in movie theaters on April 1. To tide you over, here's Donald Glover's outstanding headshot for the reveal of his role as Yoshi.
