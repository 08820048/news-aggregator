Industry conference returns from 14-16 July, GamesIndustry.biz returning as media partner and party host

         ![Panellists at Develop Brighton.](https://assetsio.gnwcdn.com/Develop-2025-panel_gXZruMt.jpg?width=570&quality=85&format=jpg&dpr=3&auto=webp)

Image credit: Develop Brighton

Tickets have gone on sale for the Develop: Brighton, which will be taking place in Brighton from 14-16 July. _GamesIndustry.biz_ is once again a media partner of the event, and as part of that our readers can benefit from a 10% discount on ticket prices.

Early Bird ticket pricing is available until March 26th, and passes can be purchased [on the Develop website](https://www.developconference.com/event-info/passes-pricing). _GamesIndustry.biz_ readers can use the code **NLEJZS** at checkout for a 10% discount.

The event is also still accepting submissions for conference sessions, which can be [submitted through the site](https://www.developconference.com/get-involved/call-for-speakers) until 11 February. Last year's keynote speaker was [Remedy's Sam Lake](https://www.gamesindustry.biz/remedys-sam-lake-to-keynote-developbrighton-2025), and sessions included MachineGames discussing the development of Indiana Jones and the Great Circle, Sony Interactive Entertainment on designing for accessibility, and Abubakar Salim on his journey from actor to studio CEO.

![Guests at the GamesIndustry.biz Summer Party in 2025.](https://www.gamesindustry.biz/develop-brighton-gi-party.png)

The GI Summer Party will also be celebrating its 20th birthday. | Image credit: GamesIndustry.biz

2026 will mark the event's 20th anniversary - and also the 20th anniversary of the _GamesIndustry.biz_ Summer Party, which has become a staple of the week and will also be returning. Tickets for that will be available later in the year. Those interested in sponsorship should contact our commercial lead George Corner, [george.corner@gamesindustry.biz](mailto:george.corner@gamesindustry.biz).
