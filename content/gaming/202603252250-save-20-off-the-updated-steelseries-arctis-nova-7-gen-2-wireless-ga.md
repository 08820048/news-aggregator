### Steelseries Arctis Nova 7 Gen 2 Wireless Gaming Headset

The Nova 7 is Steelseries' best selling headset because it offers great build quality and premium features for a competitive price. The next step up would be the Nova Pro, which retails for $380 (although [it's also on sale](https://www.ign.com/articles/steelseries-arctis-nova-pro-wireless-gaming-headset-deal-spring-sale-coupon-code) at the moment). The Nova 7 is designed for both durability and comfort; the suspension-style headband is comprised of a tough steel core and the breathable AirWeave memory-foam type ear cushions keep your ears from getting sweaty. The Nova 7 excels at in-game audio thanks to big 40mm drivers that support 360-degree spatial sound. Other features include dual simultaneous wireless connectivity (2.4GHz WiFi and Bluetooth), USB Type-C charging, and a retractable boom microphone.

In his [SteelSeries Arctis Nova 7 (Gen 1) review](https://www.ign.com/articles/steelseries-arctis-nova-7-review), Matthew Adler wrote that "SteelSeries continues to push the boundaries of what a headset is capable of. The Arctis Nova 7’s new design is sleek, lightweight, and still remains one of the most comfortable headsets I’ve ever used."
