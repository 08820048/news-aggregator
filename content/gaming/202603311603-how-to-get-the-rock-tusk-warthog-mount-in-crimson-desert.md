The **Rock Tusk Warthog** is one of the legendary mounts in [_Crimson Desert_](https://www.polygon.com/game/crimson-desert/40719/). This large boar is the perfect companion to run over enemies and break groups' defenses. It's not as suited to travel long distances as the [Snowwhite Deer](https://www.polygon.com/crimson-desert-snowwhite-deer-legendary-mount-location-how-to-get/), nor is it as sturdy as the [White Bear](https://www.polygon.com/crimson-desert-white-bear-legendary-mount-how-to-get-polar/), but the Rock Tusk Warthog has enough stamina, health, and attack power that you would expect from a legendary mount.

To unlock this mount, you must go through a few steps. Here's **how to get the Rock Tusk Warthog mount in** **_Crimson Desert_**.

* * *

## Rock Tusk Warthog mount location in Crimson Desert

Before you try to get the Rock Tusk Warthog mount in _Crimson Desert_, it's important to know that you'll need to have access to any [witch](http://www.polygon.com/crimson-desert-witch-where-to-find-location-abyss-gear-equip-hermit/) beyond Sylvia. You'll unlock Elowen after completing "The Missing Seal" quest in Chapter 5, so make sure to progress in the story if you haven't met the Witch of Wisdom yet.

To unlock the Rock Tusk Warthog mount, you must complete the **"Master of the Wetlands" quest** by defeating **Ravok of the Savage Fangs** and receive the **"Rock Tusk Warthog Molar"** key item. There are no prerequisites for this mission. You just need to enter the area for it to become available.

    ![A Crimson Desert image showing the location of the Rock Dusk Warhog legendary mount](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/crimsondesert_guide_rock_dusk_map1.jpg?q=49&fit=crop&w=825&dpr=2)Graphic: Paulo Kawanishi/Polygon | Source images: Pearl Abyss via Polygon

To find Ravok of the Savage Fangs, travel northeast of Demeniss to reach the **Tashkalp** area. **Near the Red River** to its south, you'll find the **Savage Fangs camp**. There are several enemies patrolling the area, some on foot while others are riding boars. However, the boss is always roaming around the area, so use your lantern to track their movements and locate them. You'll know when you spot them when you find a Savage Fang wearing a red cape that is followed by three other Savage Fangs. When the boss sees you, his life bar will appear at the top of the screen.

    ![A montage of two Crimson Desert images showing the Ravok of the Savage Fangs boss and his location](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/crimsondesert_guide_rock_dusk_map2.jpg?q=49&fit=crop&w=825&dpr=2)Graphic: Paulo Kawanishi/Polygon | Source images: Pearl Abyss via Polygon

The fight against Ravok of the Savage Fangs has **two phases**. Ravok will either charge at you on his boar or shoot a fire projectile using his Volcanic Blaster. However, the encounter can become dangerous if you let other enemies surround you. If you're strong enough, try to clear the area before pulling the boss. However, if you're having trouble fighting the boss along with the regular enemies, **you can lure Ravok to the river**. In my experience, when I crossed the river, only the boss followed me.

After depleting the boss's first health bar, he will disappear. **You need to track him again** around the same area where you initially found him. It's possible to repeat the same strategy and lure him out of the camp and cross the river to have a fair fight.

* * *

## How to unlock the Rock Tusk Warthog mount in Crimson Desert

By defeating Ravok of the Savage Fangs, you receive the Volcanic Blaster, an abyss artifact, a Giant Brown Sugar Cube, and the "**Rock Tusk Warthog Molar**." Take the molar to a witch and select the **"Craft Abyss Gear"** option. In the **"Special Items" tab**, craft the **Sigil of Solidarity (Rock Tusk Warthog)**.

    ![A montage of two Crimson Desert screenshot showing where you can take the Rock Tusk Warhog Molar to craft the legendary mount](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/crimsondesert_guide_rock_dusk_map22.jpg?q=49&fit=crop&w=825&dpr=2)Graphic: Paulo Kawanishi/Polygon | Source images: Pearl Abyss via Polygon

Use the Sigil of Solidarity in your inventory to unlock the Rock Tusk Warthog legendary mount. To summon it, select it in the character selection wheel by pressing F1 on the keyboard or, if you're using a controller, holding down the D-pad.
