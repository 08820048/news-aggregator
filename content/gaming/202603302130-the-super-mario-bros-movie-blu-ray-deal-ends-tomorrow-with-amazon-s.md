However, the spring sale ends tomorrow, so you’ll want to jump on this deal now before it expires.

### The Super Mario Bros. Movie - Power Up Edition Blu-ray + DVD + Digital

With The Super Mario Bros. Movie - Power Up Edition Blu-Ray, in addition to the Blu-ray, you receive a DVD and a code for a Digital Copy of the film. It’s also loaded with the special bonus content below:

-   Getting to Know the Cast
-   Leveling Up: Making The Super Mario Bros. Movie
-   The Super Mario Bros. Movie Field Guide
-   "Peaches" Lyric Video
-   Leadership Lessons From Anya Taylor-Joy

The [Super Mario Bros. Movie made Mario a movie star](https://www.ign.com/articles/how-nintendo-finally-made-mario-a-movie-star); it was the second biggest movie of 2023 and the highest-grossing video game adaptation ever. This animated feature is just a straight-up fun adventure for Mario fans, young and old, bringing back all the familiar characters and places you love. Plus, the voice cast is stacked with Chris Pratt as Mario, Charlie Day as Luigi, Anya Taylor-Joy as Peach, Keegan-Michael Key as Toad, and an unforgettable Jack Black as Bowser. His rendition of “Peaches” is still stuck in my head to this day.

IGN’s Tom Jorgensen reviewed The Super Mario Bros. Movie and found, “The Super Mario Bros. Movie is a fireball of animated fantasy. Mario, Luigi, and Peach’s adventure delights with its infectious energy and smart implementations of video game callbacks, and the top-shelf animation renders the Mushroom Kingdom as an Oz-like wonderland that begs to be explored in the inevitable sequels that will follow.”

Beyond the Mario sequel that’s dropping this week, Nintendo has a [Donkey Kong movie in the works](https://www.ign.com/articles/nintendo-has-filed-a-copyright-notice-for-an-untitled-donkey-kong-motion-picture), which will be led by Seth Rogen’s voice talents, and the highly anticipated [live-action Legend of Zelda](https://www.ign.com/articles/the-legend-of-zelda-movie-live-action-director-plot-cast-trailer-release-date-nintendo) is set to release next year. So, there’s a lot to be excited about in the game adaptation front.
