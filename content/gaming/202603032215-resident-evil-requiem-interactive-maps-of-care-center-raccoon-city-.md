If you’ve ever played a Resident Evil game, you probably know what to expect when you’re not killing zombies. Exploration is essential, as creepy mansions become puzzle boxes full of hidden items. _**Resident Evil Requiem**_**’s maps** are no different. You’ll find plenty of precious ammo stashed away in the Care Center as Grace, and then discover a small open-ended version of Raccoon City with plenty of its own secrets.

You’ll want to scour each area as thoroughly as you can. Once the story moves on from a location, there’s no going back. That means you could miss out on **backpack [upgrades](https://www.polygon.com/resident-evil-requiem-re9-best-upgrades/)**, [**weapons**](https://www.polygon.com/resident-evil-requiem-re9-best-guns-where-to-find-location-shotgun-sniper-rpg/), [**safes**](https://www.polygon.com/resident-evil-requiem-re9-safe-codes-combination-list-all/), and even **crafting recipes**. You don’t _need_ to gather everything, but your chances of survival will be much better if you do.

Here are our **interactive maps for every major area in _Resident Evil Requiem_**, including the **Care Center** and **Raccoon City**. We'll continue to update these maps as we find more collectibles. May they safely guide you through a survival horror game that goes to some truly unnerving places.
