The feline fighting game surpassed Hades 2's record

   ![A cat wearing makeup in Mewgenics.](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/02/mewgenics-news-steam-record.jpg?&fit=crop&w=1600&h=900)Image: Edmund McMillen/Tyler Glaiel via Polygon

Hold your catnip; an army of cats just took down Olympus. Turn-based, tactical cat-breeding combat game [_Mewgenics_](https://www.polygon.com/mewgenics-review-pc/), created by Edmund McMillen and Tyler Glaiel, has not just become one of the most popular games on Steam but the most-played roguelike/roguelite Steam game of all time, a title previously held by [_Hades 2_](https://www.polygon.com/game/hades-2/40880/).

By hitting an all-time peak of [115,428 concurrent players](https://steamdb.info/app/686060/charts/) on Sunday, Feb. 15, _Mewgenics_ set a new record for the highest player count among roguelike and roguelite games, surpassing the previous record of 112,947 concurrent players held by _Hades 2_. The Olympic-themed roguelite hit its peak on Sept. 28, 2025, which (perhaps unsurprisingly) was also a Sunday.

Edmund McMillen, who previously created the hit action roguelike [_The Binding of Isaac_](https://www.polygon.com/game/the-binding-of-isaac/2768/), surpassed his former game's peak of 70,701 concurrent players, a record dating back to April 2021 (The _Rebirth_ edition). Just one day after its release on Feb. 10, _Mewgenics_ had already gained more than 80,000 concurrent players.

Naturally, this means that _Mewgenics_ is a financial success, too. On Feb. 11, Tyler Glaiel announced that _Mewgenics_ had [made back its budget](https://www.polygon.com/mewgenics-sales-budget-recovered-3-hours/) within three hours, and on Feb. 12, he posted on X that they had sold half a million copies in 36 hours.

As player peaks from other highly acclaimed roguelite and roguelike games, such as the first [_Hades_](https://www.polygon.com/game/hades/40460/) (54,240 players), [_Vampire Survivors_](https://www.polygon.com/game/vampire-survivors/40834/) (77,061), and [_Balatro_](https://www.polygon.com/game/balatro/460734/) (43,905), are usually confined to the 40,000 to 80,000 range, _Mewgenics_'s record is exceptionally high. And that goes for cat games, too; while [_Stray_](https://www.polygon.com/game/stray/40674/) hit a purrfectly acceptable 62,963 player peak, _Mewgenics_ has almost doubled that record. It shows that anything is pawsible, especially if you add unlimited furry friends to your game.

Beware, though, that it may take months or years to [find every secret in the game](https://www.polygon.com/mewgenics-dlc-secret-unlock-arg-binding-of-issac/), which will give us something to do while we wait to see if _Mewgenics_ will indeed become a [GOTY 2026 contender](https://www.polygon.com/goty-2026-first-mewgenics/).
