## Brandon Sanderson’s latest crowdfunding project raises $5.5 million in under 24 hours

[Brandon Sanderson](https://www.polygon.com/24109139/brandon-sanderson-order-new-books-secret-projects-guides/) is one of the most successful and prolific fantasy and [science fiction](https://www.polygon.com/tailored-realities-brandon-sanderson-more-sci-fi/) authors of all time, and his devoted fanbase has helped him repeatedly shatter crowdfunding records. His company Dragonsteel Entertainment raised $41 million to publish [four secret novels](https://www.polygon.com/22958164/brandon-sanderson-kickstarter-new-books/) he wrote during the COVID-19 lockdown. Brotherwise Games’ [_Cosmere Roleplaying Game_](https://www.polygon.com/tabletop-games/24214470/brandon-sanderson-cosmere-ttrpg-surprise-announcement/), based on Sanderson’s sprawling fantasy universe, [raised $15 million](https://www.polygon.com/tabletop-games/446320/sanderson-cosmere-ttrpg-breaks-record-most-funded-game-kickstarter/). His latest project [launched on BackerKit](https://www.backerkit.com/c/projects/dragonsteel/hoids-storybook-collection) on March 3 and has raised $5.5 million in under 24 hours.

The campaign is focused on Hoid, an immortal fool and storyteller who appears in many of Sanderson’s novels. Hoid’s Storybook Collection spans four illustrated fables: _The Dog and the Dragon_, _Wandersail_, and _The Girl Who Looks Up_ were originally presented in the Stormlight Archive series, while _The ChasmFriends Get a Pet_ is part of Dragonsteel’s _Story Deck_ collectible trading card game.

Backers can also buy [_The Fires of December_](https://www.polygon.com/brandon-sanderson-new-cosmere-novel-the-fires-of-december/), a new Cosmere novel. Like [_Tress of the Emerald Sea_](https://www.amazon.com/Tress-Emerald-Sea-Cosmere-Projects-ebook/dp/B0BPNB19CL) and [_Yumi and the Nightmare Painter_](https://www.amazon.com/Secret-Project-Cosmere-Novel-Projects-ebook/dp/B0BPN9JT6M/ref=sr_1_1?crid=3F0U2N4Y6G782&dib=eyJ2IjoiMSJ9._RMx-_gkzktotjJ4pZVHy1Wtoylj11_l1bGQSYqDMmhsO6DlqRzb3XEmdY2t4VUNHVzWS1qay_brZIaDFwtM_oEvO3apUvtsY5kU-QlqZnlHaaNAOBxBSBjbfEoz3gCR2fzV7rdC1JIn3YrqnKKXNW9D7L5ruOHhb3uGqrNu2zs.KrbS4HaXIAVqCANJwVKnbsplxpzG_3W492TEUsPmeBs&dib_tag=se&keywords=Yumi+and+the+Nightmare+Painter&qid=1772617650&s=digital-text&sprefix=yumi+and+the+nightmare+painter%2Cdigital-text%2C311&sr=1-1), which were released as part of Sanderson’s secret projects campaign, _The Fires of December_ is a standalone novel that can provide an [introduction to the Cosmere](https://www.polygon.com/24109139/brandon-sanderson-order-new-books-secret-projects-guides/). The three books have now been grouped into a series called Hoid’s Travails since they are framed as stories Hoid is telling about the remarkable people he’s met in his world-hopping travels.

The books are expected to ship in fall 2026. Add-ons include pins and plushies depicting ChasmFiends – giant crab-like creatures from the Stormlight Archive – and a replica of Hoid’s flute.
