## The Division's free-to-play spinoff finally gets a release date, and sooner than you'd think

   ![TDR_-_Freemen_1 copy](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/tdr_-_freemen_1-copy.jpg?&fit=crop&w=1600&h=900)Image: Ubisoft

Following a delay, _Tom Clancy's The Division Resurgence_ has reemerged with an imminent release date. It'll be out March 31 for Android and iOS devices, Ubisoft announced today during [The Division](https://www.polygon.com/game/tom-clancys-the-division/13667/)'s [10th anniversary stream](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=pbEgw-CUqbk). You can pre-register now to get gold weapon skins for launch.

First revealed in 2022, [_The Division Resurgence_](https://www.polygon.com/23197037/tom-clancys-the-division-mobile-game-resurgence-release-date-free-to-play/) is the free-to-play mobile spinoff of Ubisoft's series of third-person loot shooters (which deserves some credit with popularizing the now-ubiquitous extraction shooter genre, thanks to a fan-favorite mode in the game). Initially planned for a 2024 release, Ubisoft delayed _Resurgence_ [in 2024](https://www.gamespot.com/articles/ubisoft-delays-two-upcoming-games/1100-6525110/). (Just so we're all on the same page, _The Division Resurgence_ was not _The Division Heartland_, a planned free-to-play game that [was also canceled in 2024](https://www.polygon.com/24157512/tom-clancys-division-heartland-canceled-ubisoft/).) Ubisoft conducted [closed](https://www.ubisoft.com/en-us/game/the-division/resurgence/news-updates/6hRKyKAcTjZJY3eSjMfd6Z/september-2025-closed-test-information) [tests](https://www.reddit.com/r/thedivision/comments/1p5nsfj/the_division_ressurgence_test/#:~:text=There%20is%20now%20loot%20on,get%20to%20your%20region!!) for the game in late 2025.

Ubisoft announced the news with a trailer detailing your player character, who wakes up after a monthlong coma in Manhattan, shortly after the events of the first game. The gameplay looks much like the mainline Division games: third-person shooting in a near-futuristic vision of New York City that's been upended by a devastating pandemic.

Elsewhere in the stream, Ubisoft teased [new DLC for _The Division 2_](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dyClSfR9YEc), set in Central Park. Ubisoft will also run play tests for Survivors, a true-to-form extraction shooter mode for _The Division 2_, later this year. No date on either of those yet. In the meantime, _The Division 2_ will run its 10th anniversary season through March 24, which includes a realism mode (reduced time-to-kill, "realistic" weapon damage) and free access to 2020's [_Warlords of New York_](https://www.polygon.com/2020/3/6/21168071/the-division-2-warlords-of-new-york-impressions-gameplay-story-ps4-xbox-one-pc/) expansion.
