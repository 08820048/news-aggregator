Image credit: Supergiant Games/Sassy Chap Games

The nominations for the sixth annual Gayming Awards have been announced, with Hades 2 and Date Everything each receiving three nominations across 14 categories.

Supergiant Games' Hades 2 is among the six titles nominated for Game of the Year, alongside Ball x Pit, Blue Prince, Ghost of Yōtei, Hollow Knight: Silksong, and South of Midnight.

Hades 2 and Silksong were also nominated for the Gayming Magazine Fan Favourite Award, along with Date Everything, Dead by Daylight: Steady Pulse, Kingdom Come: Deliverance 2, and Lost Records: Bloom & Rage.

Date Everything was featured in the LGBTQ+ Indie Game category, as well as Monster Prom 4: Monster Con, Quantum Witch, Takes of Seikyu, The Beekeepers Picnic, and Spirit Swap.

Winners of the LGBTQ+ Streaming Rising Star Class of 2026, LGBTQ+ Content Creator Superstar Award, and the Gayming Icon Award will be revealed in May 2026.

This year's ceremony includes a new suite of accolades called the Gayming Awards Creator Honours, which "set out to celebrate and uplift LGBTQ+ gaming content creators of all levels."

The Gayming Awards 2026 takes place on June 8 on World of Wonder's streaming service, WOW Presents Plus.

The ceremony will be hosted by last year's LGBTQ+ Voice Actor of the Year winner Erika Ishii and drag artist Dawn.
