### Ecovacs Deebot T90 Pro Robot Vacuum and Mop

The Deebot T90 Pro is both a robot vacuum and mop, and it's equipped to excel at both. Let's start first with the vacuum. The T90 Pro is rated for 30,000 pascals of suction, which is chart-topping. Most high-end robot vacuums are closer to 20,000-25,000Pa. Nearly every other robot vacuum and mop combo with a 30,000Pa or higher rating costs over $1,000 and is the flagship model of its respective brand. A stronger suction allows the robot vac to pick up stubborn dirt and hair more efficiently, especially when it's working over deep pile rugs and carpet. The included docking station collects the debris in a larger dust bag so that you don't need to empty out after each use.

The mopping system is no less impressive. The "Ozmo-Roller 3.0" system scrubs instead of wipes with a rotating roller mop that spins at up to 200rpm. The dirty water is collected in its own container after which it is emptied out and the roller mop washed at the docking station. The docking station even air drys the roller with an active heating element, a feature usually found in the most premium of models.

Other nifty features that I wish my current robot vacuum had are a mechanical climbing system that allows it to navigate over thresholds like floor transitions, upgraded top-mounted LiDAR tracking for fast and precise mapping and object detection, and a PowerBoost fast charging function that can recharge 10% of the T90 Pro's battery in just 3 minutes. The robot vacuum can be easily disassembled and nearly all of the moving parts and supplies are readily sourceable and replaceable for long-term maintenance or repair.
