As you play the Adventure mode in [_Mario Tennis Fever_](https://www.polygon.com/mario-tennis-fever/), you'll need to complete a few **quizzes** in order to get to the next rank. From Rank E to B, you'll take a total of five quizzes, with each quiz getting more difficult. If you get a question wrong, you'll need to start the quiz over from the beginning, so you'll definitely want to get it right on the first attempt.

To help speed things up, we've listed **all the academy quiz answers in _Mario Tennis Fever_** below.

* * *

## All academy quiz answers in Mario Tennis Fever

There are five quizzes that you'll need to take in the tennis academy in _Mario Tennis Fever_. Each quiz has three questions and they're required to progress in the Adventure mode. Below, we've listed all five quizzes in chronological order along with their questions and answers.

Make sure to get each question correct or else you'll need to start over from the beginning!

## Rank E quiz answers in Mario Tennis Fever

1.  **Which shot has a high bounce?** Topspin
2.  **Which shot doesn't have much of a bounce but does curve to the side a lot?** Slice
3.  **Which shot doesn't really rotate but does move really fast?** Flat

## Rank D quiz answers in Mario Tennis Fever

1.  **What type of player has a strong shot but is slow on their feet?** Power
2.  ​​**What type of player can be confident in their speed?** Speedy
3.  **What type of player has the widest reach?** Defensive

## Rank C quiz answers in Mario Tennis Fever

1.  **What type of player is skilled at hitting curving shots?** Tricky
2.  **What type of player has a nice balance of offense and defense?** All-Around
3.  **What type of player is good at hitting the corners of the court?** Technical

## Rank B quiz #1 answers in Mario Tennis Fever

1.  **What shot should you use when you need to get back to the center of the court?** Slice
2.  **What's a good shot to use when your opponent is up close to the net?** Lob
3.  **What shot should you use when your opponent is pulling back from the net?** Drop Shot

## Rank B quiz #2 answers in Mario Tennis Fever

1.  **On which court does the ball bounce the lowest and move the fastest?** Grass Court
2.  **On which court does the ball move the slowest, making it easy to keep a rally going?** Clay Court
3.  **On which court does the ball have a good balance of bounce and speed?** Hard Court
