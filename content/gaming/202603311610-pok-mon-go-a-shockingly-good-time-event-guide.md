[_Pokémon Go_](https://www.polygon.com/game/pokemon-go/38713)’s **"A Shockingly Good Time"** event runs from **March 31 until April 6** and it features electric-type Pokémon and the debut of **Mimikyu**.

Like other recent events, "A Shockingly Good Time" has a **specialized Go Pass** where you can earn points by winning raids, catching Pokémon, and hatching eggs. After you reach certain tiers, you'll get specific bonuses in addition to the usual rewards like Pokémon encounters and candy. At rank 10, you'll unlock doubled Incense duration. At rank 20, you'll get doubled catch candy. If you buy the deluxe pass for $4.99, you'll also get double catch Stardust at rank 20.

Below we list the various perks of the _Pokémon Go_ "A Shockingly Good Time" event, as well as explain **how to get Mimikyu in _Pokémon Go_**.

* * *

## How to find Mimikyu in Pokémon Go

As part of the "A Shockingly Good Time" event, there are a few ways to catch Mimikyu:

-   Reach **rank 30 and 60 in the event Go Pass**. You'll get a Mimikyu encounter at these ranks, even if you don't pay for the deluxe pass.
-   [Players in New Zealand](https://old.reddit.com/r/TheSilphRoad/comments/1s8kdri/mimikyu_in_the_wild/) have pointed out that **Mimikyu appears to be spawning in the wild on April 1**, perhaps as part of an April Fools' Day prank. (After all, Mimikyu isn't even electric-type. It's ghost- and fairy-type!)

That being said, just keep your eyes out for Mimikyu on April 1 to try to nab as many as you want.

    ![Mimikyu in both of its forms in Pokémon Go](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/jlee_260331_1001_pogo_mimikyu.jpg?q=70&fit=crop&w=825&dpr=1)Graphic: Julia Lee/Polygon | Source images: Niantic

Mimikyu has a battle-only form referred to as its "Busted Form." After it tanks a charged attack, its disguise will break. That attack will do no damage to Mimikyu, but its defense will be lowered for the rest of the battle.

**Mimikyu is not available to catch shiny yet**.

## Pokémon Go 'A Shockingly Good Time' event Field Research and rewards

Spinning a PokéStop during the event period may yield one of these tasks:

-   ​Catch 3 Pikachu (Pikachu encounter)
-   Catch 5 different species of electric-type Pokémon (Alolan Raichu encounter)
-   Catch 8 electric-type Pokémon (Magnemite, Chinchou, Mareep, Dedenne, or Pawmi encounter)
-   Power up electric-type Pokémon 5 times (Pikachu, Magnemite, Chinchou, Mareep, Joltik, or Dedenne encounter)​

## Pokémon Go 'A Shockingly Good Time' event boosted spawns

These Pokémon will spawn more frequently during the event period, according to the event blog:

-   Pikachu\*
-   Chinchou\*
-   Dedenne\*
-   Pawmi

Players have also noticed these spawning more frequently:

-   Magnemite
-   Mareep
-   Electrike
-   Joltik

\*There is an "increased chance" of finding this shiny in the wild as part of the event.

## Pokémon Go 'A Shockingly Good Time' event spotlight hours

Though there are no more weekly spotlight hours, this event introduces a daily spotlight hour from 6-7 p.m. in your local time during the event. There are no bonuses for these spotlight hours outside the featured Pokémon spawning in abundance. The spotlight hours for this event are as follows:

-   **March 31**: Mareep
-   **April 1**: Pikachu
-   **April 2**: Magnemite
-   **April 3**: Chinchou
-   **April 4**: Pawmi
-   **April 5**: Dedenne
-   **April 6**: Joltik
