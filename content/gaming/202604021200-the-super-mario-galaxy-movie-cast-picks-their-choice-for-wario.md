Sometimes the obvious pick is the right pick

## The Super Mario Galaxy Movie cast agrees on who should be the voice of Wario: Danny DeVito

   ![WarioWare Switch Nintendo Direct E3 2021 213](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/chorus/uploads/chorus_asset/file/22660226/WarioWare_Switch_Nintendo_Direct_E3_2021_213.jpg?&fit=crop&w=1600&h=900)Image: Nintendo

The Mario Cinematic Universe is now bigger than ever, thanks to the liberal use of space travel in [_The Super Mario Galaxy Movie_](https://www.polygon.com/super-mario-galaxy-movie/), which brings in crossover characters like [Star Fox's Fox McCloud](https://www.polygon.com/super-mario-galaxy-movie-surprise-cameo-star-fox-mccloud/) and Pikmin. But there's one core Mario character who has yet to appear in [Nintendo](https://www.polygon.com/nintendo/) and Illumination's animated film franchise: [Wario](https://www.polygon.com/wario/).

Sorry, the [rumors weren't true](https://www.polygon.com/super-mario-galaxy-movie-story-leak-villain-nintendo/). Wario does _not_ make an appearance in _The Super Mario Galaxy Movie_, but the film does set up the potential for future installments that could bring in an array of characters from beyond the expected Mario canon. At some point, that would logically include Wario, the antithesis and occasional antagonist of the good-natured Mario.

With that inevitability in mind, Polygon recently spoke to the cast of _The Super Mario Galaxy Movie_ and asked for their picks on who should lend their voice to Wario. Much of the cast agree that one actor is an ideal fit for the role.

## Danny DeVito should be Wario

    ![Danny DeVito in a still from Jumanji: The Next Level](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/04/devito-jumanji.jpg?q=49&fit=crop&w=825&dpr=2)Image: Sony Pictures

Charlie Day, the voice of Luigi, told Polygon that his _It's Always Sunny in Philadelphia_ costar is the obvious choice for Wario in a future Super Mario movie.

"I would choose my buddy Danny DeVito," Day said. "He's perfect for it. He's just so funny."

"Oh, that's a good choice! That's an _excellent_ choice," said Donald Glover, the voice of Yoshi in _The Super Mario Galaxy Movie_. "Just tell him to call up \[Illumination CEO\] Chris \[Meledandri\]. [It works](https://www.polygon.com/super-mario-galaxy-movie-donald-glover-yoshi/)!"

"I love Danny DeVito as Wario" — Jack Black

While DeVito may be better known for live-action roles in _One Flew Over the Cuckoo's Nest_, _Batman Returns_, and _It's Always Sunny in Philadelphia_, the 81-year-old actor has a long list of animation credits. He famously appeared in _The Simpsons_ as Homer Simpson's half-brother Herb Powell, and has prominent roles in _Space Jam_, _The Lorax_, and Disney’s _Hercules_.

Actor-director Benny Safdie, the voice of Bowser Jr., is also on board with DeVito. While Safdie originally suggested "Chris Pratt, but with a mustache" for Wario, he revised his answer to go with DeVito.

"Now you're speaking my language," said costar Jack Black, aka Bowser, when Safdie nominated DeVito. "I _love_ Danny DeVito as Wario. He's a legend. No one knows that better than Charlie Day."

Black also had a second suggestion for Wario in Pedro Pascal, but supported the idea of Evil Version Chris Pratt as Wario, proclaiming the dual-casting idea to be "like Michael B. Jordan in [_Sinners_](https://www.polygon.com/horror/556489/sinners-review-ryan-coogler-vampire-black-panther/)!"

## Keegan-Michael Key proposes a different Wario

   ![The best thrillers to watch on Netflix this November](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2024/11/MCDTASH_EC009.jpg?q=49&fit=crop&w=825&dpr=2)

TAKE SHELTER, from left: Michael Shannon, Tova Stewart, 2011. Ph: Grove Hill Productions/©Sony Pictures Classics/Courtesy Everett Collection

Image: Sony Pictures

The voice of Toad, Keegan-Michael Key, suggested someone quite different when asked for his ideal Wario.

"I'm going to go with a real deep-cut, weird pick: Michael Shannon," Key told Polygon in an interview. "Michael Shannon and I just did a film together where we both did voices in the film, and I was not aware of the fact that he can do no wrong. You gave Michael Shannon a crack at Wario? He would do something _very_ interesting."

Shannon has less experience doing voice work for animation, but not zero: He was the voice of the Unshaven Man in FX animated series _Little Demon_, which also starred DeVito.

So, if you're thinking what I'm thinking, we might not just have our Wario, but a strong Waluigi candidate as well.
