Inside the **safes** in [_Resident Evil Requiem_](https://www.polygon.com/resident-evil-requiem/), you'll find valuable items that can make Grace stronger to fight the nasty enemies that she will inevitably encounter, but you'll need their **codes** to unlock them. There are five safes in _Resident Evil Requiem,_ and opening all of them is required to complete the "No Safe is Safe" challenge. For your troubles, you'll also find valuable items like crafting materials and Antique Coins inside.

Below, you'll find **all** **_Resident Evil Requiem_** **safe codes**, organized by the areas where you find them, with the rewards listed.

\[**Ed. note:** This guide contains minor spoilers for _Resident Evil Requiem._\]

**Update (March 2):** The safe codes for insanity difficulty have been added.

* * *

## All Rhodes Hill Chronic Care Center safe codes in Resident Evil Requiem

For roughly half of the campaign, you'll be exploring the corridors of the Rhodes Hill Chronic Care Center. While playing as Grace, you have the chance to open three safes in this area: one in the West Wing, one in the East Wing, and the last one in the Basement. In each room containing a safe, there will be enemies patrolling the surroundings. It's possible to ignore them, but you must move quickly to collect the rewards and get out of their reach.

If you'd like to try finding these codes on your own, we've prepared guides explaining where to find the codes for the safes in the [Bar and Lounge](https://www.polygon.com/resident-evil-requiem-re9-bar-lounge-safe-code-location-how-to-open/), the [Examination Room](https://www.polygon.com/resident-evil-requiem-re9-examination-room-safe-code-how-to-open/), and the [Basement](https://www.polygon.com/resident-evil-requiem-re9-basement-safe-code-location-how-to-open/).

## All Ark safe codes in Resident Evil Requiem

You encounter the last two safes in the final area of the game, the Ark. The safes in this area are great sources of crafting materials, weapons, and healing items, which are all useful when playing as Grace.

If you'd like to find the safe codes on your own, we've also prepared guides explaining where to find the codes for the [Monitor Control Room](https://www.polygon.com/resident-evil-requiem-re9-monitor-control-room-safe-code-location-how-to-open/) and [Sterilization Chamber](https://www.polygon.com/resident-evil-requiem-re9-sterilization-chamber-safe-code-location-how-to-open/) safes.
