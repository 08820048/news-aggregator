## How to link your Marathon and Twitch accounts

    ![A screenshot of the Twitch inventory showing the Marathon Twitch drops.](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/02/marathon-twitch-drops.jpg?q=49&fit=crop&w=825&dpr=2)Image: Twitch via Polygon

Your _Marathon_ account, aka your Bungie account, also must be linked to your Twitch account for this to work. Follow these steps:

1.  On [Bungie.net](http://bungie.net/), log in, then head to your account settings. (Making an account is required to play _Marathon_, so you should have an account if you've played before).
2.  Find the "account linking" option, then follow the instructions to link your Twitch account.
3.  Go to [Twitch.tv](http://twitch.tv/) and find any partnered _Marathon_ streamer with drops enabled. ([This link](https://www.twitch.tv/directory/category/marathon?tl=DropsEnabled) filters all currently live _Marathon_ streamers to show only those with drops).
4.  After watching for a little while, check your Twitch inventory to ensure the watch time is progressing. After you hit each milestone, claim the rewards, and you're sorted.

Make sure you're also up-to-date on all the [_Marathon_ classes](https://www.polygon.com/marathon-classes-runner-shells-abilities-explained/) and weapons so you're fully prepared for your visits to Tau Ceti IV.
