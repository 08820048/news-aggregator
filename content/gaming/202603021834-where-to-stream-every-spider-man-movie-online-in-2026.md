## When Are New Spider-Man Movies Coming Out?

The newest Spider-Man release is an animated TV show, "[Your Friendly Neighborhood Spider-Man](https://www.ign.com/articles/how-to-watch-your-friendly-neighborhood-spider-man)," on Disney+. We're also getting a Spider Noir TV show featuring [the one and only Nicolas Cage](https://www.ign.com/articles/nic-cages-spider-noir-proves-why-we-still-need-sonys-spider-man-universe) in May.

As of now, there are two confirmed Spider-Man movies in the works: [Spider-Man: Beyond the Spider-Verse](https://www.ign.com/articles/spider-man-beyond-the-spider-verse-reveals-its-directors-no-release-date) and a [fourth live-action MCU Spider-Man movie with Tom Holland](https://www.ign.com/articles/spider-man-4-confirmed-at-marvel-studios-tom-holland-to-begin-filming-in-2025). That new Tom Holland movie, titled [Spider-Man: Brand New Day](https://www.ign.com/articles/this-is-a-new-chapter-spider-man-brand-new-day-features-a-tonal-shift-from-tom-hollands-first-trilogy), is slated to release on July 31, 2026.
