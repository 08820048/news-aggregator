**Righteous Verdict** is one of the unique swords you can find in [_Crimson Desert_](https://www.polygon.com/game/crimson-desert/40719/). Although these weapons tend to have good attributes, their power usually comes from any slotted rare abyss gear, like the [Darkbringer](https://www.polygon.com/crimson-desert-darkbringer-sword-how-to-get-location/). While Righteous Verdict doesn't come with any god-tier abyss gear, it is a fast weapon that you can upgrade to make it one of the strongest in your personal arsenal.

Before you can start tinkering with this two-handed sword, you need to find it. Below, we explain **how to get the Righteous Verdict in** **_Crimson Desert_****.**

* * *

## How to get the Righteous Verdict sword in Crimson Desert

The Righteous Verdict sword is hidden inside a cave in the **Nightfell Expedition Camp**. The camp is located on the **southern side of Demeniss**, **east of the Steel Mountains**. It has 23 attack, critical rate level three, and attack speed level two with five abyss gear slots. Additionally, the sword comes with one attack speed 2, one attack 1, and one critical rate 1 abyss gear.

    ![A montage showing the location of the Righteous Verdict sword in Crimson Desert](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/04/crimsondesert_guide_righteous_map_1.jpg?q=49&fit=crop&w=825&dpr=2)Graphic: Paulo Kawanishi/Polygon | Source images: Pearl Abyss via Polygon

There are no prerequisites to obtain this weapon. If you're planning to go after it early on, then the fastest way to get there is by going **southeast from the [Pororin Village](https://www.polygon.com/crimson-desert-unreachable-village-quest-walkthrough-pororin-relic/)**. Just keep in mind that you will need to face a large group of enemies, so you'll want to be prepared.

The Nightfell Expedition Camp is controlled by the **Righteous Inquisitors**, and you'll need to kill some of them to explore the camp without trouble. If your gear isn't strong enough, it's possible to lure some soldiers out of the camp by hitting them with arrows. This will give you the chance to face them in groups of two or three, making it a fair fight.

    ![A montage showing the location of the Righteous Verdict sword in Crimson Desert](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/04/crimsondesert_guide_righteous_map_2.jpg?q=49&fit=crop&w=825&dpr=2)Graphic: Paulo Kawanishi/Polygon | Source images: Pearl Abyss via Polygon

After you have cleared the camp, go to the **southern side**, then **turn west** to find a flight of stairs leading to **an opening in the rocks**. Enter the cave and follow the corridor to find a **waterfall**. Behind it, you'll find stairs leading to a closed door. Push it open to access a large room with a gap in the middle.

    ![paulokawanishi — 14:35 A montage showing the location of the Righteous Verdict sword in Crimson Desert](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/04/crimsondesert_guide_righteous_map_3.jpg?q=49&fit=crop&w=825&dpr=2)Graphic: Paulo Kawanishi/Polygon | Source images: Pearl Abyss via Polygon

To cross the room and reach the next door, you can either **jump across pillars** or **Force Palm** **to gain height and glide to the other side**. If you fall, don't worry. You can climb back to the room entrance. Once you have crossed the room, open the next door by pushing it, and follow the corridor to find the Righteous Verdict embedded in the middle of the next room.
