The **High-Gain Antenna project** in _[Arc Raiders](http://www.polygon.com/game/arc-raiders/443837/)_ is the latest project to be added to the game, which essentially translates to donating lots of materials to Speranza in exchange for sweet rewards. And sweet rewards are what you get here, because even though there are only three stages to High-Gain Antenna, completing them requires taking down _loads_ of Arc enemies, including some of the newest foes you can find.

So if you want to know exactly what materials you need to hoard and the rewards you'll get for completing each stage, here's a full breakdown of the **High-Gain Antenna project**.

* * *

## High-Gain Antenna project resources and rewards in Arc Raiders

There may only be three stages here, but each one requires a hefty amount of resources. Here's everything you need to get and the rewards you'll earn:

### Sturdy Base (Stage 1 of 3)

Things don't start easy. To stock up on these Arc materials, focus on killing the bigger enemies and looting Arc probes.

**Required resources**

**Rewards**

25 Arc Alloys

Torrente II

10 Arc Flex Rubbers

Extended Medium Mag III

10 Arc Performance Steels

Extended Barrel

15 Advanced Arc Powercells

Padded Stock

### Data Logger (Stage 2 of 3)

Next up, you need to head into Close Scrutiny raids, because you have to take down the new Vaporizer enemies, along with looting the Arc Assessors.

**Required resources**

**Rewards**

15 Arc Coolants

Bobcat III

15 Arc Thermo Linings

Extended Light Mag III

4 Vaporizer Regulators

Vertical Grip III

6 Assessor Matrixes

100 Raider Tokens

### Parabolic Dish (Stage 3 of 3)

Finally, you need considerably more of the same, not to mention you also need to take down Arc Surveyors.

**Required resources**

**Rewards**

10 Surveyor Vaults

Vita Spray

20 Arc Synthetic Resins

Photoelectric Cloak

15 Vaporizer Regulators

Snap Hook

18 Assessor Matrixes

400 Raider Tokens

Unfortunately, there isn't an additional reward at the end of this project, but receiving a fully kitted out Torrente and Bobcat isn't too shabby.
