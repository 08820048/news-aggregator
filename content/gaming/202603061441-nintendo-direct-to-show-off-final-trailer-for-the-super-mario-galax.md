Nintendo has announced a Nintendo Direct revealing the final trailer for [The Super Mario Galaxy Movie](https://www.ign.com/movies/the-super-mario-galaxy-movie). It is set for Monday, March 9, at 2pm PT / 11am ET / 9pm UK time.

To manage expectations, Nintendo said no game information will be included in the presentation.

Recent trailers for The Super Mario Galaxy Movie, due to hit theaters April 1, have sparked all sorts of questions about unannounced Nintendo characters who may pop up in the film. [Some fans even believe the movie will revolve around time travel](https://www.ign.com/articles/nintendo-fans-believe-the-super-mario-galaxy-movie-features-time-travel-or-some-kind-of-age-reversing-spell). Last month, [a familiar but unannounced name popped up in a theater chain's description of The Super Mario Galaxy Movie](https://www.ign.com/articles/the-super-mario-galaxy-movie-description-appears-to-spoil-a-major-unannounced-character).

Along with Mario and Luigi, the upcoming animated sequel will also see Bowser Jr, [Yoshi](https://www.ign.com/articles/new-super-mario-galaxy-movie-trailer-finally-shows-yoshi-eating-someone-and-pooping-out-an-egg), [Birdo, a T-Rex,](https://www.ign.com/videos/yoshi-birdo-t-rex-mouser-and-more-appear-in-the-super-mario-galaxy-movie-trailer) and Rosalina, the latter of whom is voiced by Marvel star and Nintendo fan Brie Larson. All of the same major cast members are back from the first movie, too, including Chris Pratt as Mario, Anya Taylor-Joy as Princess Peach, Charlie Day as Luigi, Jack Black as Bowser, Keegan-Michael Key as Toad, and Kevin Michael Richardson as Kamek.
