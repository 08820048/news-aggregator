**_Slay the Spire 2_ early access** is coming soon, giving you the chance to experience this game as it grows to its final version. While it might look like just an upgraded version of its predecessor, [_Slay the Spire 2_](https://www.polygon.com/slay-the-spire-2/) will come with new features that change the roguelike deck-building formula. It doesn't mean, however, that it's abandoning the traditional solo runs fans of the first game love so much.

If you're excited about starting the first of many runs to come, here's when the **_Slay the Spire 2_ early access will be released** in your time zone.

* * *

## What time Slay the Spire 2 early access releases in your time zone?

_Slay the Spire 2_ early access releases on **Thursday, March 5, at 1 p.m. EST,** based on what the Mega Crit studio shared in a recent [AMA](http://www.reddit.com/r/Games/comments/1rdqanv/comment/o7dkooq/?utm_source=share&utm_medium=web3x&utm_name=web3xcss&utm_term=1&utm_content=share_button) session on Reddit. Early access will only be available for Windows PC for now. Here's what this looks like in other time zones:

-   **10:00 a.m. PST** for the west coast of North America
-   **1:00 p.m. EST** for the east coast of North America
-   **3:00 p.m. BRT** for Brazil
-   **6:00 p.m. GMT** for the U.K.
-   **7:00 p.m. CET** for western Europe
-   **3:00 a.m. JST on Friday, March 6** for Japan
-   **4:00 a.m. AEDT on Friday, March 6** for the east coast of Australia

## What's different in Slay the Spire 2?

This new game gets you back to the Spire. After the events of _Slay the Spire_, where you reached the Spire's heart, it remained dormant for 1,000 years. However, it's back "hungrier and more dangerous than ever, devouring all who dare to ascend" according to the [_Slay the Spire 2_ official site](https://www.megacrit.com/news/2026-02-19-release-date-trailer/).

_Slay the Spire 2_ promises to deliver the signature solo deck-building experience from the first, but it also wants to allow more crowded runs. Adding a co-op mode to the game, _Slay the Spire 2_ gives you the option to form parties of up to four players.

When it comes to more specific changes, in the announcement trailer, the studio teases some of the additions it's bringing with _Slay the Spire 2._ Three examples stand out: as explained by [Jonathan Cook](https://theflavourtext.substack.com/p/slay-the-spire-2-everything-we-know), cards may receive new buffs called "enchantments" that modify a card during a run; enemies can afflict your cards, adding negative effects to them; and a new type of card is coming to the game: **Quest cards**. During early access, Mega Crit announced the game will receive balance updates and new content, including cards, events, environments, and enemies.
