**Maurice’s Black Market Vending Machine** is one of the weekly challenges you can pursue in _[Borderlands 4](https://www.polygon.com/game/borderlands-4/443248/)_ after you have completed the campaign.

Also known as the legendary vending machine, this unique service appears once a week with a select inventory of legendary guns and ordnance for you to buy — but only if you can find it! The Black Market Vending Machine’s location changes after [_Borderlands 4_’s weekly reset](https://www.polygon.com/borderlands-4-weekly-reset-time-challenges-when-bl4/), and its icon only appears on the map when you get near it.

To help you locate this special vending machine and grab some legendary gear before the reset, we’ve prepared this guide showing **where to find Maurice’s Black Market Vending Machine** in _Borderlands 4_ and how it works. If you need more help finding the area, check out our full [interactive _Borderlands 4_ map](https://www.polygon.com/map/borderlands-4-interactive-map-full-kairos-bl4/).

## Maurice’s Black Market Vending Machine location in Borderlands 4

    ![Borderlands 4 map showing the location of Maurice's Vending Machine in march 31](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/b4_guide_maurice_march26_map.jpg?q=49&fit=crop&w=825&dpr=2)Graphic: Paulo Kawanishi/Polygon | Source images: Gearbox Software/2K Games via Polygon

From **March 26 to April 2**, Maurice's Black Market Vending Machine can be found in the **Cuspid Climb area**. The closest [fast travel station](https://www.polygon.com/borderlands-4-all-fast-travel-locations-spots-safe-houses-silos-bl4/) is the **Heritage Opus Safehouse** a bit to the north. Start there and follow the main road south to get to the building where the vending machine is located.

    ![A montage of two Borderlands 4 images showing the path to Maurice's Vending Machine in March 31](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/b4_guide_maurice_march26_part1.jpg?q=49&fit=crop&w=825&dpr=2)Graphic: Paulo Kawanishi/Polygon | Source images: Gearbox Software/2K Games via Polygon

Once you make it to Cuspid Climb, head to the **west side** to find an excavator with a **grappling spot**. Use it to reach the upper level, then look for another grappling spot now to the **east,** a bit above where you are.

    ![A montage using two Borderlands 4 images showing the path to Maurice's Vending Machine in March 31](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/b4_guide_maurice_march26_part2.jpg?q=49&fit=crop&w=825&dpr=2)Graphic: Paulo Kawanishi/Polygon | Source images: Gearbox Software/2K Games via Polygon

**Keep heading east**, fighting the enemies. Climb the wall, head north and turn right to find another wall to climb, **behind the main pillar**.

In the next area, there will be more enemies to fight.

    ![A montage using two Borderlands 4 images showing the path to Maurice's Vending Machine in March 31](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/b4_guide_maurice_march26_part3.jpg?q=49&fit=crop&w=825&dpr=2)Graphic: Paulo Kawanishi/Polygon | Source images: Gearbox Software/2K Games via Polygon

Next, follow the **stairs on the east side**, cross the next area, and turn left to find the vending machine.

    ![A Borderlands 4 image showing Maurice's Black Market Vending Machine](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/b4_guide_maurice_march26_machine.jpg?q=49&fit=crop&w=825&dpr=2)Image: Gearbox Software/2K Games via Polygon

## Maurice’s Black Market Vending Machine, explained

Unlike other vending machines, Maurice’s Black Market Vending Machine is a special one that only sells legendary items. **You can buy as many items from it as you want to** — if you have the money, since these items are quite expensive.

If you have more than one character, **you can buy from the machine with all of them**. You can even buy more of the same item. When you use Maurice’s Black Market Vending Machine with one character, your other Vault Hunters won’t be locked out of it, and the same items will be offered to them.

    ![A Borderlands 4 screenshot showing Maurice's Black Market Vending Machine on cooldown](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2025/09/borderlands4_guide_blackmarketvendingmachine_cooldown.jpg?q=49&fit=crop&w=825&dpr=2)Image: Gearbox Software/2K Games via Polygon

The Black Market Vending Machine always gets on cooldown after you leave its menu, whether you buy something or not. **The cooldown is around 30 minutes**. After this time, you can go back and buy another item.
