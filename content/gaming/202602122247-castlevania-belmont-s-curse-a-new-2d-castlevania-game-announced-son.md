In celebration of Castlevania's 40th anniversary, Konami has announced Castlevania: Belmont's Curse, a new stylized 2D Castlevania game co-developed by Evil Empire and Motion Twin. It's due out in 2026.

Konami says this is the first of the announcements to mark the series' fourth decade in existence, which started way back on the original Nintendo Entertainment System and has gone on to become [multiple](https://www.ign.com/articles/2017/07/09/castlevania-season-1-review) [successful](https://www.ign.com/articles/castlevania-nocturne-netflix) and critically acclaimed adult animated series on Netflix.
