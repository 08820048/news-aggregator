In total, there are **186 Pokémon** in [_Pokémon Champions_](https://www.polygon.com/pokemon-champions/), not including Mega Evolutions or regional forms, according to the in-game list, which can be found when viewing your achievements in the submenu. These range from plenty of iconic Pokémon, such as Charizard, Dragonite, and Snorlax, to more recent additions in later generations that are some of the most powerful Pokémon in the battling meta. All of these Pokémon can be recruited in-game, or transferred via Pokémon Home.

Here is the full list of Pokémon in the _Pokémon Champions_ Pokédex alongside their typing, followed by a list of every Mega in _Pokémon Champions_ too.
