## Netflix's One Piece is coming back sooner than expected for season 3

   ![four men and a woman sit by each othe on set for Netflix's live-action One Piece season 2](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2025/08/one-piece-live-action.jpg?&fit=crop&w=1600&h=900)Image: Netflix

The Straw Hats' Log Pose seems to be working perfectly well as Netflix's [_One Piece_](https://www.polygon.com/tag/one-piece/) has charted a course to return to streaming with its third season much sooner than anticipated. As [announced by Netflix](https://www.netflix.com/tudum/articles/one-piece-renewed-season-3) on Tuesday, season 3 of _One Piece_ is expected to arrive in 2027. This is a much shorter break than the one separating the first two seasons, which aired in 2023 and 2026.

Season 3 of the live-action _One Piece_ show will be titled _The Battle for Alabasta_. At the end of the second season, Luffy and his crew set sail for the Egyptian-inspired Alabasta Kingdom, after vowing to help their newfound friend and nakama, Princess Vivi, to save her land and defeat the evil Mr. Crocodile and his [Baroque Works](https://www.polygon.com/netflix-one-piece-season-2-trailer-baroque-works/) group. Season 3 will thus adapt the Alabasta Arc from the [manga](http://polygon.com/manga), which was _One Piece_'s first foray into a bigger-stakes type of story, with the fate of an entire kingdom resting on Luffy's bouncy shoulders.

The cast from season 2 returns, including not just the Straw Hats but their new friends and antagonists too: Charithra Chandran as Princess Vivi, Mikaela Hoover as Tony Tony Chopper, Joe Manganiello as Mr. 0, Lera Abova as Miss All Sunday, and Sendhil Ramamurthy as Alabasta King Nefertari Cobra. They will be joined by _Cobra Kai_’s Xolo Maridueña, who plays [Luffy's brother Portgas D. Ace](https://www.polygon.com/xolo-mariduena-ace-one-piece-live-action/), and Cole Estola (_Oh, Mary!_) as the flamboyant assassin with a heart of gold, Bon Clay.

Considering the time it took for season 2 to be produced, it's a pleasant surprise that Netflix's _One Piece_ will already be coming back in 2027. However, a faster season turnaround could be the norm going forward. The _One Piece_ manga is nearing its 1,200th chapter, and the anime is also getting closer to that number of episodes. If Netflix truly has the ambition to adapt Eiichiro Oda's epic tale in its entirety, waiting almost three years for a new season is unfeasible, unless they plan to switch Iñaki Godoy from playing Luffy to portraying his grandfather Garp.

With the battle for Alabasta reaching a bigger scale than anything seen before, season 3 will be an important test to see if a live-action show can continue to adapt a manga that will only keep getting bigger, weirder, and more spectacular with each new saga.
