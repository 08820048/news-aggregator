After a [_Borderlands 4_ downloadable-content drop in March](https://www.polygon.com/borderlands-4-bl4-dlc-story-pack-1-mad-ellie-vault-damned-release-time-edt-pdt/), no one expected a new game announcement. However, series developer Gearbox Software isn't behind this title — Zynga is. Yes, the latest [Borderlands](https://www.polygon.com/game/borderlands/8286/) is a mobile game. The good news is that _Borderlands Mobile_ looks to be pretty true to its cel-shaded source material.

The things you'd expect from a Borderlands game, like vaults, skags, and Claptrap are all here. Critically, _Borderlands Mobile_ is still a co-op experience where you hunt down increasingly powerful guns alongside your friends. The gameplay has been optimized for mobile touchscreens, but you could use an accessory like the Backbone controller to aim with real buttons. There's only one playable character at the moment, though eventually there will be four Vault Hunters in total.

There's an original campaign for this version of the game, and the missions are appropriately short for the platform. Based on early footage, _Borderlands Mobile_ does not appear to have the expansive open world of [_Borderlands 4_](https://www.polygon.com/borderlands-4-review/). Some features also aren't live yet, including the pricing for the game's battle pass. What is available in _Borderlands Mobile_ so far is surprisingly robust, players say — especially for a game that has been released with little notice or fanfare.

"I’m genuinely shocked," [one player wrote](https://www.reddit.com/r/Borderlands/comments/1sgshdv/borderlands_mobile_is_out_on_ios/?sort=new) in a Reddit thread full of surprised fans. "Just loaded in, it’s actually Borderlands. Not some gimmick."

The game appears to be exclusive to Apple devices in the United States for the time being, and it will presumably launch in the rest of the world at some point. Some fans report that they are having trouble finding _Borderlands Mobile_ in the App Store, but you can [check it out directly here](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/borderlands-mobile/id6756075968).
