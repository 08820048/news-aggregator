### 1-Pack Apple AirTag (Gen 1)

A second generation version of the Apple AirTag was released in January of 2026 and costs $29.99 for a single. The one on sale is the **first generation** model. The Apple Airtag is a small coin-shaped device that you can put in your wallet, attach to a keychain, or stow away discreetly in a bag. It works as a little Wi-Fi keyfinder that helps locate your lost objects by pinging its general location to your iPhone using Bluetooth 5.0. However, if your [iPhone model](https://www.ign.com/articles/the-best-iphones-to-buy) has a U1 chip with Ultra Wideband, then you can take advantage of the "Precision Finding" mode. This gives you numerical distance and direction guidance when your lost item is close by. It points you right to it.

The CR2032 coin battery is user-replaceable, so your AirTag won't end up in a landfill when the battery depletes, nor will you have to deal with battery degradation like you would with a non-replaceable lithium battery. Because the AirTag itself doesn't include any kind of loop or attachment, you may need to pick up an accessory to provide you with more versatile options. Fortunately, aftermarket accessories are common and inexpensive on Amazon.
