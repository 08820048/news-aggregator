The **Spire of the Stars puzzle** in [_Crimson Desert_](https://www.polygon.com/game/crimson-desert/40719/) is all about uncovering hidden stones, finding secret patterns, and, above all, figuring out how to move the glowing wall tiles. As part of the main story, it's important to make it to the top of the Spire of the Stars as you won't be able to enter the next area of the Abyss without it.

Here's **how to solve the Spire of the Stars puzzle** in _Crimson Desert_.

* * *

## Spire of the Stars puzzle walkthrough

The "Spire of the Stars" quest in _Crimson Desert_ starts after you've completed the [Scholastone puzzle](https://www.polygon.com/crimson-desert-scholastone-institute-puzzle-gate-otherworld-how-to-solve-quest/) and successfully [beat Tenebrum](https://www.polygon.com/crimson-desert-tenebrum-boss-how-to-beat-scholastone/) at the Scholastone Institute in southwest Hernand. To find the Spire of the Stars, take the snowy path north of the Scholastone Institute. At the top, you'll find a chamber with a set of glowing tiles on the back wall.

    ![The location of the Spire of the Stars in Crimson Desert.](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/crimson-desert-spire-of-the-stars-puzzle-2.jpg?q=49&fit=crop&w=825&dpr=2)Graphic: Marloes Valentina Stella/Polygon | Source image: Pearl Abyss via Polygon

First, you must **collect the three engraved stones**. Press the large stone buttons on the alcoves to your left and right to turn them around (use Force Palm or the normal push ability), each revealing an engraved stone.

    ![Arrows point to an alcove and the engraved stone it contains in the Spire of the Stars in Crimson Desert.](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/crimson-desert-spire-of-the-stars-puzzle-3.jpg?q=49&fit=crop&w=825&dpr=2)Graphic: Marloes Valentina Stella/Polygon | Source image: Pearl Abyss via Polygon

Place the engraved stones in the empty slots in the middle of the room to activate the puzzle. The second tile from the left, the one with the circle, already has a stone.

    ![Kliff uses engraves stones to activate the Crimson Desert Spire of the Stars puzzle.](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/crimson-desert-spire-of-the-stars-puzzle-4.jpg?q=49&fit=crop&w=825&dpr=2)Graphic: Marloes Valentina Stella/Polygon | Source image: Pearl Abyss via Polygon

To solve the Spire of the Stars puzzle, the pattern on the large wall should match the pattern shown on the walls to your left and right. Let's start with the wall on the left; as you can see, the **circular symbol** is already in the correct place (the second-highest position) so we don't need to touch it. The **square symbol**, however, should be lowered to the bottom row.

To do so, **climb up** to the tile with the square symbol and hang onto it. Your weight will drag it down.

    ![Kliff drags a tile downward to solve the Spire of the Stars puzzle in Crimson Desert.](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/crimson-desert-spire-of-the-stars-puzzle-5.jpg?q=49&fit=crop&w=825&dpr=2)Graphic: Marloes Valentina Stella/Polygon | Source image: Pearl Abyss via Polygon

As you can see on the wall to the right, the **bow-shaped symbol** is already placed correctly (at the top), so you don't need to move it. The **triangular symbol**, however, should take the second-lowest position. Again, climb up, hang onto the tile, and let go when it's in the right spot.

Once that's done, the _Crimson Desert_ Spire of the Stars puzzle solution should look like this:

    ![An arrow points to a symbol on the left, and the right side of the picture shows the full Spire of the Stars puzzle solution in Crimson Desert.](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/crimson-desert-spire-of-the-stars-puzzle-6.jpg?q=49&fit=crop&w=825&dpr=2)Graphic: Marloes Valentina Stella/Polygon | Source image: Pearl Abyss via Polygon

Should you accidentally drag a tile too far down, don't worry; you can reset the tile by dragging it to the bottom position and pushing the symbol below.

Now that you've completed the Spire of the Stars puzzle, you can **take the circular elevator platform** upward. If you missed the elevator, push the button on the pillar. At the top of the staircase, you'll find the entrance to the Obsession and Madness Abyss.
