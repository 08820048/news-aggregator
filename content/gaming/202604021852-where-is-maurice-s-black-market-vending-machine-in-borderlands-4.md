**Maurice’s Black Market Vending Machine** is one of the weekly challenges you can pursue in _[Borderlands 4](https://www.polygon.com/game/borderlands-4/443248/)_ after you have completed the campaign.

Also known as the legendary vending machine, this unique service appears once a week with a select inventory of legendary guns and ordnance for you to buy — but only if you can find it! The Black Market Vending Machine’s location changes after [_Borderlands 4_’s weekly reset](https://www.polygon.com/borderlands-4-weekly-reset-time-challenges-when-bl4/), and its icon only appears on the map when you get near it.

To help you locate this special vending machine and grab some legendary gear before the reset, we’ve prepared this guide showing **where to find Maurice’s Black Market Vending Machine** in _Borderlands 4_ and how it works. If you need more help finding the area, check out our full [interactive _Borderlands 4_ map](https://www.polygon.com/map/borderlands-4-interactive-map-full-kairos-bl4/).

## Maurice’s Black Market Vending Machine location in Borderlands 4

With a new week, comes a new place to find Maurice's Black Market Vending Machine. During **April 2-9**, you can find the special machine in the **Dissected Plateau**, in the external **northeastern area of the Killing Floors facility**. The closest [fast-travel station](https://www.polygon.com/borderlands-4-all-fast-travel-locations-spots-safe-houses-silos-bl4/) is the Wyclef's Reprieve Safehouse to the west.

    ![A montage using two Borderlands 4 images showing the location of Maurice's Black Market Vending Machine in April 2nd](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/04/b4_guide_maurice_april2_map_1.jpg?q=49&fit=crop&w=825&dpr=2)Graphic: Paulo Kawanishi/Polygon | Source images: Gearbox Software/2K Games via Polygon

From the Safehouse, **follow the road east**, but, instead of entering the facility using the main gate, **turn left and keep going east** while **going around the farm**. Eventually you will find a hill leading to some **mangler nests** near some trees. Climb your way up the eastern side of this area, then use the stairs to access a platform, and find Maurice's Vending Machine a bit ahead.

## Maurice’s Black Market Vending Machine, explained

Unlike other vending machines, Maurice’s Black Market Vending Machine is a special one that only sells legendary items. **You can buy as many items from it as you want to** — if you have the money, since these items are quite expensive.

If you have more than one character, **you can buy from the machine with all of them**. You can even buy more of the same item. When you use Maurice’s Black Market Vending Machine with one character, your other Vault Hunters won’t be locked out of it, and the same items will be offered to them.

    ![A Borderlands 4 screenshot showing Maurice's Black Market Vending Machine on cooldown](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2025/09/borderlands4_guide_blackmarketvendingmachine_cooldown.jpg?q=49&fit=crop&w=825&dpr=2)Image: Gearbox Software/2K Games via Polygon

The Black Market Vending Machine always gets on cooldown after you leave its menu, whether you buy something or not. **The cooldown is around 30 minutes**. After this time, you can go back and buy another item.
