### Arcade1Up Mortal Kombat II Deluxe Arcade Cabinet

This Arcade1Up Cabinet has spectacular art on the sides of Raiden from Mortal Kombat II, with the classic MKII logo sitting along the front. There's even a molded coin door to bring that authentic arcade experience! The cabinet itself features a 17" LCD, with two joysticks and sets of buttons for easy 2P action.

As part of this cabinet, you'll get four MK games: Mortal Kombat, Mortal Kombat II, Mortal Kombat 3, and Ultimate Mortal Kombat 3. Additionally, ten other games are packed in, such as Toobin', Defender, Rampage, Bubbles, Paperboy, and more. Wi-Fi capability is also supported, so you can connect to online leaderboards and multiplayer to battle it out with others whenever you wish.
