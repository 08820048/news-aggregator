## Life Is Strange fans to have a million feelings over the TV series casting

It's a [hella](https://preview.redd.it/s1-the-most-cringe-phrases-of-the-first-game-v0-wcp79xwu27r81.jpg?width=732&format=pjpg&auto=webp&s=5abe25404d0633233da0ca1b0f571d90d9b534b2) exciting time to be a Life is Strange fan. Not only is a [brand-new game](https://www.polygon.com/life-is-strange-reunion-preview/) on the way this March, but the latest news is that the _Life is Strange_ TV adaptation from Prime Video has found the perfect actors to play Max and Chloe.

Tatum Grace Hopkins (_Queen of Versailles_) will take on the awkward mantle of shy geek Max Caulfield, with Maisy Stella (_My Old Ass_) stepping into the spiked boots of troubled punk, Chloe Price. Hopkins celebrated the announcement on Instagram, wearing a shirt that eagle-eyed Life is Strange fans instantly [recognized as a reference](https://x.com/AdnanRiaz9/status/2028986438612668475?s=20).

The adaptation is based on the critically acclaimed video game franchise from Dontnod, which follows photography student Max, who discovers she can rewind time after witnessing a tragic incident involving her childhood best friend, Chloe. The pair team up to try to understand Max's new powers, all while investigating the mysterious disappearance of Rachel Amber. Their investigation leads them to uncover a dark side of Arcadia Bay, one that forces them to make an impossible choice.

    ![An image from Life is Strange Remastered. It shows Chloe Price and Max Caulfield sitting in Chloe's truck together. Sunlight filters inside the car and over their faces.](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/01/life-is-strange-1.jpg?q=49&fit=crop&w=825&dpr=2)Image: Square Enix

Rumors of a TV series based on the time-bending narrative adventure have circulated since [2016](http://kotaku.com/life-is-strange-is-getting-adapted-into-a-digital-tv-se-1784398251), but it was only in September last year that the project was confirmed to be greenlit, with _The End of the F\*\*\*king World c_reator Charlie Covell serving as executive producer and showrunner. The news left [some fans](https://www.reddit.com/r/gaming/comments/1n99ilv/life_is_strange_tv_series_set_at_amazon_from/) feeling conflicted about [how the series would handle a game](https://www.polygon.com/life-is-strange-tv-adaptation-fan-debates-max-chloe/) with two very different endings.

The new casting reveal hasn't exactly eased my concerns about that all-important and life-shattering ending of the original game, but it does leave me feeling hopeful about the direction Prime Video is taking the adaptation. The casting of Stella in particular seems an inspired choice, as her portrayal in _My Old Ass_ of someone [navigating the minefield of sexual expression](https://pridesource.com/article/how-maisy-stellas-own-queerness-enriches-her-performance-in-my-old-as) and queer identity was poignant and deeply authentic. While _Life is Strange_ blends sci-fi, fantasy, and mystery with a modern-day setting, queerness still plays a key role in Max and Chloe's relationship. It's too soon to say whether that'll be addressed openly in the adaptation, but if it is, I know Stella has the range to play it beautifully.

After a decade with these characters, only time will tell if Hopkins and Stella will be able to capture the same emotional intensity (or the cringeworthy dialogue) as the original game. Considering the [positive fan reaction](http://www.reddit.com/r/lifeisstrange/comments/1rjv3yv/comment/o8g10fz/?utm_source=share&utm_medium=web3x&utm_name=web3xcss&utm_term=1&utm_content=share_button) so far, however, it looks as though the Life is Strange community is ready for whatever the TV adaptation is willing to throw at them, [shaka brah](https://preview.redd.it/s1-the-most-cringe-phrases-of-the-first-game-v0-in8rs01v27r81.jpg?width=1080&crop=smart&auto=webp&s=af1e8d38fdb202012bedbbcf7d720953a2a687b1).
