Zootopia 2 was Disney's biggest movie in 2025 thanks to a monstrous worldwide box office run. In fact, the popular sequel is currently [highest-grossing animated Hollywood film](https://www.ign.com/articles/zootopia-2-becomes-highest-grossing-animated-hollywood-film-of-all-time-28-years-later-the-bone-temple-struggles-during-opening-weekend) of all time, and is even [up for an Oscar](https://www.ign.com/articles/2026-oscar-nominations-live) at this weekend's ceremony. If you missed your chance to see it in theaters, the good news is that you now have the option to watch it at home.

### Zootopia 2

Zootopia 2 has officially landed on Disney+, where it will be streaming exclusively for the forseeable future. IGN had the chance to review the film when it first came out and [gave it a solid 8 out of 10](https://www.ign.com/articles/zootopia-2-review), saying: "Zootopia 2 brings all the same animated whodunnit action and clever animal jokes as the 2016 version with a welcome new cast of allies."

If you aren't interested in a Disney+ subscription, you can also choose to buy Zootopia 2 on digital. Prime Video initially offered options to rent Zootopia 2 digitally for $24.99 or buy it for $29.99, but right now the service is offering a deal that [drops that purchasing price to $12.99](https://zdcs.link/z3OqOw?object_uuid=71ce4931-b205-45ab-9a97-acc904de8bde&t=article). Purchasing the film means you can watch it whenever you'd like (as long as Amazon retains the rights to it).
