### Beats Studio Pro Wireless Noise Canceling Headphones

The Studio Pro is Beats' flagship headphone. It sounds better and is more comfortable than the Beats Solo 4 and boasts excellent active noise cancelation, which the Solo 4 lacks altogether. It carries over a lot of Apple's trademark features like spatial audio with head tracking, one touch pairing, and "Find My" functionality. It also has a Transparency mode so you can listen to the environment around you without removing your headphones.

The Beats Studio Pro has a USB Type-C port for charging.. You can also use the USB port to connect to your device in wired mode, which is uncommon nowadays (although you can also still use a traditional 3.5mm audio cable as well). In wireless Bluetooth mode, the Studio Pro can last up to 40 hours on a single charge. It folds compactly for easy transport.
