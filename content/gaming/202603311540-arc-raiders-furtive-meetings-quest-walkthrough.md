The **_Arc Raiders_** **"Furtive Meetings" quest** is all about finding secret meeting spots on Stella Montis. In one round, you must find three specific locations, then take the document you find at the end to Shani, so completing this with a safe pocket available is highly recommended. What's even better is that the reward for this quest may be the best of any in _Arc Raiders_: **a legendary Aphelion weapon**.

Here's **where to find all three secret meeting spots on Stella Montis for the "Furtive Meetings" quest** in [_Arc Raiders_](https://www.polygon.com/game/arc-raiders/443837/).

* * *

## 'Furtive Meetings' quest steps in Arc Raiders

There are five quest steps in total for "Furtive Meetings," and they all must be completed in one round. Here's what you need to do in order:

1.  Search for the secret meeting spot under a staircase in the Medical Research Area.
2.  Find the secret meeting spot near the ventilation shaft between Robotic Sandbox B and Seed Vault.
3.  Find the secret meeting spot at the bottom of the staircase connecting the Cultural Archives and Seed Vault.
4.  Search the meeting area.
5.  Bring the secret meeting info to Shani.

* * *

## Where to find the secret meeting spot under a staircase in Medical Research

    ![The left-hand side of the image shows the player in Arc Raiders looking down the corridor towards the sceret meeting spot in Medical Research on Stella Montis while the right-hand side of the image shows the location marked on the in-game map with a star.](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/arc-raiders-furtive-meetings-medical-research.jpg?q=49&fit=crop&w=825&dpr=2)Graphic: Ford James/Polygon Images: Embark Studios via Polygon

First up, head to Medical Research. The location you're looking for is in the southwesternmost corner; you'll be able to see the stairs at the end of the long corridor. Go under the stairs and you'll be given a prompt to photograph the meeting spot.

* * *

## Where to find the secret meeting spot near the ventilation shaft

    ![The left-hand side of the image shows the player in Arc Raiders taking a photograph for the objective while the right-hand side of the image shows the location marked on the in-game map with a star.](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/arc-raiders-furtive-meetings-ventilation-shaft.jpg?q=49&fit=crop&w=825&dpr=2)Graphic: Ford James/Polygon | Source images: Embark Studios via Polygon

Next up, you need to hightail it over to the area between Robotic Sandbox B and Seed Vault. Don't go all the way down the corridor towards the Seed Vault though because the spot you're looking for is to the side of the entrance to that path. The exact spot is next to some picture frames leaning against the wall.

* * *

## Where to find the secret meeting spot at the bottom of the staircase

    ![The left-hand side of the image shows the player in Arc Raiders taking a photograph for the objective while the right-hand side of the image shows the location marked on the in-game map with a star.](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/arc-raiders-furtive-meetings-cultural-archives.jpg?q=49&fit=crop&w=825&dpr=2)Graphic: Ford James/Polygon Images: Embark Studios via Polygon

The third and final meeting spot is at the bottom of another staircase, but this time, it's over near Cultural Archives. There's another staircase at the westernmost tip of Cultural Archives that connects it to the Seed Vault below. Photograph the meeting spot here and you'll be given a prompt to search the meeting area, which just involves interacting with the cardboard box nearby.

Search this to find the secret meeting info, then put that in your safe pocket to ensure you can return it to Shani. Do so and the quest is complete! Congratulations on your new Aphelion!
