## '3 for 2' Sale Highlight: All of A Court of Thorns and Roses

To score the buy two, get one free deal, you just need to add three qualifying books to your cart. In this case, any of the ACOTAR books 1-5 will qualify. When you check out, the lowest-priced item will be free. You don’t even need to be a Prime member to score these savings. Beyond books, this ‘[3 for 2’ deal applies to Blu-rays](https://www.ign.com/articles/amazon-3-for-2-4k-blu-ray-deal-spring-sale-2026), vinyl, CDs, and other physical media, so that you can mix and match to your liking.

The first and title book of the series, [A Court of Thorns and Roses](https://zdcs.link/QOjAgB?object_uuid=5784fe9b-36c6-485d-9b1b-ccee6ac20f16&t=article), follows human girl Feyre, who ends up in Prythian, a magical fairy realm, after an unfortunate hunting accident. There, she must adapt and learn how to survive. Beyond power struggles, combat, and danger, there are complex relationships and romance, too.

It’s a truly gripping read that leaves you fully invested in the world and desperate to grab [A Court of Mist and Fury](https://zdcs.link/9yP48O?object_uuid=5784fe9b-36c6-485d-9b1b-ccee6ac20f16&t=article), the second book, immediately after. It also happens to be most fans' favorite. The rest of the series also takes place in Prythian and covers the politics, wars, and relationships that rule this awesome fantasy realm.

If you’re looking to stay in the same universe as ACOTAR, Sara J Maas also has the Throne of Glass series. While both are standalone series, they live in the same universe and have a few connections. Many of the books from the [Throne of Glass series](https://zdcs.link/922w2Y?object_uuid=5784fe9b-36c6-485d-9b1b-ccee6ac20f16&t=article) are also a part of the buy 2, get 1 free sale if you’re looking to expand your romantasy collection even further.
