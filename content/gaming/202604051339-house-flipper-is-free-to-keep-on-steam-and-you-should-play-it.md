## You should play this house flipping game that's free to keep on Steam

  ![An abandoned building full of trash, including newspapers and broken furniture.](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/chorus/uploads/chorus_asset/file/10918739/house_flipper_2.jpg?&fit=crop&w=1600&h=900)

Home renovation game [_House Flipper_](https://www.polygon.com/2018/5/21/17377110/house-flippers-steam-best-seller/) is available for free on Steam until April 6. Not only is _House Flipper_ a great game, this deal will net you a discount if you decide to pick up its upcoming remaster.

Initially released by developer Empyrean and publishers Frozen District and PlayWay in 2018, _House Flipper_ fully lives up to its name. It's all about working on and fixing up homes to meet the demands of potential buyers, turning an arduous real-life task into a game that's surprisingly chill and cozy. If most of your time playing games like _Animal Crossing: New Horizons_ and _The Sims 4_ is spent working on your house, then you'll probably like _House Flipper_ or [its 2023 sequel](https://www.polygon.com/reviews/24012583/house-flipper-2-review/).

A House and Home Fest sale is currently running on Steam, and _House Flipper_ is available for free (rather than $24.99) until that sale ends on April 6. With just about a day left for this deal, make sure you take a moment to [go to its Steam page](https://store.steampowered.com/app/613100/House_Flipper/) and redeem it, regardless of if you plan on playing it immediately. It's worth picking up even without all its DLC.

Sometime in Q2 2026, [_House Flipper Remastered Collection_](https://store.steampowered.com/app/3710840/House_Flipper_Remastered_Collection/) will be released, bundling all the original's content together in one package with improved visuals, added voice acting, and some new jobs and items.

The collection will launch at $49.99, though simply having the original in your Steam library will [lower the price](https://store.steampowered.com/app/3710840/House_Flipper_Remastered_Collection/) to $46. The price will go down even further depending on how much DLC you own, with _House Flipper Remastered Collection_ costing $16 for those who own the original and six of its DLC packs.

Whether you just want to play the original or want that discount on the upcoming remaster, _House Flipper_ is worth adding to your Steam library before this free-to-keep deal ends at 1 p.m. EDT on April 6.
