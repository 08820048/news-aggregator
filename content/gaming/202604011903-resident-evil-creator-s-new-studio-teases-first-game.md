Shinji Mikami is back and making a new game under new owner Shift Up

## Resident Evil creator's new studio acquired by Stellar Blade dev, teases new horror game

   ![A photo of Shinji Mikami of Unbound and Hyung-Tae Kim of Shift Up pose](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/04/shift-up-x-unbound-_-partnership-announcement-ft-shinji-mikami-4-23-screenshot.jpeg?&fit=crop&w=1600&h=900)Image: Unbound, Shift Up

[Resident Evil](https://www.polygon.com/game/resident-evil/9315/) and [The Evil Within](https://www.polygon.com/game/the-evil-within/11251/) creator [Shinji Mikami](https://www.polygon.com/features/2014/2/20/5425802/shinji-mikami-the-evil-within/)'s new studio, Unbound Inc., has joined Shift Up, the developer behind [_Stellar Blade_](https://www.polygon.com/reviews/24137538/stellar-blade-review-shift-up-playstation-5/) and _Goddess of Victory: Nikke_. The two companies announced their partnership on Wednesday in a video that offers strong hints about Mikami's next game.

Mikami, who serves as founder and CEO of Unbound, says he’s “actively creating and producing a new game title” and that he's been "fully involved on-site" in developing what he describes as a "large game."

“I think you can expect a lot from us soon,” he says.

Based on the content of the video announcement for Unbound and Shift Up's partnership, it certainly looks like Mikami is working on a new horror game. While the video presents a tour of Unbound's Tokyo-based office and offers soundbites from the studio's game developers, those scenes are filled with terrifying-looking creatures skulking in the backgrounds: winged demons, imps, and what appears to be a "[biblically accurate angel](https://tvtropes.org/pmwiki/pmwiki.php/Main/AngelicAbomination)." Even a team dinner shows staff about to dine on a horrifying anglerfish.

Mikami is best known for the games he created while working at Capcom in the '90s and '00s. Mikami served as director on the original _Resident Evil_ and _Resident Evil 4_, and as producer on games like _Devil May Cry_ and _Dino Crisis_. He left Capcom in 2006 to co-found PlatinumGames, where he directed _Vanquish_. The veteran game designer then formed Tango Gameworks in 2010, where he directed and produced games [_The Evil Within_](https://www.polygon.com/2014/10/14/6896155/the-evil-within-review-xbox-one-PS4/), [_Hi-Fi Rush_](https://www.polygon.com/reviews/23574584/hi-fi-rush-review-game-pass-xbox-series-x-pc/), and [_Ghostwire: Tokyo_](https://www.polygon.com/reviews/22988757/ghostwire-tokyo-review-ps5-pc-release-date/).

Unbound was founded in May 2023, after [Mikami left Tango Gameworks](https://www.polygon.com/23611036/shinji-mikami-quits-tango-gameworks-bethesda-microsoft-evil-within/). According to [the company's website](https://www.unbound-games.co.jp/), Unbound aims to "grow into a team of around 100 people, and to be deeply involved from the inception of a new original IP project aimed at a worldwide audience." Shift Up says it has completed the acquisition of Unbound by acquiring all the company's shares as of April 1.

Unbound says it's working on a "high-end" original IP project powered by Unreal Engine 5 for PlayStation 5, Windows PC, and Xbox platforms.
