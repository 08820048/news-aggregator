Where to find a bulldone Pokémon in Withered Wasteland

   ![A Pokémon Pokopia screenshot of Onix](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/pokopia_guide_onix_header.jpg?&fit=crop&w=1600&h=900)Image: Koei Tecmo, Game Freak/Nintendo, The Pokémon Company via Polygon

**Onix is a Bulldoze Pokémon** required in [_Pokémon Pokopia_](https://www.polygon.com/pokemon-pokopia/) to progress in the main storyline in the Withered Wasteland.

Pokémon with this specialty are capable of removing debris, which is just the kind of ability you need to start rebuilding the first Pokémon Center in the game. While none of the initial creatures you meet can help you with that, deep inside the caves of the Withered Wasteland, you will find exactly who you need: Onix.

If you haven't been able to locate the giant rock snake, we will explain **where you can find Onix in** **_Pokémon Pokopia_**.

* * *

## How to get Onix in Pokémon Pokopia

When looking for a Bulldoze Pokémon to clean the Pokémon Center area, **you can find Onix inside a cave on the northwest side** of the Withered Wasteland, near the gate that will eventually take you to Rocky Ridge.

    ![A montage showing Onix's location in Pokémon Pokopia](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/pokopia_guide_onix_map.jpg?q=49&fit=crop&w=825&dpr=2)Graphic: Paulo Kawanishi/Polygon | Source images: Koei Tecmo, Game Freak/Nintendo, The Pokémon Company

To reach the exact location, start from the destroyed Pokémon Center and **go west**, following the path of red bricks. **Break through the wall under the bridge with Rock Smash** to access the shore on the other side.

**Keep heading north**, entering the rocky area, until you see a **green gate**. Before the gate, to the **east side**, there's an opening that takes you into a cave. Once you step inside, **you will hear someone asking for help**. Break down the brown rocky wall (the same kind of block you broke under the bridge), and you then you'll be able to see Onix through a small opening.

Although you have found Onix, you'll need to figure out a way to release it, since the rocks trapping Onix are too hard for Ditto, and even for Onix, to break. To release it, **you need to go talk to Professor Tangrowth and begin the "**[**Yawn up a Storm!**](https://www.polygon.com/pokemon-pokopia-yawn-up-a-storm-walkthrough-make-it-humid-100-point/)**" request** to [increase the area's humidity](https://www.polygon.com/pokemon-pokopia-increase-humidity-stuck-at-71/) and make it rain.
