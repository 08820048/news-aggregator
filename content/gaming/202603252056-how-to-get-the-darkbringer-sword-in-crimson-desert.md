The powerful **Darkbringer** in [_Crimson Desert_](https://www.polygon.com/game/crimson-desert/40719/) adds a high-fantasy flavor to Kliff's medieval adventure. There are many special weapons in the game, like the [Soul Spear](https://www.polygon.com/crimson-desert-soul-spear-how-to-get-antumbra-ritual-grounds-location/) and the [Frozen Anguish](https://www.polygon.com/crimson-desert-frozen-anguish-sword-how-to-get-location/). Obtaining them, however, requires you to face challenging foes and explore dangerous locations — a fair price to pay for a weapon that can be a game-changer.

If you want to add a strong sword with a unique special ability to your arsenal, here's **how to get the Darkbringer in** **_Crimson Desert_**.

* * *

## How to get the Darkbringer sword in Crimson Desert

If you plan on getting the Darkbringer sword, you must prepare for a long journey. This weapon is located at the **White Wastes Sanctuary** on the northwestern side of **Pailune**, which we've marked with a star on the map below.

    ![A Crimson Desert image of the in-game map showing the location of the WHite Wastes Sacntuary in Pailune](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/crimsondesert_guide_darkbringer_map.jpg?q=49&fit=crop&w=825&dpr=2)Graphic: Paulo Kawanishi/Polygon | Source images: Pearl Abyss via Polygon

Unfortunately, there is no quick way to reach the sanctuary. You can either get there by moving **north from Hernand** or by **heading northwest from Demeniss**. Additionally, Pailune is a mid-to-late-game area, so you'll encounter a lot of **Black Bear camps** during your trip. It's possible to avoid confrontation, but be sure to carry a bunch of food and unlock any abyss nexus on the way to the White Wastes Sanctuary.

Once you reach the sanctuary, you'll see a **giant skeleton** with a **yellow cloth in its mouth**. Approach the skeleton's head and collect the Darkbringer sword.

    ![A Crimson Desert screenshot showing a giant skeleton buried in snow](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/crimsondesert_guide_darkbringer_skeleton.jpg?q=49&fit=crop&w=825&dpr=2)Image: Pearl Abyss via Polygon

This is a two-handed weapon with 22 attack, critical rate level three, and attack speed level one. It has five abyss gear slots, and comes with three already equipped. Faster than the Frozen Anguish, this sword has a solid cleave, capable of hitting about three enemies simultaneously.

    ![A Crimson Desert screenshot showing Kliff using the Darkbringer, which released two yellow orbs](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/crimsondesert_guide_darkbringer_skill.jpg?q=49&fit=crop&w=825&dpr=2)Image: Pearl Abyss via Polygon

However, the main reason to go after this sword is because of **Ator's Orb**, an equipped abyss gear. The Ator's Orb gear will spawn a golden orb that will attack your enemies whenever you use a heavy attack. If you'd like to put it on a different weapon, head to a [witch](https://www.polygon.com/crimson-desert-witch-where-to-find-location-abyss-gear-equip-hermit/) to remove it from the Darkbringer sword and attach it on to another weapon.
