## Amazon Prime members can claim 12 free games this month, including a much-loved XCOM

   ![Soldiers with guns running past a holographic earth in a screenshot from XCOM Enemy Unknown Steam screen](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/04/xcom-enemy-unknown-steam-screen.jpg?&fit=crop&w=1600&h=900)Image: Firaxis Games, 2K

Amazon Prime members can get 12 free PC games this month. Unlike Sony's [free PlayStation Plus games](https://www.polygon.com/playstation-plus-april-2026-essential-free-games-list-ps4-ps5/), which you can only play while your subscription is active, Amazon's free games come via redeemable codes from various PC gaming storefronts, like the [Epic Games Store](https://www.polygon.com/epic-store-free-game-april-2-clone-drone-in-the-danger-zone/) and GOG. You'll still own and have access to them even if your Amazon Prime subscription comes to an end.

April's free games are headlined by [_XCOM: Enemy Unknown_](https://www.polygon.com/game/xcom-enemy-unknown/2731/), the seminal turn-based strategy game. Command your troops as you push back against an alien invasion. The code is for the _Complete Pack_ edition, meaning you'll get three packs of DLC in addition to the base game.

_XCOM: Enemy Unknown_ is available to claim now via a GOG code. _Total War: Pharaoh Dynasties_ is also available now through the Epic Games Store. They join [some of March's additions that are still claimable](https://www.polygon.com/amazon-prime-gaming-free-games-march-2026/), like _TinyTina’s Assault on Dragon’s Keep: A Wonderlands One-Shot_ and _Total War: Rome 2 — Emperor Edition_.

Here's everything new for April:

-   _XCOM: Enemy Unknown Complete Pack_ (GOG) — available now
-   _Total War: Pharaoh Dynasties_ (Epic Games Store) — available now
-   _A Rat's Quest: The Way Back Home_ (Epic Games Store) — April 9
-   _King of Retail_ (GOG) — April 9
-   _Snake Core_ (GOG) — April 16
-   _Monster Harvest_ (Epic Games Store) — April 16
-   _Detective Agency: Gray Tie Collector's Edition_ (Legacy Games) — April 16
-   _Neo Cab_ (GOG) — April 23
-   _The Pale Beyond_ (GOG) — April 23
-   _KinnikuNeko: Super Muscle Cat_ (Epic Games Store) — April 30
-   _Fantasy General_ (GOG) — April 30
-   _Pinball Spire_ (GOG) — April 30

Head to [Amazon Luna](https://luna.amazon.com/claims/home?g=s) (formerly Prime Gaming) to start claiming the free games. Amazon Prime members can also stream games to play in addition to owning the free ones. Here's everything newly available to stream in April:

-   _Bug Fables: The Everlasting Sapling_
-   _EA Sports FC 26_
-   _A Game About Digging a Hole_
-   _The Jackbox Party Pack 2_
-   _Letter Trek_
-   _A Little Golf Journey_
-   _Smurf's Kart_
-   _WRC Generations: Fully Loaded Edition_
