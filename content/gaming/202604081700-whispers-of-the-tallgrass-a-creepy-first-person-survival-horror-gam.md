Get your first look at the creepy Whispers of the Tallgrass, an upcoming first-person atmospheric survival horror game in which players must evade a stubborn predator amidst the tall grass of the abandoned and overgrown industrial wasteland, scavenging for survival and, uncovering the secrets behind what happened here, and hopefully, escaping.

You won't be save-scumming this one in order to preserve the horror and tension, as you can only save your progress in certain spots. Item placement is also randomized in each playthrough, though there are multiple escape paths – each with different risk factors. Check out the announcement trailer above and the first screenshots in the gallery below.

Whispers of the Tallgrass is due out later this year for PC (via Steam). [Wishlist](https://store.steampowered.com/app/4544620/Whispers_of_the_Tallgrass) it if you're interested.
