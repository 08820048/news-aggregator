## Starfield is finally coming to PS5, with a price drop

After months of speculation, leaks, and more than two years of Xbox console exclusivity, Bethesda Game Studios is bringing its 2023 sci-fi role-playing game [_Starfield_](https://www.polygon.com/game/starfield/40409/) to PlayStation 5. _Starfield_'s PS5 version will be released on April 7, alongside the "biggest update to the game since launch," Bethesda Softworks announced Tuesday.

The PS5 release of _Starfield_ will take advantage of the [PlayStation](https://www.polygon.com/playstation/) console's distinctive features. The game will support the [DualSense controller](https://www.polygon.com/playstation-5-ps5-anniversary-dualsense-controller/)'s light bar, adaptive triggers, and touch pad. On [PlayStation 5 Pro](https://www.polygon.com/news/446873/playstation-5-pro-price-release-date-specs/) consoles, _Starfield_ will feature two graphics options: a Pro Performance Mode to prioritize a higher-target frame rate or Pro Visual Mode.

_Starfield_'s leap to PS5 signals a shift for Bethesda, which became a first-party Xbox studio in 2021. In a [2020 interview with Kotaku](https://kotaku.com/xbox-boss-phil-spencer-on-series-x-launch-halo-infinit-1845392984), before Microsoft's acquisition of Bethesda was finalized, then head of Xbox Phil Spencer said the company did not need to put _The Elder Scrolls 6_ on PlayStation to recoup that investment, suggesting games developed and published by Bethesda would be Xbox console exclusives. _Starfield_'s release bore that out, as did the 2024 release of _Indiana Jones and the Great Circle_ (which is also now on PS5). But in a [2025 interview with Save State Plus](https://www.ign.com/articles/is-starfield-coming-out-on-ps5-phil-spencer-wont-rule-it-out), Spencer would not rule out that _Starfield_ could come to PS5.

The base edition of _Starfield_’s PS5 version will cost $49.99, the same price as the Xbox Series X and Windows PC versions of the game going forward. A Premium Edition release for PS5 will also be available, both digitally and physically.

Bethesda Game Studios will release two major content updates for _Starfield_ on April 7: new story DLC expansion _Terran Armada_ and a free major update called Free Lanes. You can read more about those additions to _Starfield_ in our preview of the update.

_Starfield_ launched in September 2023, to mostly positive reviews. On review aggregation site [OpenCritic](https://opencritic.com/game/14907/starfield), _Starfield_ has a top critic average score of 85. But we were cooler on the game than other outlets, writing in [our review](https://www.polygon.com/reviews/23852522/starfield-review-xbox-series-x-pc-exclusive-bethesda-microsoft/) that "Bethesda’s ambition has exceeded its craft" with _Starfield_.

"There is so much to do and so many people to meet across the cosmos, on new worlds to which humans have escaped after fleeing a blighted Earth," Polygon reviewer Nicole Carpenter wrote at the time. "For all of this vastness, though, _Starfield_ often feels sterile, and it buries its best moments beneath so much tedium."
