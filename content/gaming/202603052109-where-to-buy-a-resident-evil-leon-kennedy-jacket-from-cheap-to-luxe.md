## There's a cool Leon Kennedy jacket for every budget

   ![Resident_Evil_Requiem_-_Leon_Artwork](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/resident_evil_requiem_-_leon_artwork.jpg?&fit=crop&w=1600&h=900)Image: Capcom

_Resident Evil Requiem_ has arrived in grand fashion, becoming the [fastest-selling game in the series](https://www.polygon.com/resident-evil-requiem-sales/) by moving more than 5 million copies in five days. And the star of the show is returning hero Leon S. Kennedy, now a greying hottie that the entire internet seems to be [thirsting over](https://www.polygon.com/resident-evil-requiem-leon-kennedy-thirst/). But Leon's appeal goes further than just his smoldering gaze, bulging biceps, and timely one-liners — the man has impeccable taste in jackets, and enterprising online retailers have taken note. There are tons of replica Leon Kennedy jackets out there, in a wide range of price points, ranging from just shy of $40 to nearly two grand.

You'll find more of Leon's _Resident Evil 4_ looks, since that game's been out so much longer. But we're already starting to see a couple riffs on his black leather peacoat with fur trim from _Requiem_. Whether you're looking for a low-cost cosplay option, or a durable and luxurious leather jacket to wear for years to come, we've got you covered.

## A near-match Requiem look in real leather

**Men's Shearling B7 Bomber**

While Leon's _RE4_ look was inspired by the B3 bomber jackets of the World War II era, his _Requiem_ outerwear takes inspiration from the B7, another aviator style. While this black-on-black number from Arcane Fox ditches the double-breasted buttons of Leon's in-game look, the zippers give it a more modern and practical vibe. It's made of genuine leather with a faux shearling lining, and hits at about mid-thigh for cold winter temperatures. It's not an exact duplicate, but it's a versatile leather jacket for under $400.
