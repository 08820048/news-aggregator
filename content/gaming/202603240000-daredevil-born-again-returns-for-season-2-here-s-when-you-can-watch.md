## **Daredevil: Born Again Season 2 Release Schedule**

The second season of Daredevil: Born Again will premiere on March 24, 2026. The season will run for eight episodes total, with **new episodes releasing at 9pm ET/6pm PT every Tuesday**.

After a series of time jumps in the revival’s first season, the new season picks up in the middle of the MCU’s Phase 6. We’ve gotten a couple of sneak peeks of what to expect from Season 2 in teasers and trailers, including a [new Daredevil suit](https://www.ign.com/articles/daredevil-has-a-new-blackout-suit-in-born-again-season-2-and-theres-a-very-specific-reason-for-it) and the [return of Jessica Jones](https://www.ign.com/articles/krysten-ritter-officially-returning-as-jessica-jones-for-daredevil-born-again-season-2).

Since the first season, the Daredevil himself has also achieved a new world of fame from voicing Gustave in last year’s [Game of the Year winner](https://www.ign.com/articles/clair-obscur-expedition-33-sets-world-record-for-game-of-the-year-awards-surpassing-elden-ring), Clair Obscur: Expedition 33. While Cox has [continuously remarked](https://www.ign.com/articles/daredevil-star-charlie-cox-says-he-feels-like-a-total-fraud-whenever-someone-congratulates-him-for-clair-obscur-expedition-33s-success-i-was-in-a-studio-for-four-hours-maybe) that he isn’t a gamer, he [recently revealed](https://www.ign.com/articles/daredevil-star-charlie-cox-working-on-new-video-game-following-clair-obscur-expedition-33-success) that he's already working on a new gaming project.

### **What We Know About Season 3**

Daredevil: Born Again has already been greenlit [for a third season](https://www.ign.com/articles/daredevil-born-again-officially-greenlit-for-season-3-shooting-starts-next-year), with shooting starting this summer. As of now, Season 3 is scheduled to premiere in Spring 2027. We still have plenty of Phase 6 between now and then, including [Spider-Man: Brand New Day](https://www.ign.com/movies/spider-man-brand-new-day) and [Avengers: Doomsday](https://www.ign.com/movies/avengers-doomsday). You can check out the [full MCU schedule](https://www.ign.com/articles/upcoming-new-next-marvel-movies-2026-tv-shows-release-dates) for more details on that.
