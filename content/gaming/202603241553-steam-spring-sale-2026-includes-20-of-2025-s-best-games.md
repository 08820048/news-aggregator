The [Steam Spring Sale](https://www.polygon.com/steam-spring-sale-2026-deals-best/) is on, and it's a great way to stock up on some of [last year's biggest games](https://www.polygon.com/steam-spring-sale-2026-deals-best/) without breaking the bank. It's an even better way to round up a boatload of lesser-known indie games that you may have missed for the same price as one full-priced, big budget game. Tons of excellent indies are currently on sale, with many excellent options available for under $10.

Not sure what to pick up? It just so happens that most of the indie games that made our [top 50 games of 2025](https://www.polygon.com/best-games-2025/) list are up to 40% off. Here's a rundown including 20 of our favorite 2025 gems that you can get for a discount until the sale ends on March 26.

## Baby Steps

    ![Baby Steps’ main character, Nate, walking up the side of a very dangerous looking cliff](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/chorus/uploads/chorus_asset/file/24711593/Copy_of_Baby_Steps_5.jpg?q=49&fit=crop&w=825&dpr=2)Image: Devolver Digital

[_Baby Steps_](https://www.polygon.com/baby-steps-review/) is no joke, even if it's hilarious. The deviously difficult walking simulator from the brains behind _Ape Out_ tests your patience as you try to guide the wobbly-limbed Nate through a massive fantasy world. It's a riotously funny physical comedy, but it has a surprisingly effective story about a failson struggling with the pressures of modern masculinity. It's [currently 35% off](https://store.steampowered.com/app/1281040/Baby_Steps/), taking the price down to $12.99, so pick it up and find out why it made our top 10 last year.

## Despelote

   ![The main character of Despelote kicks a soccer ball into a pyramid of cones](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2025/10/despelote.jpeg?q=70&fit=crop&w=825&dpr=1)

_Despelote_

Image: Julián Cordero, Sebastian Valbuena/Panic

In a year ruled by grand RPGs, the two-hour [_Despelote_](https://www.polygon.com/review/601192/despelote-review-childhood-ecuador-football-sorry-soccer/) blew us away so much that it secured the #3 spot on our year-end list. You'll understand why as soon as you play it. The slice-of-life story of a kid growing up in Ecuador amid World Cup fever is an extraordinary coming-of-age story about how sports intersect with culture. You can [grab it for $8.99](https://store.steampowered.com/app/2367820/despelote/) during the Steam Spring Sale, knocking 40% off its ususal price tag.

## Promise Mascot Agency

    ![Pinky rides in the back of a truck in Promise Mascot Agency.](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2025/12/promise-mascot-agency-pinky-in-van.jpg?q=49&fit=crop&w=825&dpr=2)Image: Kaizen Game Works

What was the best open-world game of 2025? For my money, the answer is easily [_Promise Mascot Agency_](https://www.polygon.com/gaming/560594/promise-mascot-agency-review/). The quirky game from the minds behind _Paradise Killer_ rolls a Yakuza crime story, Forza Horizon, a mascot management sim, a card-battler, and a mayoral debate game into one eclectic experience. Its [30% price cut](https://store.steampowered.com/app/2585830/Promise_Mascot_Agency/) during the Steam Spring Sale takes the price down to $17.49, which is a fair price to pay for all the memorable zaniness _Promise Mascot Agency_ brings.

## Blippo+

    ![A newscaster in Blippo+ considers the best games of 2025](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2025/12/blippo-plus.jpg?q=49&fit=crop&w=825&dpr=2)Image: Yacht, Noble Robot, Telefantasy Studios/Panic

Have you ever had the experience of watching TV during an all-consuming global news event? That's what _Blippo+_ simulates in delightfully bizarre fashion. The intergalactic channel surfing simulator simply has you watching short shows broadcast from the planet Blipp. Through those clips, you'll slowly uncover the story of a mysterious event that could change the universe as the Blippians know it. You just need to spend time with it to appreciate it, so take advantage of its [30% price reduction](https://store.steampowered.com/app/3323850/Blippo/) and grab it for $9.74. Otherwise, get bent!
