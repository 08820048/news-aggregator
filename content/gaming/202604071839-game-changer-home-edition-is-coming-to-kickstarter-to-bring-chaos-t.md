Not only is a [new season of _Game Changer_ on the way](https://bsky.app/profile/gamechanger.dropout.tv/post/3mits4kmfh72s), but so is a new way to experience its chaos and comedy — at your house. On Tuesday, Dropout CEO [Sam Reich](https://www.polygon.com/q-and-a/553902/game-changer-season-7-premiere-one-year-later-paraloubis-voice-mail-musical/) announced the company will be launching a Kickstarter next month for _Game Changer: Home Edition_.

[_Game Changer_](https://www.polygon.com/dropout-game-changer-jacob-wysocki-interview/) has been a flagship series for the streamer since it debuted in 2019. Each episode (save for a few "game samers") features contestants playing a different game, with none of the participants knowing exactly what they're walking into. True to the premise, what games _Game Changer: Home Edition_ will include aren't known at this time. In a series of posts on [Bluesky](https://bsky.app/profile/samreich.bsky.social/post/3miw6x4prcr2n), Reich told fans to [follow the Kickstarter](https://www.kickstarter.com/projects/dropout/game-changer-home-edition?ref=dh8fbm) to find out what games they can expect to play (and [terrorize their friends with](https://www.polygon.com/24128357/game-changer-bingo-twist-season-6/)). He also teased the games will be inspired by different episodes of the show.

Reich said the company could have launched a home game years ago, but it wanted to wait "to get it right." He noted "this game ~format~ will allow for us to continue to evolve the game over time, making it something Dropout can continue to invest in."

Dropout is using Kickstarter so that it can evolve the game over time due to backer feedback. "As you’ll see when we launch our Kickstarter on May 5, we’ve got very specific plans for involving all of you in finishing the game!" an answer on the game's [FAQ](https://gamechangerhomeedition.reamaze.com/kb/frequently-asked-questions/kickstarter-pre-launch-faqs) reads.

This isn't the first time Dropout has made a game based on one of its shows via Kickstarter. It partnered with Wiggles 3D to make a board game version of [_Um, Actually_](https://www.polygon.com/ify-nwadiwe-um-actually-dropout-interview-season-11/), which is [now available to purchase from Dropout's store](https://store.dropout.tv/products/um-actually-board-game?_pos=2&_sid=573903330&_ss=r). It's not yet known if _Game Changer: Home Edition_ will be made available to purchase outside the Kickstarter at a later date, though don't expect to see it on store shelves anytime soon. The Kickstarter FAQ says it "will not be available for wholesale at this time."

Expect to hear more details about _Game Changer: Home Edition_ and its campaign rewards once the Kickstarter launches on May 5.
