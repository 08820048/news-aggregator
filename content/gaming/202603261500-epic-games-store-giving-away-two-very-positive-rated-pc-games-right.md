## PC gamers only have one week to claim these games for free

   ![An image with the Epic Games Store logo next to an illustration showing various shapes and user interfaces.](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/chorus/uploads/chorus_asset/file/24491371/egspublishing.jpg?&fit=crop&w=1200&h=675)Image: Epic Games

The Epic Games Store offers a couple of free [PC](https://www.polygon.com/pc/) games every week to users, and the picks are always a different mix of genres. This week Epic users can claim an ocean-bound cozy sim and a space-themed retro shooter for no charge.

## Havendock

[_Havendock_](https://store.epicgames.com/en-US/p/havendock-64983e) is a cozy sim where you build up a colony on the ocean. You'll create your character, build a town from the ground up (or, should I say, _water_ up), and manage your fellow colonists. Raise animals, grow crops, invent gadgets, explore what's beneath the surface, and more. Over on Epic's competitor Steam, _Havendock_ has over 1,400 reviews and a "Very Positive" rating.

## Hyper Echelon

Retro shoot-'em-ups, like [_Minishoot' Adventures_](https://www.polygon.com/minishoot-adventures-switch-recommendation/), are always a good time, and you can get one for free from Epic. [_Hyper Echelon_](https://store.epicgames.com/en-US/p/hyper-echelon-2d23ff) has you battling an evil alien force via top-down shooting. Pilot and upgrade your little star fighter across 10 different worlds and four different gameplay modes, including a boss rush mode and survival missions. Grab a friend for the ride as _Hyper Echelon_ supports two-player local co-op.

If _Havendock_ and _Hyper Echelon_ catch your eye, all you need to do is log in to the [Epic Games Store](https://store.epicgames.com/en-US/) and claim them. They'll be free until Thursday, April 2, at 11 a.m. EDT.

Earlier in March, Epic gave away [_Electrician Simulator_ and DLC for _World of Warships_](https://www.polygon.com/epic-store-free-games-march-19-electrician-simulator/); [_Cozy Grove_ and _Isonzo_](https://www.polygon.com/free-on-epic-games-store-cozy-grove/); and [free content for _Idle Champions of the Forgotten Realms_ and _Turnip Boy Robs a Bank_](https://www.polygon.com/epic-store-free-games-march-5-idle-champions-dragonlance-dlc/).
