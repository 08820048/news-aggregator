## The Super Mario Galaxy Movie has a massive Star Fox cameo and a big name attached

   ![Mario rides on Yoshi's back in artwork from The Super Mario Galaxy Movie](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/mario-yoshi-galaxy-movie.jpg?&fit=crop&w=1600&h=900)Image: Nintendo, Illumination

The week before Nintendo and Illumination's release of [_The Super Mario Galaxy Movie_](https://www.polygon.com/super-mario-galaxy-movie-nintendo-direct-new-trailer/), the film's creators have decided to reveal one of its major cameos early. The reveal will certainly reignite speculation that the "Nintendo Cinematic Universe" encapsulates more than just [Super Mario characters](https://www.polygon.com/super-mario-galaxy-movie-trailer-easter-eggs/) (and Pikmin).

On Thursday, Nintendo and Illumination confirmed that the rumors were true: Fox McCloud from the [Star Fox](https://www.polygon.com/game/star-fox/11516/) games will appear in _The Super Mario Galaxy Movie_. It's currently unconfirmed just how big Fox's role will be in Mario's new adventure, but he sure looks like a major character. In a teaser released on [the Mario movie's Instagram account](https://www.instagram.com/p/DWWTs4CAtYL/), we see Fox McCloud posing dramatically in front of his Arwing while an Ukiki looks on in adoration.

Let _The Super Smash Bros. Movie_ rampant speculation begin! Er, continue!

On Friday, there was another big reveal: Fox McCloud's voice actor is none other than Glen Powell (_Top Gun Maverick_, _Hit Man_). Powell [revealed his role in _The Super Mario Galaxy Movie_ in a video](https://www.instagram.com/p/DWY33wIAHOg/) in which he's dressed up as McCloud.

Fox McCloud made his debut in the original _Star Fox_ released in 1993 for the Super NES. Developed by Nintendo and Argonaut Software, _Star Fox_ is a rail shooter game that employed early 3D graphics and served as a showcase for the SNES' Super FX chip. Mario creator Shigeru Miyamoto served as a producer and designer on the game. Fox McCloud has starred in multiple sequels, including the most recent release, _Star Fox 2_, a game that was originally completed in 1995, but was only released in 2017 as part of the [Super NES Classic Edition](https://www.polygon.com/2017/9/27/16372832/snes-classic-review-super-nintendo-mini-edition/). _Star Fox 2_ is also available [via Nintendo Switch Online](https://www.polygon.com/2019/12/4/20996289/nintendo-switch-online-snes-nes-games-december-star-fox-2-super-punch-out/).

It was previously confirmed that _The Super Mario Galaxy Movie_ will feature some characters who are not typically thought of as part of the Super Mario Bros. universe, including Pikmin and Nintendo's ROB, but just how far Nintendo wants to stretch the boundaries of Mario canon.

We'll know much more soon. _The Super Mario Galaxy Movie_ opens in movie theaters on April 1.
