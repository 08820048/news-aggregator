There are a lot of Pokémon in [_Pokémon Pokopia_](https://www.polygon.com/pokemon-pokopia/), and the game has its own **Pokédex**, which sorts the Pokémon loosely based on where you can find them, with legendary Pokémon being at the bottom of the list.

If you're trying to figure out what Pokémon is in that gap in your Pokédex or you just want to Ctrl + F to see if your favorite made the list, we got your back. Below we list the Pokédex Pokémon in _Pokopia_ (say that three times fast, jeez), as well as their habitats and specialties.

* * *

## Pokémon Pokopia Pokédex and available Pokémon list

Below, we table out all the Pokémon we can confirm in the game so far, along with their [required habitat](https://polygon.com/pokemon-pokopia-habitat-dex-list-requirements-unlock/) and what specialty they bring to the table. Some Pokémon are only available during daytime or nighttime (so far, we haven't really found any Pokémon that are only available in the mornings/dawn or evenings/dusk, but we do still list those the same way the in-game description does), so keep that in mind as you wait for your desired Pokémon to show up.

We're still working on confirming more Pokémon and habitats (there's a lot!), but we'll update this guide with more as we find them. Note that the Pokémon with question marks next to their names indicate that we haven't confirmed this Pokémon yet — but it's likely in this spot given that it's an evolution of something else we've found.
