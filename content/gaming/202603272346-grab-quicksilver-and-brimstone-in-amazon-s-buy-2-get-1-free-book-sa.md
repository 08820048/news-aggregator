The author of the series, Callie Hart, is a master of the genre, bringing magic, war, and the classic enemies-to-lovers trope to life in a compelling, can’t-put-it-down story. That means now’s a great time to pick up this deal on Quicksilver and Brimstone to catch up or reread one of the [best romantasy book](https://www.ign.com/articles/best-romantasy-book-series) series out there.

## Fae & Alchemy ‘3 for 2’ Sale

To score the buy 2, get 1 free deal, all you need to do is add three qualifying books to your cart, and at checkout, the cheapest one will be automatically discounted. If you don’t have another book you want to grab, the [‘3 for 2’ deal applies to Blu-rays](https://www.ign.com/articles/amazon-3-for-2-4k-blu-ray-deal-spring-sale-2026), vinyl, comics, and other physical media, and you can mix and match to your choosing. Both the standard hardcover and deluxe editions of Quicksilver and Brimstone are included in the sale, and the awesome metallic edges and unique cover effects of the deluxe editions are especially great for collectors.

### More About the Fae & Alchemy Series

These stories center on Saeris, a 24-year-old woman who’s skilled at thievery and possesses hidden alchemical powers. She accidentally ends up being transported to an icy Fae realm. There, she becomes unwillingly intertwined with a dark and brooding Fae warrior, and finds herself stuck in the middle of a prolonged war between the fae and an enemy in the realm. Politics, magic, secrets, and dark romance are all central themes that keep you hooked on the series.
