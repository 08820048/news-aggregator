The **"Bustling Hill" quest** in [_Crimson Desert_](https://www.polygon.com/game/crimson-desert/40719/) is part of Chapter 3's **"Pioneering" mission**, a mandatory step in the campaign and a special one. Up until this point, your group has been growing, with more members always coming. Now, the Greymanes need to expand their power. Marius came up with a plan that involves sending members of the group to complete quests while Kliff takes care of the more important tasks.

In this guide, we explain **how to dispatch the "Howling Hill Camp Expansion" mission** during the **"Bustling Hill" quest in _Crimson Desert_**, and unlock the **game's mission system**.

* * *

## How to dispatch the 'Howling Hill Camp Expansion' mission

The "Bustling Hill" quest begins right after you read the letter from the dragon head at the Howling Hill camp during the "A Gentle Touch" quest. The goal is to dispatch a couple Greymanes to complete the "Howling Hill Camp Expansion" mission.

The first step is to access the Howling Hill mission menu. **Open the map** and use the **Inspect command** on the **Greymanes' camp** by either pressing Y (Xbox) and Triangle (PlayStation), or double-clicking with a mouse on the camp. The command only works if you're properly selecting the location, which is indicated by its map icon appearing raised.

    ![A Crimson Desert screenshot showing the Howling Hill camp while using the Inspect command](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/crimsondesert_guide_bustlinghill_inspect.jpg?q=49&fit=crop&w=825&dpr=2)Image: Pearl Abyss via Polygon

After a short cutscene that zooms into the camp, a new screen will appear. In the **top left corner**, you will see four tabs, with one of them being **"Missions."** Select it to find the only mission available: the **"Howling Hill Camp Expansion."**

    ![A Crimson Desert screenshot showing the Howling Hill Camp Expansion menu screen](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/crimsondesert_guide_bustlinghill_menu1.jpg?q=49&fit=crop&w=825&dpr=2)Image: Pearl Abyss via Polygon

Select it and, in the next step, you will have to choose Kliff's comrades to send out. For the "Howling Hill Camp Expansion," you only need two members, which is good considering you only have Luke and Ronald. Dispatch them by pressing F on the keyboard or X (Xbox) and Square (PlayStation) on the controller.

    ![A Crimson Desert screenshot showing the progress of the Howling Hill Camp Expansion mission](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/crimsondesert_guide_bustlinghill_progress.jpg?q=49&fit=crop&w=825&dpr=2)Image: Pearl Abyss via Polygon

The "Howling Hill Camp Expansion" mission takes **17 in-game hours to complete**. Although time passes faster in-game, you can speed up the process, by sleeping on a bed in your [house](https://www.polygon.com/crimson-desert-house-when-do-you-get-first-home) or camp, or waiting at a bonfire. However, you can only sleep and wait for up to 12 hours, so you'll still have to wait a bit for the mission to be completed.

Once the mission is complete, you receive a message saying the mission has ended and Kliff's companions are back.

## How to dispatch missions in Crimson Desert

By finishing the "Howling Hill Camp Expansion," you gain **500 experience points** with the **Greymane faction**. Completing the "Bustling Hill" quest also rewards you with a unique prize: **the mission dispatch system**. With this new system, you can send Kliff's group members to complete more missions to obtain **experience points with factions, money, and materials**.

    ![A Crimson Desert screenshow showing the mission meny of Timberham Sawmill](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/crimsondesert_guide_bustlinghill_rewards.jpg?q=49&fit=crop&w=825&dpr=2)Image: Pearl Abyss via Polygon

There are **two ways** to find available missions. First, **you can repeat the same process used during the "Bustling Hill" quest** and open the map, hover over a location, and use the Inspect command. This is the best method to use when you're away and exploring.

    ![A montage using two Crimson Desert images showing the location of Ross in the Greymanes' camp](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/crimsondesert_guide_bustlinghill_ross_map.jpg?q=49&fit=crop&w=825&dpr=2)Graphic: Paulo Kawanishi/Polygon | Source images: Pearl Abyss via Polygon

However, if you are at the Greymanes' camp, you want to talk to **Ross**, on the west side of the main road. He is the base **camp dispatch coordinator**. Besides giving you the option to manage your comrades, Ross will also list all the available missions, making the process of choosing missions much quicker.
