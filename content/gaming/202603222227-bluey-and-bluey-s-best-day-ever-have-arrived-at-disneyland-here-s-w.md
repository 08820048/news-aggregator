Bluey has officially arrived at Disneyland, complete with 'Bluey's Best Day Ever!' party at Fantasyland Theatre, themed food and merch, and so much more.

To ensure you don't miss a thing, here's everything you need to know about this big arrival of the beloved blue heeler and her family and friends. And yes, this is for real life!

[

](https://assets-prd.ignimgs.com/2026/03/22/bluey-1774217734122.png)

## Bluey's Best Day Ever! Is Perhaps Also the Best Party Ever Too

'Bluey's Best Day Ever!' party has arrived at Fantasyland Theatre, as the area has been reimagined as the grounds of Bluey's school, complete with a newly designed entryway with nods to the series itself.

Guests can look forward to seeing Bluey and Bingo on stage throughout the day in two unique rotating shows, and they will be joined by a troupe of comedic performers and musicians. Together, they will play beloved games (Keepy Uppy!), music, and dances with "cheeky moves" from the series.

There will also be some fun surprise appearances from the likes of Chattermax, Unicorse, a troupe of Grannies, and even a cavalcade of "larger-than-life" inflatables of Bluey's friends, including Jack, MacKenzie, Indy, Honey, and Rusty.

The music will also be a treat, as iconic songs will have new musical arrangements, and they will be performed by a five-piece band on drums, guitar, trumpet, saxophone, and trombone.

Elsewhere in the theatre, guests will be able to step into the life-sized Gnome Village and Fairy Garden, race Barky Boats down an imaginary waterway, hop on Bluey's helicopter stump for a pretend ride, take photos by Bluey's family car, fun fair map, and a class photo of Bluey and friends, and so much more.

## Bluey Food and Merch For the Whole Family

The fun extends beyond the theatre, as there will be a ton of delicious Bluey-inspired food to enjoy alongside some pretty special merch.

[

](https://assets-prd.ignimgs.com/2026/03/22/2026-dlr-bluey-whats-cooking-header-1774217818584.png)

At the Troubadour Tavern, guests can chow down on the following;

-   **Pizza Girls Baked Potato:** Pepperoni, sausage, bacon bits, marinara, mozzarella, alfredo sauce, and parmesan herb topper
-   **The Troubadour Baked Potato:** Butter, sour cream, cheese, bacon bits, and green onions
-   **Mini All-Beef Hot Dog** served with a Cuties Mandarin Orange, applesauce, and a choice of small DASANI Water or lowfat milk _(Kids meal)_
-   **Poffertjes! Poffertjes!:** Mini Dutch pancakes topped with buttered condensed milk and powdered sugar
-   **Watermelon Ice Block**
-   **Magic “Asparagus” Pretzel:** Pretzel rod coated in white chocolate
-   **Pavlova Parfait:** Meringue bites, vanilla yogurt mousse, berry compote, vanilla cake, fresh berries, and candied kiwi (New)
-   **Mud Cake Cold Brew:** Joffrey’s Coffee French Roast Cold Brew with a malted milk topper, fudge brownie bits, and sprinkles (Non-alcoholic)
-   **Lime Ice Block Drink:** Pineapple limeade with coconut milk and watermelon ice block (Non-alcoholic)
-   **Bluey Sipper**: Includes choice of fountain beverage (Limit two per person, per transaction; available while supplies last)

The Plaza Inn will also be home to the Stumpfest Cake (Chocolate roulade with white chocolate mousse and ganache), and select popcorn carts near Fantasyland Theatre, at small world Promenade, near Central Plaza, and near City Hall will have a Bluey Bucket while supplies last.

As for the merch, there will be a selection of Bluey apparel that features kids' tees and a kids' zip fleece, a Bluey and Bingo pin and lanyard, and even, at a later date, Bluey and Bingo Create Your Own Headband plushes.

[

](https://assets-prd.ignimgs.com/2026/03/22/dlp-dlr-novelty-blueysipper-02052026-dsc03222dn-revisedorange-1774217323743.JPG)

This merch, while supplies last, can be found at World of Disney, Elias & Co., and Fantasyland Theatre Valet.
