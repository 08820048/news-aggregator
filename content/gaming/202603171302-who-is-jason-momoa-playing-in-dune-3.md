Jason Momoa's return in Dune 3 is part of a plot that connects all of Frank Herbert's books

## Duncan Idaho died in Dune so who is Jason Momoa playing in Dune 3?

Legendary Entertainment shared [a series of character posters](https://www.instagram.com/Legendary/) for Denis Villeneuve’s [_Dune 3_](https://www.polygon.com/24085898/dune-3-release-date-cast-news/) on March 16 to tease the film’s first trailer, which will be released on TikTok at noon ET on March 17. The first images from the film show [Paul Atreides](https://www.polygon.com/24072878/dune-2-timothee-chalamet-paul-hero-denis-villeneuve-interview/) (Timothée Chalamet) and Chani (Zendaya) looking pretty rough. Alia, the baby [Lady Jessica](https://www.polygon.com/22738416/dune-voice-witch-nun-box-jessica-powers-bene-gesserit/) (Rebecca Ferguson) was pregnant with in _[Dune 2](https://www.polygon.com/reviews/24078649/dune-2-review-release-date-cast-zendaya-denis-villeneuve-part-two/)_, is all grown up and played by Anya Taylor-Joy. But the biggest surprise is the return of [Jason Momoa](https://www.polygon.com/helldivers-movie-jason-momoa-justin-lin/) as Hayt. Momoa played the Atreides swordmaster Duncan Idaho in _Dune_, but his character died heroically helping Paul and Jessica escape. So who is Hayt?

The answer is in [_Dune Messiah_](https://www.polygon.com/24085138/dune-2-messiah-sequel-underrated-classic-good-book/), Frank Herbert’s 1969 sequel to _Dune_, which will serve as the inspiration for _Dune 3_. The book is set 12 years after _Dune_ and follows Paul’s attempts to guide the empire and humanity in the bloody aftermath of the war he unleashed as leader of the Fremen. A group of conspirators trying to overthrow Paul gives him a gift they hope will actually weaken him. Hayt is a ghola, a clone of Paul’s former mentor Duncan Idaho. The artificial being is a violation of Fremen taboos, causing Paul to lose some of his support. Hayt is also implanted with commands from his scheming creators.

Villeneuve [just wants to make three Dune movies](https://www.vanityfair.com/hollywood/story/denis-villeneuve-dune-movies-interview-awards-insider), but if the series continued, we’d be seeing a lot more of Momoa. Duncan is the only character to appear in all six _Dune_ novels. He’s cloned and killed over and over again for thousands of years. He marries Alia, tries to kill Paul’s sandworm-human hybrid son [Leto II](https://www.polygon.com/23716984/god-emperor-of-dune-movie-adaptation-denis-villeneuve/), and is turned into a weapon by the Bene Gesserit, who create a version of Duncan who is [irresistible to women](https://www.polygon.com/24090250/bene-gesserit-sex-powers-frank-herbert-books-vs-movies/). Paul is the most important character in the Dune films, but Momoa is playing the real star of Herbert’s books.

Dune 3 releases in theaters on Dec. 18.
