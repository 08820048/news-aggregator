[_Honkai: Star Rail_](https://www.polygon.com/game/honkai-star-rail/40897) just closed out its version 4.2 livestream, teasing what’s to come in the next update, which launches the evening of April 21. Alongside new characters and events, Hoyoverse uses the stream to distribute codes that reward free Stellar Jade and other upgrade materials.

This update continues the Phantasmoon Games on Planarcadia, pulling Silver Wolf and Evanescia more into the story. Alongside this, those two characters will also become playable this patch. Silver Wolf gets another version (Silver Wolf Lv. 999), which is an Imaginary Elation unit, and Evanescia makes her playable debut as a Physical Elation unit.

Below we list the **_Honkai: Star Rail_ version 4.2 livestream codes**.

* * *

## Honkai: Star Rail 4.2 livestream codes

These codes are only active until the day ends, so you’ll want to make sure to use them quickly.

The _Honkai: Star Rail_ reward codes from the version 4.2 livestream are:

-   [HERESTHECODE](https://hsr.hoyoverse.com/gift?code=HERESTHECODE)
-   [SHAREANDSAVEIT](https://hsr.hoyoverse.com/gift?code=SHAREANDSAVEIT)
-   [HAPPY3RDANNIV](https://hsr.hoyoverse.com/gift?code=HAPPY3RDANNIV)

These codes will expire on **April 11 at 11:59 a.m. EDT / 8:59 a.m. PDT**, so use them quickly or you’ll miss out on the free loot.

* * *

## How to redeem codes in Honkai: Star Rail

You can redeem the codes in game by opening the menu and tapping on the ellipses in the top right corner. A “redemption code” option will come up that you can select.

    ![March 7th looks at her phone in Honkai: Star Rail with the menu open](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/chorus/uploads/chorus_asset/file/24612314/hsr_code_redeem.jpg?q=70&fit=crop&w=825&dpr=1)Image: Hoyoverse via Polygon

You can also redeem them online through the [_Honkai: Star Rail_ code redeem website](https://hsr.hoyoverse.com/gift). You’ll need to select your correct server and login through the website in order to get rewards. You will also need to have a working UID for _Honkai: Star Rail_. If you’ve never logged into _Honkai: Star Rail_ in any form using your Hoyoverse account, it will not work.
