Amazon’s [Big Spring Sale](https://www.ign.com/articles/amazon-spring-sale-2026-best-deals) is in full swing, and that means tons of Amazon devices have received some hefty discounts, including the Kindle. If you’ve been waiting to grab one of these [top e-ink tablets](https://www.ign.com/articles/best-e-ink-tablets), now’s the time, as chances are good you won’t see deals like these again until [Prime Day](https://www.ign.com/events/amazon-prime-day). The last time prices dropped this low was during Black Friday.

## Kindle Deals: New Discounts on Almost Every Version

Nearly every Kindle model is discounted, from the Paperwhite, our top pick for the [best reading tablet](https://www.ign.com/articles/best-reading-tablets), to the Scribe, a convenient digital notebook. Even kids' devices are on sale, plus you can save on a bunch of Kindle bundles.

The [best discount is on the Kindle Colorsoft](https://www.ign.com/articles/amazon-kindle-colorsoft-drops-to-its-all-time-low-price), Amazon's full color e-reader. It’s back to its lowest price ever, $169.99, down $80. To sweeten the deal a little further, almost all of these Kindles come with 3 months of [Kindle Unlimited](https://www.ign.com/articles/kindle-unlimited-plans-and-prices) free for new subscribers, bringing access to millions of books and thousands of audiobooks.

### Amazon Kindle Scribe (16GB)

While you can read on the Scribe like a typical Kindle, you’ll want this device for jotting notes. It’s a 10.2-inch digital notebook that brings that pen-and-paper feel. It’s great for jumping between reading and note-taking, and can be especially beneficial to students. There’s even AI summarization built in.

Only the older model of this e-ink tablet is on sale, but the low cost outweighs the new features on the latest Scribe. In fact, it’s down to its lowest price ever on Amazon, offering $150 in savings.
