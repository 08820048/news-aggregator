It's not cheap, but much more palatable than its $79.99 suggested retail price.

Woot is owned by Amazon — which is [having its own big sale right now](https://www.polygon.com/best-gaming-deals-amazon-big-spring-sale/) — and Amazon Prime members can get free shipping on any orders through Woot. These discounts are available for the next week or while supplies last. Seeing as plenty are already sold out, you'll want to snag your Switch games soon if you're looking to save.
