Black Desert is free to play ahead of Crimson Desert's launch

## Before Crimson Desert, the team made a sweeping RPG — and it's free on PS5 and Xbox this weekend

   ![A woman holding a big sword in a screenshot from Black Desert](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2025/12/black-desert-steam-screen-1.jpg?&fit=crop&w=1600&h=900)Image: Pearl Abyss

The [_Crimson Desert_ hype train](https://www.polygon.com/what-is-crimson-desert-rpg-open-world-ps5-xbox-release-date-trailer-overview/) is in full force ahead of its launch on March 19. Before the RPG arrives, though, you can check out developer Pearl Abyss' previous game for free. For a limited time, PlayStation 5 and Xbox Series X owners can play _Black Desert_ free of charge.

_Black Desert_ is an expansive MMO with a variety of gameplay mechanics. You can relax with farming, fishing, cooking, horse training, and more professions. For something a little more fast-paced, _Black Desert_ offers hack-and-slash combat as well as large-scale, siege-based PvP wars.

Despite the similarities in titles, _Black Desert_ and _Crimson Desert_ aren't wholly connected — though that wasn't always the case. _Crimson Desert_ [began development as a prequel to the _Black Desert_ MMO](https://www.pcgamer.com/crimson-desert-release-date-trailer-online-gameplay/) before developer Pearl Abyss shifted gears and worked _Crimson Desert_ into its own IP and into a single-player game. By all accounts, _Crimson Desert_ is an expansive game, with prospective players trying to find things they [_won't_ be able to do in the game](https://www.polygon.com/crimson-desert-diving-underwater-exploration/) ahead of its release. What it will have is the [ability to speed through cutscenes](https://www.polygon.com/crimson-desert-cutscene-speed-2x-skip/), because our attention spans are borked.

If you want to smooth over the wait for _Crimson Desert_, you can try out _Black Desert_ for free this weekend. The PS5 version's trial will be playable through March 8, and PlayStation Plus will not be required to play it. Xbox players will have a little longer — it will be free to play until March 9.
