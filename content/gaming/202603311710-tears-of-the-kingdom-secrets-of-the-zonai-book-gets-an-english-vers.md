## A massive Tears of the Kingdom book is getting an English translation this fall

   ![Princess Zelda holds the Master Sword in a still from The Legend of Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/chorus/uploads/chorus_asset/file/24582904/zelda_master_sword_totk.jpg?&fit=crop&w=1600&h=900)Image: Nintendo

We may not have a new [Zelda](https://www.polygon.com/game/the-legend-of-zelda/10941/) game for Nintendo Switch 2 yet, but fans of _[The Legend of Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom](https://www.polygon.com/legend-zelda-tears-kingdom/)_ will be eating good come fall. Dark Horse, the publisher behind several stellar Zelda reference books like 2013's _[Hyrule Historia](https://amzn.to/4sL8gVZ),_ will expand that collection on Oct. 20 with _The Legend of Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom - Secrets of the Zonai._

According to [Dark Horse](https://www.darkhorsedirect.com/products/the-legend-of-zelda-tears-of-the-kingdom-secrets-of-the-zonai-hc?variant=53093335204154), this 472-page hardcover book will include:

-   50 pages of beautiful illustrations, character art, and promotional images
-   Nearly 300 pages of behind-the-scenes sketches, notes, rough designs, concept art, and recollections that provide an exclusive look at the development and creation of the game
-   Over 80 pages of Hyrule’s history that include insights into the mysterious past of the Zonai all the way up to the events of the game
-   Interviews with producer Eiji Aonuma, director Hidemaro Fujibayashi, and art director Satoru Takizawa

    ![Secrets of the Zonai](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/secrets-of-the-zonai.jpeg?q=70&fit=crop&w=825&dpr=1)Image: Dark Horse / Nintendo

_Secrets of the Zonai_ will be available in two editions. The Standard Edition hardcover will have an MSRP of $59.99 and include all the juicy, behind-the-scenes info detailed above. The deluxe Hero's Edition will be priced at $119.99 and includes the book (with an exclusive cover), a cloth map of the Depths, an art print of Link and the Sages, and a replica of Zelda's Secret Stone.

Both editions of _Secrets of the Zonai_ are available to pre-order now at [Dark Horse](https://www.darkhorsedirect.com/products/the-legend-of-zelda-tears-of-the-kingdom-secrets-of-the-zonai-hc?variant=53093335236922) ahead of their October release.
