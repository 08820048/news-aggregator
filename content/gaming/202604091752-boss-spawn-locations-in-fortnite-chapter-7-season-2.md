Where to find Dark Voyager, Ice King, and Squibbly

   ![The Ice King, a man in frosty armor, preparing to shoot ice from his gloves in Fortnite.](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/04/ice-king_fn.jpg?&fit=crop&w=1600&h=900)Image: Epic Games via Polygon

**Bosses** are stronger NPC opponents that give you powerful buffs in [_Fortnite_](https://www.polygon.com/game/fortnite/2417/) Chapter 7 Season 2. In past chapters and seasons, bosses were known to spawn in a singular location and drop a token that would allow you to transform into them, but now, each boss has multiple spawn points and no longer drop a token.

Luckily, their spawns are a little more catered to their character in Chapter 7 Season 2. For example, one of the Ice King's spawn locations is at the Frigid Fortress, which is pretty on-brand for a guy in frozen armor. Once you defeat a boss, they'll drop a powerful mythic weapon for you to use.

Below, we've listed **where to find the bosses in _Fortnite_ Chapter 7 Season 2**, along with what mythic weapon they drop.

## Boss locations in Fortnite Chapter 7 Season 2

    ![A map of the Golden Coast in Fortnite Chapter 7 Season 2 with the locations of the three bosses marked with pins.](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/04/c7s2-boss-locations-map_fn.jpg?q=70&fit=crop&w=750&dpr=1)Graphic: Johnny Yu/Polygon | Source images: Epic Games via Polygon

In _Fortnite_ Chapter 7 Season 2, all three bosses will spawn at the start of the game, and their spawn points will vary between every match. In the map above, we've marked where you can find every boss on the Golden Coast, and, in the table below, we've listed **where to find the Dark Voyager, Dread Punisher Squibbly, and the Exalted Ice King** along with what weapons they'll drop upon defeat.

Spawn locations

Drops

-   Latte Landing
-   Squibbly Shores

Squibbly's Combat Assault Rifle or a legendary Combat Assault Rifle

Exalted Ice King

-   Frigid Fortress
-   Dark Dominion

-   Fore Fields

Ice King's Gauntlets

If you're trying to defeat bosses for the Week 4 quest, we recommend dropping near Dark Dominion as it's home to two boss locations and another boss location can be found close to the east.
