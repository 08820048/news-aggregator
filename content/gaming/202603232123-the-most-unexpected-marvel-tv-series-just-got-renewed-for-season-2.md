It's only the third MCU series to get a second season

## Marvel's Wonder Man renewed for season 2, to everyone's utter shock

   ![ Yahya Abdul-Mateen II as Simon Williams in Wonder Man takes a call on his cell phone with his eyes and mouth wide open in shock](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/wondermanwaaaaaaht.jpeg?&fit=crop&w=1600&h=900)Image: Marvel Studios/Disney

The best thing to happen to Marvel TV in years is going to happen again. [Marvel Studios announced on Monday](https://x.com/MarvelStudios/status/2036172114764468525) that [its 2026 series](https://www.polygon.com/wonder-man-review-mcu-best-show-marvel-spotlight/) _[Wonder Man](https://www.polygon.com/wonder-man-review-mcu-best-show-marvel-spotlight/)_ is getting a second season. That puts the show in a rarefied category of Marvel Cinematic Universe programs. Of the 17 MCU tie-in shows released since _WandaVision_ kicked off the Disney Plus era of MCU TV in 2021, only _Loki_, _What If…?_, and _Daredevil: Born Again_ have been renewed for follow-up seasons.

Series creators Destin Daniel Cretton (director of the upcoming _Spider-Man: Brand New Day_) and Andrew Guest (_Community_) are returning for the second season, along with series stars Yahya Abdul-Mateen II (_Aquaman_) as [struggling superpowered actor Simon Williams](https://www.polygon.com/wonder-man-marvel-superhero-comic-book-origins-explained/) and Ben Kingsley as disgraced actor [Trevor Slattery, aka The Mandarin](https://www.polygon.com/wonder-man-trevor-slattery-ben-kingsley-mcu/), a role Kingsley has played across several projects, starting with _Iron Man 3_.

Even though _Wonder Man_'s first season wrapped on an open-ended note, this is still surprising news for several reasons. First, most MCU shows don't get follow-up seasons because their stories continue via other projects, for instance with _WandaVision_'s sole season setting up _Doctor Strange in the Multiverse of Madness_, _Agatha All Along_, and _VisionQuest._ Second, _Wonder Man_ was a somewhat oddball project for Marvel Studios, a story only lightly tied to any other Marvel continuity (one of the goals of the "less homework, more personal stories" [Marvel Spotlight banner](https://www.polygon.com/23948889/marvel-spotlight-mcu-echo-standalone-too-many-movies-man/)), and focusing more on the mundane life of actors trying to get work in Hollywood, and forming a friendship in the process, than on fighting villains or saving lives.

    ![Trevor (Ben Kingsley) and Simon (Yahya Abdul-Mateen II) get fitted for costumes in Wonder Man](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/01/cbk-107-06639_r-2.jpg?q=49&fit=crop&w=825&dpr=2)Image: Suzanne Tenner/Marvel

And third, _Wonder Man_ is both a satisfyingly fresh take on the Marvel setting, and a really great show, which suggests to cynics that it wasn't likely to find an audience. [The Hollywood Reporter](https://www.hollywoodreporter.com/movies/movie-news/wonder-man-season-2-yahya-abdul-mateen-ii-ben-kingsley-1236544737/) says the show "racked up 618 million minutes of watch time, according to Nielsen, and landed in the metric tracking service’s top 10 original streaming series." Oddly, in February, those same numbers were being called ["weak" and a "viewership disappointment"](https://thedirect.com/article/wonder-man-marvel-viewership-shows) as well as ["a new low for Marvel on Disney+."](https://cosmicbook.news/marvel-wonder-man-absolutely-tanks-lowest-ratings-rejected-by-fans)

Regardless of how you spin or interpret the viewership numbers, Marvel Studios apparently had confidence in Cretton and Guest's vision for a continuing story, given the renewal. There were always signs that [in spite of ongoing Marvel fatigue](https://www.polygon.com/24016931/superhero-fatigue-movie-flops-2023-mcu-dceu/), the studio felt people would enjoy the show if they just tried it. _Wonder Man_ is a Disney Plus exclusive, but the streamer posted the series premiere free on YouTube in hopes of luring viewers in.

The timeline for the second season of _Wonder Man_ isn't clear yet, and given Marvel Studios' usual production timelines, it likely wouldn't appear until 2028 at the earliest. Expect to see Cretton asked at least a few questions about the show in the near future when he's on the press tour behind _Spider-Man: Brand New Day_, which opens on July 31, 2026.
