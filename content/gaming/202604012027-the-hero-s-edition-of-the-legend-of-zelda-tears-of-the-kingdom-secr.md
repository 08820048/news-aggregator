### The Legend of Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom - Secrets of the Zonai (Hero's Edition)

The Legend of Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom - Secrets of the Zonai comes with an impressive collection of concept art, storyboards, anecdotes from the development team, exclusive interviews with series veterans like producer Eiji Aonuma and director Hidemaro Fujibayashi, and so, _so_ much more. The book itself is 472 pages and measures at 12in by 9in., so if you have the other Dark Horse encyclopedias it'll fit nicely next to them -- just make sure you have enough space on your shelf.

With this Hero's Edition, you'll get all the content included in the base book ([which will run you $59.99](https://zdcs.link/QOjEX7?object_uuid=9b563c66-ad6f-4245-925e-c169ad99e886&t=article)), but you'll get a handful of exclusive goodies as well. You'll get an exclusive alternate cover art for the book itself, a beautiful cloth map of the Depths, an art print of Link and the Sages, and a one-to-one replica of the Secret Stone of Time you encounter in the game.
