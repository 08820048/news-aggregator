Warren Spector's Thick as Thieves is 'switching it up'

## Deus Ex creator's new stealth game ditches PvPvE for solo and co-op play

   ![A first-person view of Thick As Thieves, with a thief approaching the Mac Neish Jeweller building](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/04/thick-as-thieves.jpg?&fit=crop&w=1600&h=900)Image: OtherSide Entertainment

When developer OtherSide Entertainment [revealed the next game from Warren Spector](https://www.polygon.com/game-awards-tga/495677/game-awards-2024-biggest-announcements-trailers/), best known for his work on [Deus Ex](https://www.polygon.com/game/deus-ex/6309/) and Ultima Underworld, the game was pitched as a new PvPvE stealth-action multiplayer game. But that was back in 2024, and the developer has since made a substantial change to that project, _Thick as Thieves_. The studio has reworked its stealth game to focus on solo and cooperative play.

"When we first revealed _Thick as Thieves_ we intended to focus on PvPvE gameplay," [the studio said in an update posted to Steam](https://store.steampowered.com/news/app/3341000/view/519742851965256606). "However, as development progressed and the world of Kilcairn has come to life, we found that we were having more fun with solo and co-op play. So, we’ve taken a decision we feel is right for the game: _Thick as Thieves_ is now focused on two-player co-op and single player."

[OtherSide Entertainment](https://www.polygon.com/2016/2/17/11034836/warren-spector-otherside-entertainment/) said the change in approach will let the studio focus on "what makes _Thick as Thieves_ truly special – dynamic stealth gameplay."

The developer added, "We’re already seeing how this has strengthened the moment-to-moment action, whether you’re playing solo or with a partner in co-op. We’ve had some fantastic moments while testing the game. Now we’re putting all our energy into making things even better as we build up to launch."

OtherSide plans to show _Thick as Thieves_ at next week's [Triple-i Initiative](https://www.polygon.com/triple-i-iniative-2026-announcement-interview/), the gaming showcase that will also feature titles like _Castlevania: Belmont’s Curse_, _Windrose_, and _Dead as Disco_. The developer describes its new game as "A PvPvE multiplayer stealth game set in a 1910s metropolis filled with magic and intrigue. Hide, sneak, and steal your way through the ranks as you match your cunning against rival thieves in an immersive living world of stories and shadows."

The change to _Thick as Thieves_ will help it stand out from what is becoming an increasingly crowded PvPvE market. That space is currently dominated by [extraction shooter games](https://www.polygon.com/arc-raiders-2025-extraction-shooters-floodgates/) like _Arc Raiders_, _Marathon_, and _Escape from Tarkov_, and will face further competition from upcoming PvPvE titles like Deep Worlds' _Beautiful Light_, Krafton's _PUBG: Black Budget_, and FromSoftware's _The Duskbloods_.

_Thick as Thieves_ does not have an announced release date, but is expected to launch in 2026 for PlayStation 5, Windows PC, and Xbox Series X.
