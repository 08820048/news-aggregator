[_Pokémon Pokopia_](https://www.polygon.com/pokemon-pokopia/) is a dense game, full of things to do. In fact, there's so much to do, that you will no doubt get sidetracked. You'll set off to make some lumber, but get distracted by a shaking [habitat](https://polygon.com/pokemon-pokopia-habitat-dex-list-requirements-unlock/) nearby, focus on helping that new Pokémon... and you'll often forget what you were doing in the first place. However, that's not a bad thing — it just means there's lots of good fun to be had on this semi-deserted island.

With things [changing every day](https://polygon.com/pokemon-pokopia-daily-reset-time-tomorrow-new-day/), there's always a good reason to boot up the game.

## Build your dream island

Whether you want to build an industrial city or something more in-tune with nature, _Pokopia_ has options for you. You'll need to collect materials, like [lumber](https://www.polygon.com/pokemon-pokopia-lumber-how-to-make-small-logs-chop/), brick, and paper, in order to achieve your goals. If your friends have neat items you want to steal, you can use the nifty [3D printer](https://polygon.com/pokemon-pokopia-copy-items-3d-printer/) to copy the items, too.

## Help the Pokémon around you

It's not just you (the Ditto) who is confused about what's going on. There are hundreds of different Pokémon who are also looking for answers, and you'll need to help them out! Whether you're trying to [make it rain in the Withered Wasteland](https://polygon.com/pokemon-pokopia-yawn-up-a-storm-walkthrough-make-it-humid-100-point/) or [throw a party at Rocky Ridges](https://polygon.com/pokemon-pokopia-guide-time-to-party-walkthrough-raise-the-mood/), the other Pokémon need _you_ and your special abilities to get stuff done.
