## Punisher TV special release date raises more questions than it answers

The MCU's Punisher TV special is coming soon, and its release date is raising questions as to what the future holds for Jon Bernthal's Frank Castle. Titled [_The Punisher: One Last Kill_](https://x.com/ThePunisher/status/2036432235226652717), the "Marvel Television Special Presentation" will release on May 12 on Disney Plus — exactly one week after the season 2 finale of [_Daredevil: Born Again_](https://www.polygon.com/daredevil-born-again-season-2-trailer-jessica-jones/) airs.

Bernthal returned as the [Punisher](https://www.polygon.com/10-years-ago-mcu-punisher/) last year for a guest-starring role in the first season of _Daredevil: Born Again_. The final episode found him captured by Mayor Wilson Fisk's Anti-Vigilante Task Force, and a mid-credits scene teased his escape. It's unclear if he'll return for _Born Again_'s second season ([different rumors](https://www.gamesradar.com/entertainment/marvel-tv-shows/the-punisher-wont-return-for-daredevil-born-again-season-2-because-of-his-upcoming-disney-plus-tv-special/) say [different things](https://www.gamesradar.com/entertainment/marvel-tv-shows/a-daredevil-born-again-cast-member-may-have-just-spoiled-the-return-of-an-mcu-hero/)), but the timing of the Punisher special does make one wonder.

As _Daredevil: Born Again_ finds Charlie Cox's Daredevil amassing a group to battle Fisk, including a returning [Krysten Ritter as Jessica Jones](https://www.polygon.com/tv/599010/daredevil-born-again-season-2-jessica-jones-marvel/), thematically it makes sense for the Punisher to join the fray at some point. The special could serve as an epilogue of sorts for season 2 based on the timing, but that's anyone's guess. Plot details have not been revealed yet.

The subtitle _One Last Kill_ is interesting here as well. It seems to imply this is Castle's last rodeo, or even that he'll meet an untimely end, yet we know both those things aren't true. Bernthal is confirmed to star in July's [_Spider-Man: Brand New Day_](https://www.polygon.com/spider-man-brand-new-day-trailer-marvel-sony/). We're not sure how large a role he'll have in the film, but he does play a prominent role in the first trailer.

_Brand New Day_ is seemingly [taking cues from the '90s animated Spider-Man show](https://www.polygon.com/spider-man-brand-new-day-animated-series-arc/), specifically an arc where Peter turns into the monstrous Man-Spider. The Punisher may end up hunting him in the film, just like he did in the animated series.

For now, we have a lot of questions about what Marvel's plans are for the Punisher. We'll have to wait until _The_ _Punisher: One Last Kill_ streams on Disney Plus on May 12 for some answers.
