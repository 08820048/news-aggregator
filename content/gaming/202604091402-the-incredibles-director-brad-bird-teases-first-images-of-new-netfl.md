## The Incredibles and Iron Giant director teases a first look at his long-awaited return to big-budget sci-fi

   ![A blue holographic woman in a retro futuristic city from Ray Gunn](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/04/ray-gunn-brad-bird-3.jpg?&fit=crop&w=1600&h=900)Image: Skydance Animation/Netflix

Skydance Animation and Netflix released a first look at the upcoming _Ray Gunn_ today, Brad Bird's next sci-fi outing. The director may not have the longest filmography, but he's directed more than his fair share of animated hits: [_The Iron Giant_](https://www.polygon.com/animation-cartoons/2020/6/10/21285346/iron-giant-streaming-brad-bird-1990s-2000s-animation-fandom/), [_The Incredibles_](https://www.polygon.com/animation-cartoons/616644/incredibles-best-fantastic-four-movie/), and [_Ratatouille_](https://www.polygon.com/movies/22239548/best-pixar-movies-ranked/), and the upcoming _Ray Gunn_ has the chance to be his next. It's targeting a 2026 release, and will be Bird's first film since _The Incredible 2_ was released in 2018.

It's been a long time coming for _Ray Gunn_ as [Bird first wrote the script for the film in 1996](https://www.thewrap.com/ray-gunn-brad-bird-skydance-animation/) (the [same year he signed up for _The Iron Giant_](https://web.archive.org/web/20060321055611/http://movies.warnerbros.com/irongiant/cmp/production.html)). It takes place in Metropia, a retro futuristic city, and follows the private eye Raymond Gunn, voiced by Sam Rockwell. He investigates a case "involving aliens, murder, and a multimedia star named Venus Nova," according to the film's official description.

"_Ray Gunn_ has been in my mind for over 30 years," Bird said in a news release. "The film is a blend of sci-fi and classic detective movies from the '40s... it's _Maltese Falcon_ meets Buck Rogers. I've been a fan of both of those sort of genres, and blending them together seemed fun, and a chance to play with a lot of very cinematic elements, and extreme characters."

While a trailer hasn't been shared yet, Skydance and Netflix revealed a few images from the film, which you can check out below.

Work on _Ray Gunn_ began in 2022 and finds Bird reuniting with his old Pixar colleague and _Ray Gunn_ producer John Lasseter, [who left Pixar and Disney in 2018 after allegations of sexual misconduct](https://variety.com/2018/film/news/disney-john-lasseter-harassment-bob-iger-1202734060/). Lisa Beroud, David Ellison, and Dana Goldberg are also producing, with Scarlet Johansson (Venus Nova) and Tom Waits (Eyera) rounding out the voice cast so far.

"Having the opportunity to collaborate with Brad Bird is a career milestone for me," Johansson said. "This project is so uniquely special because it is a total realization of where Brad is currently on his artistic journey. I can’t wait for audiences to see this extraordinary animation that looks like nothing else out there."

_Ray Gunn_ will be released in 2026 on Netflix.
