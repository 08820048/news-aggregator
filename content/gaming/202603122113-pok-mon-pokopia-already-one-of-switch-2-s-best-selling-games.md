[_Pokémon Pokopia_](https://www.polygon.com/pokemon-pokopia-review/) is only a week old, and it's already one of the Nintendo Switch 2's best-selling titles. On Thursday, Nintendo announced the [Pokémon](https://www.polygon.com/pokemon/) spinoff [sold over 2.2 million copies in its first four days](https://www.nintendo.co.jp/corporate/release/en/2026/260312.html) on sale, with 1 million of those in Japan alone.

Those 2.2 million sales are good enough to make it the [fourth-best-selling title on Switch 2](https://www.nintendo.co.jp/ir/en/finance/software/index.html) behind [_Pokémon Legends: Z-A – Nintendo Switch 2 Edition_](https://www.polygon.com/pokemon-legends-z-a-review/) (3.89 million), [_Donkey Kong Bananza_](https://www.polygon.com/review/616250/donkey-kong-bananza-nintendo-switch-2/) (4.25 million), and [_Mario Kart World_](https://www.polygon.com/review/605637/mario-kart-world-review-nintendo-switch-2/) (a whopping 14.03 million). [_Kirby Air Riders_](https://www.polygon.com/kirby-air-riders-review/) rounds out the top five with 1.76 million copies sold. There is a caveat to this in that the data for the other games in the top five captures sales until the end of 2025, so there is a slight chance _Kirby Air Riders_ sold more than _Pokopia_'s 2.2 million with its 2026 sales factored in. Even if that's the case, though, being in the top five is still a feather in _Pokopia_'s cap.

It's not like Nintendo Switch 2 owners aren't starved for first-party offerings, either. [Nearly every month since its launch, a new flagship title has been released for Nintendo Switch 2](https://www.polygon.com/analysis/615967/nintendo-switch-2-game-release-calander-2025-analysis/). Sometimes they're full games, like December's [_Metroid Prime 4_](https://www.polygon.com/metroid-prime-4-beyond-review/) and February's [_Mario Tennis Fever_](https://www.polygon.com/mario-tennis-fever-review/). Other months have gotten expansions for great Switch games, like August's _Kirby and the Forgotten Land_'s [_Star-Crossed World_](https://www.polygon.com/kirby-forgotten-land-star-crossed-world-review-switch-2/) upgrade. (And they've all made it onto [our list of best Switch 2 games](https://www.polygon.com/best-switch-2-games/).)

_Pokémon Pokopia_ has done well relative to other Pokémon spinoff games as well. As the [King of Pokémon himself Joe Merrick](https://www.polygon.com/pokemon-serebii-joe-merrick-interview/) pointed out on [X](https://x.com/JoeMerrick/status/2031899651624886670), _Pokopia_ is one of 13 Pokémon spinoffs to sell over two million copies. (And there's been [_a lot_ of spinoffs over the series' nine generations](https://www.polygon.com/pokemon-generations-ranked/) thus far.)

Physical copies of _Pokopia_ were briefly sold out for a time, and [Amazon temporarily raised the game's price to $80](https://www.polygon.com/pokemon-pokopia-amazon-price-increase-80/) in response. (It's back in stock at major retailers, and back to $69.99 at Amazon, at this time of writing.) _Pokopia_ has done so well that Nintendo's stock gained over 10% in value, [Bloomberg](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2026-03-11/nintendo-shares-rally-10-as-new-pokemon-game-lifts-sentiment) recently reported.

Players and [critics alike have been loving _Pokémon Pokopia_](https://www.polygon.com/pokemon-pokopia-review-round-up/) as it's also one of the [Switch 2's best-reviewed games](https://opencritic.com/browse/switch%202). It sits at an 89 on both [OpenCritic](https://opencritic.com/game/19667/pok-mon-pokopia) and [Metacritic](https://www.metacritic.com/game/pokemon-pokopia/).
