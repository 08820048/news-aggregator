## Netflix's spring anime slate includes Blue Lock, One Piece, and much more

[Spring anime season](https://www.polygon.com/best-spring-anime-2026-dorohedoro-one-piece-daemons-of-the-shadow-realm/) is here. Several winter titles have already reached their conclusion, including [the third season of _Jujutsu Kaisen_](https://www.polygon.com/jujutsu-kaisen-season-3-finale-recap-review/) and _Trigun Stargaze_, but there is a lot to look forward to. To celebrate this spirit of new beginnings, Netflix recently [announced](https://about.netflix.com/en/news/anime-japan-2026) its Spring 2026 lineup at this year’s AnimeJapan event. The slate includes Netflix Originals like _JoJo’s Bizarre Adventure: Steel Ball Run_ and _Dandelion_, along with titles that will arrive on Netflix after their original run on platforms like Crunchyroll.

Heavy hitters like _Jujutsu Kaisen_ and [_One Piece_](https://www.polygon.com/one-piece-anime-seasonal-26-episodes-end-of-an-era/) are already on Netflix, but the spring slate will allow global audiences to keep up with new seasons or arcs. For instance, season 2 of _Jujutsu Kaisen_ will finally be available from May 1 onward, while _One Piece_’s highly anticipated Elbaph Arc can be watched starting April 11 (as opposed to its [April 5 premiere](https://www.polygon.com/one-piece-anime-elbaph-arc-release-date/) on Crunchyroll). Here is the list of new and updated titles that are coming to the platform in the U.S. this spring:

-   _Dorohedoro_ (season 2) - April 1
-   _The Ramparts of Ice_ - April 2
-   _Spy x Family_ (season 3) - April 4
-   _One Piece_ Elbaph Arc - April 11
-   _Blue Lock_ (season 1) - April 25
-   _My Dress-Up Darling_ (season 1) - April 25
-   _Jujutsu Kaisen_ (season 2) - May 1
-   _Assassination Classroom_ (season 1) - May 1
-   _Shangri-La Frontier_ (season 1) - May 1
-   _Overload: The Sacred Kingdom_ - May 10
-   _Akane Banashi_ - May (TBD)

Apart from this wide range of stories, you can also expect highly anticipated entries like _[Witch Hat Atelier](https://www.polygon.com/witch-hat-atelier-episodes-1-2-review-impressions/)_ to grace the platform, adding much-needed variety to the saturated fantasy genre. Additionally, the Netflix Original _Dandelion_, which is based on _Gintama_ creator Hideaki Sorachi's one-shot manga, will premiere on April 16.

You can also enjoy watching acclaimed titles like [_Cosmic Princess Kaguya!_](https://www.polygon.com/cosmic-princess-kaguya-review-anime-netflix/)_,_ [_Love Through a Prism_](https://www.polygon.com/love-through-a-prism-netflix-anime-review/), and [_100 Meters_](https://www.polygon.com/100-meters-anime-movie-heated-rivalry/), which are already available to stream on Netflix. Although specifics vary, most of these titles will be available in several languages along with audio descriptions.
