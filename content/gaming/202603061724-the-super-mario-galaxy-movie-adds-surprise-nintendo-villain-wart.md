## The Super Mario Galaxy Movie brings back a criminally overlooked Nintendo villain

   ![super mario galaxy movie still with Mario, Bowser, and Luigi](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/super-mario-galaxy-movie.jpeg?&fit=crop&w=1600&h=900)Image: Nintendo, Illumination

[Nintendo](https://www.polygon.com/nintendo/) has a new Nintendo Direct showcase coming on Monday, March 9 — aka Mario Day Eve — that will reveal the final trailer for [_The Super Mario Galaxy Movie_](https://www.polygon.com/super-mario-galaxy-movie-trailer-nintendo-direct/). But Nintendo had a more important revelation on Friday, when the game maker revealed the full poster for its next Super Mario animated movie, and confirmation of a surprise, all-but-forgotten (by Nintendo, anyway) villain.

That's right: Wart is going to be in _The Super Mario Galaxy Movie_.

Wart is the main antagonist of _Super Mario Bros. 2_, a massive toad that belches deadly bubbles and is deathly allergic to fruits and vegetables. Originally known as Mamu in the Famicom game _Yume Kōjō: Doki Doki Panic_, upon which _Super Mario Bros. 2_ was based, Wart's subsequent appearances in Nintendo games have been few and far between. He has mostly appeared in cameo form in games like _The Legend of Zelda: Link's Awakening_, remixes of _Super Mario Bros. 2_, and as a Spirit in _Super Smash Bros. Ultimate_. Wart has also appeared in various Super Mario Bros. comics and manga over the years.

For fans of a certain age, Wart has a reputation as an under-used foil for the Mario Bros. His role as the big final boss of _Super Mario Bros. 2_ set an expectation that he'd return at some point, but Nintendo has consistently relied on Bowser to be the ultimate villain of the franchise.

    ![super mario galaxy movie poster](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/super-mario-galaxy-movie-poster.jpeg?q=70&fit=crop&w=750&dpr=1)Image: Nintendo, Illumination

With _The Super Mario Galaxy Movie_, Wart will finally get his due. And while his inclusion is something of a surprise, it's consistent with the sprawling ensemble cast we've seen so far, which will include appearances from other _Super Mario Bros. 2_ baddies like Birdo, Mouser, and Clawgrip.

What remains to be seen is whether [another rumored antagonist](https://www.polygon.com/super-mario-galaxy-movie-story-leak-villain-nintendo/), Wario, will also appear in _The Super Mario Galaxy Movie_, or whether there was some sort of Wart/Wario mixup at some point.

_The Super Mario Galaxy Movie_ stars Chris Pratt as Mario, Anya Taylor-Joy as Peach, Charlie Day as Luigi, Jack Black as Bowser, Keegan-Michael Key as Toad, and Kevin Michael Richardson as Kamek, reprising their roles from the first film. New to the cast are Brie Larson as Rosalina and Benny Safdie as Bowser, Jr. Hopefully we learn soon who's behind Wart.

_The Super Mario Galaxy Movie_ opens in movie theaters on April 1.
