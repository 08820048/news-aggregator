## Amazon Prime members can play over 20 games for free this month, including Control and Mortal Shell

   ![Jesse Faden Force pushing an enemy in a Control gameplay screenshot](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/control-gameplay-screenshot.jpg?&fit=crop&w=1600&h=900)Image: Remedy Entertainment

Amazon Prime members can start playing some great games for free this month. Additionally, a batch of 13 [PC games](https://www.polygon.com/pc/) are free to claim and own through various storefronts, like the Epic Games Store.

The most notable addition to the Luna games library, which includes over 50 PC games Amazon Prime members can stream, is Remedy's [_Control: Ultimate Edition_](https://www.polygon.com/2020/8/12/21364834/control-steam-ultimate-edition-free-next-gen-console-upgrade-expansions-awe-alan-wake/). The third-person shooter follows Jesse Faden as she investigates mysteries in and uncovers the secrets of the Federal Bureau of Control, an organization that focuses on paranormal oddities. In addition to her modifiable gun, Jesse can use Force-like telekinetic powers to take down enemies. [Polygon's review](https://www.polygon.com/reviews/2019/8/26/20829631/control-review-pc-ps4-xbox-one-ray-tracing/) called _Control_ "one of 2019’s best and weirdest games." If you haven't played _Control_ yet, there's no better time than now with [a sequel right around the corner](https://www.polygon.com/control-resonant-action-rpg-gameplay/).

-   _Wobbly Life_
-   _Overcooked!_
-   _House of Golf 2_
-   _ClayFighter_

Amazon also makes a variety of games free to download for subscribers. The downloadable games come via redeemable codes from various PC gaming storefronts, like the Epic Games Store and GOG, meaning you'll still own and have access to them even if your Amazon Prime subscription comes to an end. March's offerings are headlined by [_TinyTina’s Assault on Dragon’s Keep: A Wonderlands One-Shot_](https://www.polygon.com/22770978/borderlands-tiny-tinas-assault-on-dragon-keep-release-date-free-epic-games-store-dlc/), the precursor to [_Tiny Tiny's Wonderlands_, which was made free last month](https://www.polygon.com/amazon-prime-gaming-free-games-february-2026/) and is still available to claim.

Here are all the free games, where you can snag them, and when they're available:

-   _TinyTina’s Assault on Dragon’s Keep: A Wonderlands One-Shot_ (Epic Games Store) — available now
-   _Tattoo Tycoon_ (Epic Games Store) — available now
-   _Siege of Avalon_ (GOG) — available now
-   _Total War: Rome 2 - Emperor Edition_ (Epic Games Store) — March 12
-   _Turmoil_ (Epic Games Store) — March 12
-   _Veil of Darkness_ (GOG) — March 12
-   _Mahokenshi - The Samurai Deckbuilder_ (GOG) — March 12
-   _Sir Questionnaire_ (GOG) — March 19
-   _Rebel Galaxy_ (GOG) — March 19
-   _Total War: Three Kingdoms_ (Epic Games Store) — March 26
-   _Chimp Quest: Spirit Isle_ (Legacy Games) — March 26
-   _Phantasie Memorial Set_ (GOG) — March 26
-   _Deep Sky Derelicts_ (Amazon Games App) — March 26

Amazon Prime members can head to [Amazon Luna](https://luna.amazon.com/claims/home?g=s) to start claiming and playing the free games.​
