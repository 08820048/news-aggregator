### The Woot MSI Component Sale Includes GeForce RTX 50 Series Graphics Cards

Of all the Blackwell cards released thus far, the RTX 5070 Ti offers the best value for high-fps 4K gaming. It performs neck-and-neck with the RTX 4080 Super but with the added bonus of newer GDDR7 VRAM and [multi-frame generation](https://www.ign.com/articles/dlss-4-explained-everything-you-need-to-know-about-nvidias-latest-ai-upscaling-tech). If you plan on using this card for AI, the RTX 5070 Ti is a better value than the RTX 5080 since both have same amount of RAM.

Compared to AMD, the RTX 5070 Ti is roughly comparable in raw performance to the [AMD RX 9070 XT](https://www.ign.com/articles/amd-radeon-rx-9070-xt-review), but the 5070 Ti has better upscaling technology (especially with DLSS 4.5) and ray tracing performance.

[Hardware Unboxed](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sVEdBAP_BY8) recently tested both cards on the upcoming [Crimson Desert](https://www.ign.com/articles/crimson-desert-review) game, and the RTX 5070 Ti is the only one of the two cards that can achieve sustained 60fps in 4K cinematic mode.
