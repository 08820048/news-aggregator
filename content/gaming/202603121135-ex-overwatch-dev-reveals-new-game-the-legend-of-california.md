## Ex-Overwatch lead reveals new open world cowboy shooter game

   ![An image from The Legend of California. It shows a player stalking up to two buffalo, gun in hand. They are in a forest area.](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/the-legend-of-california-hed.jpg?&fit=crop&w=1600&h=900)Image: Kintsugiyama

Ever wanted to be a cowboy and stake your claim on the land? _The Legend of California_ is an indie multiplayer action-survival FPS that lets you do just that. Developed by [Kintsugiyama](https://www.kintsugiyama.com/) and published by [Dreamhaven](https://www.polygon.com/2020/9/23/21452257/blizzard-mike-morhaime-dreamhaven-secret-door-moonshot-games/) (the game company of former Blizzard CEO Mike Morhaime), _The Legend of California_ is headed by a name that [_Overwatch_](https://www.polygon.com/overwatch/) fans will recognize: Jeff Kaplan.

Kaplan was one of the developers of _World of Warcraft_ and the lead game director of _Overwatch_ before he [departed Blizzard](https://www.polygon.com/22394111/overwatch-jeff-kaplan-quits-blizzard-aaron-keller/) in 2021. Despite being away from the franchise for years, it's clear from [the comments](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7faxOQTwETk) on _The Legend of California_ teaser trailer that Kaplan's time at _Overwatch_ has earned him lifelong fans and support.

    ![An image showing a cowboy on a horse overlooking a forest, mountain-esque area. It's a screenshot from The Legend of California from ex-Overwatch developers.](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/the-legend-of-california.jpg?q=49&fit=crop&w=825&dpr=2)Image: Kintsugiyama

This Western-themed multiplayer is set on a mythical island called California during a gold rush era. Players can craft, gather, hunt, fight, and build across the island, thriving and creating bountiful communities. Depending on their playstyle, players can choose to do all of this alone or forge their destiny alongside friends.

The trailer shows players engaging in thrilling gunfights, as well as gathering resources in the mines to build their own homestead, making _The Legend of California_ feel like a fine mix between the _[Red Dead Redemption](https://www.polygon.com/red-dead-redemption/)_ and [_Mafia_](https://www.polygon.com/game/mafia-the-old-country/443251/) series.

"We formed our team around a single passion project: _The Legend of California_," Kintsugiyama shared on their [official website](http://www.kintsugiyama.com/). "Four years in the making and there's still a ways to go, but we're excited to start sharing the world we're building with players."

[_The Legend of California_](https://store.steampowered.com/app/2550530/The_Legend_of_California/) is coming to Early Access on PC in 2026.
