With the **release of** **_Solasta 2_** **early access**, the path to a new adventure presents itself. Developer Tactical Adventures' new game is a fantasy CRPG that brings many elements players who might miss a game like _Baldur's Gate 3_ will enjoy: rolling D20s to pass tests, great voice acting that brings the narrative to life, and a magical world to explore.

If you're looking for a fantastical world to explore and character creation menus to master, here's the **_Solasta 2_ early access release time** **in your time zone**, plus a little bit about what to expect from the game.

## Solasta 2 early access release time in your time zone

    ![An official image of SOlasta 2 release time map](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/solasts2_releasetime_map.jpg?q=49&fit=crop&w=825&dpr=2)Image: Tactical Adventures

_Solasta 2_ early access releases on **Thursday, March 12, at 12 p.m. EDT** for Windows PC, as stated by the developer in a recent [post on X](https://x.com/tct_adventures/status/2031007543305138450/photo/1). That means, depending on your location, you might noe be able to play until March 13, technically. Here's when that is in your time zone:

-   **9:00 a.m. PDT** for the west coast of North America
-   **12:00 p.m. EDT** for the east coast of North America
-   **1:00 p.m. BRT** for Brazil
-   **4:00 p.m. GMT** for the U.K.
-   **5:00 p.m. CET** for western Europe
-   **1:00 a.m. JST on Friday, March 13** for Japan
-   **3:00 a.m. AEDT on Friday, March 13** for the east coast of Australia

## What to expect from Solasta 2 early access

Releasing a game in early access allows the developer to continue fine-tuning the game alongside feedback from players. By playing _Solasta 2 in_ early access, you will experience this CRPG as it evolves. That said, it's important to keep in mind that the game is far from complete and the scope of what you can do in the game is very limited. Here's what _Solasta 2_ early access comes with and what Tactical Adventures is planning for future updates.

**_Solasta 2_ early access will include only Act 1 of the campaign**. According to the developer, it takes about 10 to 15 hours to complete this chapter of the adventure. But since there are hidden bosses and bonus locations, that playtime can easily turn into 20 or even more if you explore everything the map has to offer.

The campaign isn't the only thing to enjoy in the _Solasta 2_ early access release. The game comes with **six classes**, **13 subclasses**, and multiclassing. The **level cap is four**, so testing every method to make an overpowered build won't necessarily work at such an early stage of the game. You can, however, try out all **four ancestries**, _Solasta 2_'s equivalent to races.

Although there's a lot to play with in the _Solasta 2_ early access launch, Tactical Adventures is already planning future updates. The studio said that among the features being added to the game in the future are the **multiplayer**, **faction system**, and **crafting system**. You can also expect to see more ancestries and classes.
