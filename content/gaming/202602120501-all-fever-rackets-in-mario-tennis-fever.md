**Fever rackets** are the new special mechanic in [_Mario Tennis Fever_](https://www.polygon.com/mario-tennis-fever/), and each racket comes with its own unique ability. There are a bunch of Fever rackets, but you'll have a few to start out with. Some of the best rackets and power-ups are unfortunately locked behind challenges, so, if you want to dominate your opponents with Fever shots, you'll need to play a decent amount of matches.

Here's how to unlock all the Fever rackets in _Mario Tennis Fever_ and what each racket does.

* * *

## All Fever rackets in Mario Tennis Fever

There are 30 Fever rackets in _Mario Tennis Fever_ with six available from the get-go and the remaining 24 needing to be unlocked. Below, we've listed how to unlock every Fever racket along with their abilities.

Fire Flower Racket

Deals damage in a small area where the ball bounces, and creates fireballs each time the character hits the ball. Anyone hit by fireballs takes damage, catches on fire, and quickly runs around the area.

Clear Rank E in Adventure.

   ![Ice Flower Racket as seen in Mario Tennis Fever.](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/02/ice-flower-racket_mtf.jpg?q=70&fit=crop&w=597&dpr=1)

Ice Flower Racket

Deals damage in a small area where the ball bounces, and creates ice balls each time the character hits the ball. Anyone hit by ice balls takes damage and freezes for a short period of time.

Clear Rank D in Adventure.

   ![Star Racket as seen in Mario Tennis Fever.](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/02/star-racket_mtf.jpg?q=70&fit=crop&w=597&dpr=1)

Star Racket

Deals damage in a small area where the ball bounces, makes the player invincible, and slightly boosts speed.

Clear Rank C in Adventure.

   ![Tornado Racket as seen in Mario Tennis Fever.](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/02/tornado-racket_mtf.jpg?q=70&fit=crop&w=597&dpr=1)

Tornado Racket

Deals damage in a small area where the ball bounces, and creates a tornado each time the player hits the ball. Characters hit by a tornado take damage, spin around in a circle, and incoming balls are redirected.

Clear Rank B in Adventure.

   ![Pokey Racket as seen in Mario Tennis Fever.](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/02/pokey-racket_mtf.jpg?q=70&fit=crop&w=597&dpr=1)

Pokey Racket

Creates a Pokey where the ball bounces. When a Pokey is hit by a ball or a character, the Pokey splits up into pieces and land onto the court. Any character that runs into the pieces takes damage.

Clear Rank A in Adventure.

   ![Shadow Racket as seen in Mario Tennis Fever.](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/02/shadow-racket_mtf.jpg?q=70&fit=crop&w=597&dpr=1)

Shadow Racket

Deals damage in a small area where the ball bounces, and creates a shadow double that moves around the court and returns shots on its own.

Play 2 tennis matches.

   ![Fire Bar Racket as seen in Mario Tennis Fever.](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/02/fire-bar-racket_mtf.jpg?q=70&fit=crop&w=597&dpr=1)

Fire Bar Racket

Creates a Fire Bar where the ball bounces. Characters who run into the Fire Bar take damage, catch on fire, and quickly run around the area.

Play 4 tennis matches.

   ![Freezing Racket as seen in Mario Tennis Fever.](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/02/freezing-racket_mtf.jpg?q=70&fit=crop&w=597&dpr=1)

Freezing Racket

Scatters chunks of ice where the ball bounces. Characters who run into the ice take damage and freeze for a short period of time.

Play 6 tennis matches.

   ![Amp Racket as seen in Mario Tennis Fever.](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/02/amp-racket_mtf.jpg?q=70&fit=crop&w=597&dpr=1)

Amp Racket

Deals damage in a small area where the ball bounces, and turns balls hit by the player into Amps that connect with lightning. Characters who run into the lightning take damage and are temporarily stunned.

Play 8 tennis matches.

   ![Swerve Racket as seen in Mario Tennis Fever.](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/02/swerve-racket_mtf.jpg?q=70&fit=crop&w=597&dpr=1)

Swerve Racket

Deals damage in a small area where the ball bounces, adds a curve to the player's lob and topspin shots, and enhances the curve of the player's shots.

Play 12 tennis matches.

   ![Inky Racket as seen in Mario Tennis Fever.](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/02/inky-racket_mtf.jpg?q=70&fit=crop&w=597&dpr=1)

Creates ink splotches where the ball bounces and covers your screen in ink when an ink-covered ball is hit.

Play 14 tennis matches.

   ![Banana Racket as seen in Mario Tennis Fever.](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/02/banana-racket_mtf.jpg?q=70&fit=crop&w=597&dpr=1)

Banana Racket

Scatters banana peels where the ball bounces and after Fever Shot Counters. Characters who run into a banana slip and take damage.

Play 16 tennis matches.

   ![Volcano Racket as seen in Mario Tennis Fever.](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/02/volcano-racket_mtf.jpg?q=70&fit=crop&w=597&dpr=1)

Volcano Racket

Creates a crater where the ball bounces that shoots volcanic bombs. Hit players take damage, catch fire, and quickly run around the area.

Play 18 tennis matches.

   ![Ghost Racket as seen in Mario Tennis Fever.](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/02/ghost-racket_mtf.jpg?q=70&fit=crop&w=597&dpr=1)

Ghost Racket

Turns the player and the balls they hit invisible to other players.

Play 22 tennis matches.

   ![Golden Dash Racket as seen in Mario Tennis Fever.](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/02/golden-dash-racket_mtf.jpg?q=70&fit=crop&w=597&dpr=1)

Golden Dash Racket

Deals damage in a small area where the ball bounces and allows the player to leap toward shots with a burst of speed.

Play 24 tennis matches.

   ![Chargin Chuck Racket as seen in Mario Tennis Fever.](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/02/chargin-chuck-racket_mtf.jpg?q=70&fit=crop&w=597&dpr=1)

Chargin' Chuck Racket

Changes the shape of balls hit by the player into a football, giving them a chaotic bounce.

Play 26 tennis matches.

   ![Spiny Racket as seen in Mario Tennis Fever.](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/02/spiny-racket_mtf.jpg?q=70&fit=crop&w=597&dpr=1)

Spiny Racket

Deals damage in a small area and scatters Spinies where the ball bounces. Spinies move toward players, and push and damage any touched character.

   ![Magic Racket as seen in Mario Tennis Fever.](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/02/magic-racket_mtf.jpg?q=70&fit=crop&w=597&dpr=1)
