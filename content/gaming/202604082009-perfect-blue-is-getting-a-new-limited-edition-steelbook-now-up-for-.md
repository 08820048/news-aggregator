Perfect Blue is rightfully considered one of the best anime movies of all time, so it’s no surprise that it continues to get love from physical distributors. Following up on a [collector’s edition steelbook](https://zdcs.link/aoJ13l?object_uuid=4e17e8b5-c28a-4bb3-91ff-feb0bfc122c8&t=article) from December, Perfect Blue is getting a new _limited_ edition 4K steelbook that’s now up for preorder.

Set to release on June 16, the new steelbook is $25 less than the existing collector’s edition (currently down to [$41 on Amazon](https://zdcs.link/a07VGA?object_uuid=4e17e8b5-c28a-4bb3-91ff-feb0bfc122c8&t=article) or [$38.99 on Gruv](https://zdcs.link/aR7drO?object_uuid=4e17e8b5-c28a-4bb3-91ff-feb0bfc122c8&t=article)), but still includes all of the same digital special features.

## **Perfect Blue Limited Edition Steelbook Now Up for Preorder**

Satoshi Kon’s directorial debut has long been one of my favorite movies, period. I know I started this off with the ‘best anime movie’ caveat, but I truly do not think that moniker does this psychological thriller justice. This is a rare movie that gets under your skin and never really leaves, making it a worthy addition to any shelf.

### Perfect Blue Limited Edition Steelbook (4K Ultra HD)

This new limited edition steelbook kicks things off with a fresh cover design based on Satoshi Kon's original illustrations. A darker illustration of Mima is on the front, with a group shot of Mima as part of the girl group CHAM! on the back. Open up the steelbook and you’ll see images of the audience watching Mima’s performance from the opening scene.

### **Bonus Features**

-   Remastered Film on Blu-ray
-   Original Definition Feature Presentation
-   Complete 117 minutes of Lectures by Director Satoshi Kon subtitled in English for the First Time
-   “Angel of Your Heart” Recording Session and Full English Version
-   Cast and Crew Interviews
-   Theatrical Trailers

You can still find some listings for that one floating around from [third-party resellers](https://zdcs.link/QpB1vv?object_uuid=4e17e8b5-c28a-4bb3-91ff-feb0bfc122c8&t=article), though you'll also find hiked prices.

### Perfect Blue Collector's Edition Steelbook (4K UHD)

A collector’s edition 4K steelbook was also released this past December. While it quickly sold out on Amazon, it’s [still available through Gruv](https://zdcs.link/aoJ13l?object_uuid=4e17e8b5-c28a-4bb3-91ff-feb0bfc122c8&t=article). The main bonus of this version, which is more expensive at $69.99, is some extra physical goodies. In addition to the bonus features noted above, you’ll also get:

-   128-Page Booklet
-   10 Art Cards
-   Poster
