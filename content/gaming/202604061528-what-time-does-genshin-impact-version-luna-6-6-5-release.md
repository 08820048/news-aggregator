_[Genshin Impact](https://www.polygon.com/game/genshin-impact/40711/)_ patch 6.5 is coming soon, adding a new area to Mondstadt and introducing one new playable character to the gang. This update also has a special "Where Waves Meet the Reef" event, where you can unlock a free copy of Jahoda.

Below, we explain when _Genshin Impact_ maintenance starts and ends, alongside the release time of version "Luna 6" (aka 6.5). We’ve also added some details about what to expect from **_Genshin Impact_ patch 6.5**.

* * *

## What time does Genshin Impact go down for maintenance?

_Genshin Impact_ will go down for maintenance on **Tuesday, April 7 or Wednesday, April 8** (depending on your time zone) at the following times and will be unavailable for approximately five hours:

-   **3 p.m. PDT on Tuesday, April 7** for the west coast of North America
-   **6 p.m. EDT on Tuesday, April 7** for the east coast of North America
-   **7 p.m. BRT on Tuesday, April 7** for Brazil
-   **11 p.m. BST on Tuesday, April 7** for the U.K.
-   **12 a.m. CEST on Wednesday, April 8** for western Europe
-   **7 a.m. JST on Wednesday, April 8** for Japan
-   **8 a.m. AEST on Wednesday, April 8** for the east coast of Australia

Make sure to burn your resin before maintenance starts, if you wanted to.

* * *

## What time does Genshin Impact version 'Luna 6' (6.5) start?

_Genshin Impact_ should come back from maintenance with the 6.5 version at the following times:

-   **8 p.m. PDT on Tuesday, April 7** for the west coast of North America
-   **11 p.m. EDT on Tuesday, April 7** for the east coast of North America
-   **12 a.m. BRT on Wednesday, April 8** for Brazil
-   **4 a.m. BST on Wednesday, April 8** for the U.K.
-   **5 a.m. CEST on Wednesday, April 8** for western Europe
-   **12 p.m. JST on Wednesday, April 8** for Japan
-   **1 p.m. AEST on Wednesday, April 8** for the east coast of Australia

The servers may come up early or late, depending on how maintenance goes, but these are the approximate times _Genshin Impact_ should go live. Usually, Hoyoverse is pretty good by abiding by maintenance times. We don’t usually get extended maintenance, but it does end early sometimes!

* * *

## What’s new in Genshin Impact version 6.5?

This update adds new area Dornman Port to the north of Mondstadt, as well as a new playable character: Linnea, a five-star Geo bow user. There will also be a Nod-Krai event where you can recruit Jahoda, a four-star Anemo bow wielder.
