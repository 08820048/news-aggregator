IGN Entertainment and Dentsu Gaming will host a Women in Gaming UK event on Friday, April 17 in partnership with Meta.

Taking place at Dentsu London, the event features curated panels with industry leaders in addition to a pop-up headshot studio, Meta hardware demos, and networking opportunities. More details are to be announced.

Women in Gaming UK will run from 9:30am to 12:30pm. The guestlist is now open and you can [register your attendance here](https://www.ign.com/special/women-in-gaming-uk/).

IGN Entertainment [will also be hosting its annual IGN Live event in June](https://www.gamesindustry.biz/tickets-go-on-sale-for-consumer-event-ign-live-2026-returning-to-la-on-6-june), which will feature two days of livestream showcases, game reveals, and a showfloor featuring hundreds of playable games.
