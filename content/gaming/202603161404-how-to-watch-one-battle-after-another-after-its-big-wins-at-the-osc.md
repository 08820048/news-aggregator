While [this year’s Oscars ceremony](https://www.ign.com/articles/oscars-winners-2026-the-full-list-of-winners-from-the-98th-academy-awards) saw big wins for Sinners, Hamnet, and KPop Demon Hunters, no movie came away with as many awards as Paul Thomas Anderson’s One Battle After Another. The film picked up six awards total, including Best Picture, Best Director, the new Best Casting award, and Best Supporting Actor for Sean Penn, who happened to skip the ceremony.

We weren’t entirely surprised by the wins, having given the film a 10/10 and going so far as to call it a “masterpiece” in [our review](https://www.ign.com/articles/one-battle-after-another-review-leonardo-dicaprio-pta). If you haven’t checked out PTA’s latest yet, here’s where you can watch One Battle After Another after its big Oscars night.

## **How to Watch OBAA Online**

### Sign Up for HBO Max

Subscriptions start at $10.99/month

OBAA is streaming exclusively on HBO Max, where you'll also find fellow Best Picture nominee and Best Actor winner [Sinners](https://www.ign.com/articles/sinners-review-michael-b-jordan-ryan-coogler). Subscriptions [start at $10.99](https://www.ign.com/wikis/hbo-max/Plans_and_Pricing), and while the service does not offer a free trial, there are a number of ways to [save through bundles](https://www.ign.com/articles/the-best-hbo-max-deals). Because OBAA was a Warner Bros. release, there’s a possibility its streaming home ends up shifted by whatever [deal lands with Paramount](https://www.ign.com/articles/paramount-insists-hbo-will-operate-with-independence-after-warner-bros-buyout-though-streaming-service-will-be-combined-with-paramount).

### One Battle After Another

Washed-up revolutionary Bob exists in a state of stoned paranoia, surviving off-grid with his daughter, Willa. When his evil nemesis resurfaces and she goes missing, the former radical scrambles to find her while both battle the consequences of his past.

If you’re not already a subscriber, you can also rent or purchase One Battle After Another digitally through marketplaces like [Prime Video](https://www.ign.com/wikis/prime-video). Right now, Prime has actually dropped the digital purchasing price by 50%, from $19.99 to $9, but if you’re dropping real cash, I’d highly recommend checking out a physical Blu-ray first.

## **What Is One Battle After Another About?**

One Battle After Another is a very loose adaptation of Thomas Pynchon’s Vineland, which generally follows “DiCaprio’s Bob Ferguson, a former lefty revolutionary, who is raising his daughter Willa (Chase Infiniti). He’s been living in hiding ever since things went bad with his revolutionary group, the French 75, years earlier.” You can learn more about the film and how it adapts the original novel in Michael Calabro’s [full 10/10 review for IGN](https://www.ign.com/articles/one-battle-after-another-review-leonardo-dicaprio-pta).
