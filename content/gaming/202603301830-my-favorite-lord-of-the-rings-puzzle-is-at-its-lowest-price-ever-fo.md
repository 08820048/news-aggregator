If you're looking for a [good Lord of the Rings gift](https://www.ign.com/articles/the-best-lord-of-the-rings-gift-ideas) for yourself, it's one of my favorite things to suggest.

It's also currently at its lowest price ever for [Amazon's Big Spring Sale](https://www.ign.com/events/amazon-big-spring-sale). In fact, almost the entire lineup of Theory11 puzzles based on major franchises has dropped under $20 for a limited time. This is a great price for quality 1000-piece puzzles.

## Theory11 Lord of The Rings Jigsaw Puzzle

### Lord of The Rings Jigsaw Puzzle

What I personally love about this puzzle is the design. It feels like a movie poster that was converted into a beautiful mosaic and then split into puzzle pieces. You've got Gandalf, Gimli, Frodo, Sam, Aragorn, and the rest of the crew all wreathed in gold surrounding a menacing Balrog in the center. You've also got the iconic Lord of the Rings title font right in the middle to tie it all together. I finished this puzzle over a year ago and still haven't put it away because I just like looking at it.

Like most theory11 jigsaw puzzles, the Lord of the Rings version is 1,000 pieces. This isn't one of the [largest puzzles out there](https://www.ign.com/articles/largest-jigsaw-puzzles), but it's definitely a challenge if you're not looking to dedicate multiple days of your life to completing a puzzle. I have a [dedicated puzzle table](https://www.ign.com/articles/best-puzzle-tables) in my office that I use and usually take 2-3 weeks to actually finish putting together puzzles of this size in between other more pressing aspects of my life.
