## The best Stardew Valley merch to bring the farm to your home

   ![A big farm in Stardew Valley](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/chorus/uploads/chorus_asset/file/22215831/Stardew_Valley_Beginner_s_guide_Polygon___3x3.png?&fit=crop&w=1200&h=675)Image: ConcernedApe

Few games manage to be as charming and life-consuming as [_Stardew Valley_](https://www.polygon.com/game/stardew-valley/38933/), the open-ended farming sim where you grow seasonal crops, raise animals, and woo the townsfolk by repeatedly offering them gold bars and beer. Whether you're shopping for a gift for a _Stardew_\-loving friend or family member or simply looking to treat yourself, there are plenty of cute and thoughtful options out there. These are a few of our favorites.

## [A custom-made Stardew diorama](http://www.etsy.com/listing/4311371851/custom-stardew-valley-3d-cube)

    ![paperspective crafts via etsy](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/04/paperspective-crafts-via-etsy.jpeg?q=70&fit=crop&w=825&dpr=1)Image: Paperspectivecrafts via Etsy

It's not surprising that some of the most creative and memorable _Stardew_ merch out there comes from the fan community on Etsy. These customizable 3D cubes make a sentimental gift for a friend, family member, or sweetheart. Pricing starts just under $50, though these pieces are hand-made in Spain, so the shipping price to the US is an additional $30 or so. Seller Paperspectivecrafts makes a number of _Stardew_\-themed cubes, in addition to gaming franchises like The Legend of Zelda, Pokémon, and Undertale.

[Buy at Etsy (From $47.82)](https://www.etsy.com/listing/4311371851/custom-stardew-valley-3d-cube)

## [An analog Stardew Valley board game experience](https://www.amazon.com/Stardew-Valley-The-Board-Game/dp/B09MDPKV5K?th=1&linkCode=sl2&tag=polyus-20&linkId=14f01e997586540233964e537df73a19&language=en_US&ref_=as_li_ss_tl)

    ![stardew valley shop](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/04/stardew-valley-shop.jpeg?q=49&fit=crop&w=825&dpr=2)Image: Stardew Valley Shop

What could be better on a dreary spring day than cozying up for a little tabletop Stardew sesh? In this official cooperative boardgame adaptation, you'll need to farm, fish, and find various resources to fulfill Grandpa's Goals and restore the Community Center. The game is for 1-4 players and you should allow 45 minutes per player to finish. While it might look cute and approachable, this experience isn't for board game dabblers. According to [the description on the Stardew Valley Shop](https://shop.stardewvalley.net/products/pre-order-stardew-valley-the-board-game-3rd-printing), "It's really important to us that as a buyer you understand _Stardew Valley: The Board Game_ is not a casual quick game. While it is easy to play once you know the rules, it's meant to be challenging. We wanted an experience with depth and replayability."

There's a lot of legally distinct wordplay going on in the description for this building set from MyFunToy, but there's no denying that this 1,000-plus piece (alleged) rendering of the (supposed) Community Center is awfully cute. The haphazardly placed flowers and drooping eaves are a nice touch. The interior of the structure isn't totally accurate to the games, though there us a fish tank and workbench to play with, both of which are accessible from the fully open rear side. The "Star Community Farmhouse Toy" is currently sitting at 4.6/5 stars on Amazon with 44 reviews.

## [One for the romantics](http://www.fangamer.com/collections/stardew-valley/products/stardew-valley-mermaid-pendant)

    ![fangamer mermaid's pendant](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/04/fangamer-mermaid-s-pendant.jpeg?q=49&fit=crop&w=825&dpr=2)Image: Fangamer

Hoping to make a grand gesture? Look no further than Fangamer's adorable Mermaid's Pendant, which replicates the in-game item used to propose marriage to a lucky _Stardew Valley_ villager of your choosing. The pendant is made of iridescent purple-blue glass and measures 1.75 inches in length. It's on a silver-plated chain that includes two small charms, a teardrop-shaped crystal and a branch of coral. It also comes in a squeal-worthy blue seashell box that's perfect for keeping it safe and secure when it's not being worn.
