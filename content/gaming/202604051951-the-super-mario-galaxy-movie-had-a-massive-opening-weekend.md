The Super Mario Galaxy Movie's opening weekend is the highest-grossing of 2026 so far

_The Super Mario Galaxy_ _Movie_ arrived in theaters on April 1 and subsequently had a extraordinarily successful opening weekend. The animated movie is estimated to have grossed $190.1 million in the United States in its first five days.

Per [Deadline's reporting](https://deadline.com/2026/04/box-office-super-mario-galaxy-movie-1236771415/), that is not only the biggest opening weekend of any film in 2026, but of any movie year-to-date. Even if you narrow the opening weekend to its three-day gross, _The Super Mario Galaxy Movie_ still made an impressive $130.9 million domestically and $372.5 million internationally. The only Illumination movie to outperform its three-day opening was 2023's _The Super Mario Bros. Movie_.

Deadline also shared what the top ten films were at the domestic box office over this three-day weekend, which includes fellow newcomer _The Drama_ from A24. You can check out that list below:

1.  _The Super Mario Galaxy Movie_: $130.9 million
2.  _Project Hail Mary_: $30.6 million
3.  _The Drama:_ $14.4 million
4.  _Hoppers:_ $5.8 million
5.  _Reminders of Him_: $2.2 million
6.  _A Great Awakening_: $2.1 million
7.  _They Will Kill You_: $1.93 million
8.  _Dhurandhar: The Revenge_: $1.82 million
9.  _Ready or Not 2: Here I Come_: $1.8 million
10.  _Scream 7_: $915,000

_The Super Mario Galaxy Movie_ was clearly big hit with kids and families, although critics have not been nearly as kind to it. [Polygon's review](https://www.polygon.com/super-mario-galaxy-movie-review/) argued that the movie "relies on nostalgia and dazzling visuals to carry a paper-thin plot," and that it feels "even less focused" than its predecessor. It definitely does not have a cohesive plot, even if it is entertaining to finally see characters like [Rosalina](https://www.polygon.com/super-mario-galaxy-movie-trailer-nintendo-direct/), Bowser Jr., [Yoshi](https://www.polygon.com/super-mario-galaxy-movie-donald-glover-yoshi/), and [Fox McCloud](https://www.polygon.com/super-mario-galaxy-movie-surprise-cameo-star-fox-mccloud/) on screen. _The Super Mario Galaxy Movie's_ high-grossing opening weekend shows how critical reception and the box office performance of movies aren't always aligned.

_The Super Mario Galaxy Movie_ is going to be in theaters for the foreseeable future after this massive opening weekend. There has been no confirmation of when it will arrive on streaming platforms yet.
