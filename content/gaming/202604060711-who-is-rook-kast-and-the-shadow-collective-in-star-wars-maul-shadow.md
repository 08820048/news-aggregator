## Everything you need to know about Rook Cast and the Shadow Collective in Star Wars: Maul — Shadow Lord

   ![Death Watch](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/04/death-watch_624f6efa.jpg?&fit=crop&w=1200&h=675)Image: Lucasfilm

[Dave Filoni’s](https://www.polygon.com/lucasfilm-kathleen-kennedy-dave-filoni-star-wars/) new animated [Star Wars](https://www.polygon.com/star-wars/) series [_Maul – Shadow Lord_](https://www.polygon.com/star-wars-darth-talon-maul-shadow-lord/) follows the former Sith lord (Sam Witwer) as he seeks vengeance against his former crime syndicate allies and looks for a new apprentice. The Mandalorian Rook Kast (Vanessa Marshall) serves as Maul’s right hand, helping him set up operations on Janix, an urban world on the edge of the new Galactic Empire’s control. Filoni introduced Rook and Maul’s criminal ties in [_Star Wars: The Clone Wars_](https://www.polygon.com/2020/2/20/21144190/best-clone-wars-episodes-to-watch-before-season-7/)_,_ so here’s everything you need to know going into _Shadow Lord_ if you haven’t watched that show. This can also be a good refresher if you’ve forgotten some details since the [seventh and final season](https://www.polygon.com/star-wars/2020/1/22/21076822/star-wars-the-clone-wars-season-7-trailer-release-date/) of _The Clone Wars_ was released in 2020.

Maul had a really bad time after getting bisected by Obi-Wan Kenobi in [_The Phantom Menace_](https://www.polygon.com/star-wars/2019/5/18/18629598/phantom-menace-star-wars-prequels-expanded-universe-best-books-tv-series-comics/), going mad with rage and [building himself spider legs](https://www.polygon.com/darth-maul-spider-legs-star-wars/). He was rescued by his brother, the ridiculously named Savage Opress. The two worked together to get vengeance against Obi-Wan, teaming up with Death Watch, a splinter group of Mandalorians fed up with the planet’s pacifist leader, Duchess Satine.

Knowing he’d need more muscle to take Mandalore, Maul started recruiting from criminal organizations including the Black Sun, Pyke Syndicate, and the Hutt Clan. He united those gangs and the Nightbrothers, other Zabrak from Maul’s home planet of Dathomir, under the name the Shadow Collective. As Maul’s reach grew, so did his ambition. Maul saw taking control of Mandalore as a first step in setting up a power base that could rival that of his former master Darth Sidious aka Chancellor Palpatine.

    ![A group of Mandalorians and Zabrak climb a skyscraper in Maul Shadowlord](https://static0.polygonimages.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2026/04/maul_trailer1_still_1.jpg?q=49&fit=crop&w=825&dpr=2)Image: Lucasfilm/Disney

Death Watch’s leader Pre Vizsla turned on Maul and declared himself ruler of Mandalore, but Maul killed him in a duel and took the throne for himself. While some Mandalorians didn’t accept an outsider as their leader, Rook was one of the first to pledge her loyalty and paint her Beskar armor with Maul’s colors.

Maul’s holdings were repeatedly attacked by both the Jedi and Sith, with Sidious getting directly involved to kill Savage. Maul was ultimately defeated on Mandalore by the former Jedi Ahsoka Tano and her Clone Troopers. While Maul was being taken into Republic custody, Sidious executed his master plan and forced the clones to turn on the Jedi through [Order 66](https://www.polygon.com/2019/11/16/20967280/what-is-order-66-star-wars-jedi-survived-clone-wars-fallen-order/). Ahsoka set Maul free so he would create a distraction she could use to escape.

_Maul – Shadow Lord_ picks up soon after the events of _The Clone Wars_ season 7 and [_Revenge of the Sith_](https://www.polygon.com/star-wars-revenge-of-the-sith-annotated-novelization/). Palpatine has become emperor and killed most of the Jedi. The new Galactic Empire is looking to establish a new regime across the galaxy. Rook is still serving Maul, who has come to Janix along with a small group of Mandalorians and Zabrak to start rebuilding his empire and get revenge on the criminals he feels betrayed him in his hour of need. He also feels destiny is guiding him to find a new apprentice to follow the [Sith Rule of Two](https://www.polygon.com/star-wars/24171289/star-wars-sith-boring/) and once again challenge his former master’s power.
