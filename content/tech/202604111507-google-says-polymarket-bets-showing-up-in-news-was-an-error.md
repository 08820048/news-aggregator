Polymarket bets started [popping up in Google News](https://futurism.com/future-society/google-news-polymarket) alongside legitimate news articles. But now those results aren’t showing, and Google says they were never supposed to. Spokesperson Ned Adriance told _The Verge_ that “Google News is designed to show sources that create content about current issues, events, and important topics, and we have policies for sites to be eligible to appear. This site briefly appeared in Google News in error, and it is no longer surfacing in News.”

The links led directly to betting markets tied to specific news events. For instance, before the results were removed, _[Futurism](https://futurism.com/future-society/google-news-polymarket)_ searched “will ships transit the strait,” (the Strait of Hormuz), and just below credible sources like _The Guardian_ and _Reuters_ was a Polymarket bet on the specific number of ships that would be allowed to pass.

Initially, there was speculation that Google might be testing Polymarket results as part of an integration with the service. Polymarket and Kalshi have been [aggressively](https://www.theverge.com/business/881967/polymarket-kalshi-journalism-sponsorship-ad) pursuing partnerships with [journalists](https://www.theverge.com/news/897388/kalshi-polymarket-journalists-partnership-deals) and [news outlets](https://www.theverge.com/news/907942/fox-news-cuts-a-deal-with-kalshi), even [less reputable ones](https://www.theverge.com/news/851007/kalshi-and-polymarket-keep-partnering-with-fake-newsbreaker-accounts-on-x). It’s unclear how Polymarket ended up in Google News results, or when exactly it started. Though reports popped up on [social media](https://x.com/AnthonyHigman/status/2037160748107866476) as early as [January](https://bsky.app/profile/smartvpants.bsky.social/post/3mbjrgicqm22k).

Google has already partnered with both Kalshi and Polymarket to bring their data to [Google Finance](https://www.theverge.com/news/815300/google-finance-gets-ai-deep-search). Whether that deal and the results in Google News are related in any way is unclear. We’ve reached out to Google for additional information.
