Taking a break from calling Bruce Springsteen [a boring loser](https://truthsocial.com/@realDonaldTrump/116334990675013056) on Thursday, Donald Trump followed that up with [another Truth Social post](https://truthsocial.com/@realDonaldTrump/posts/116336247856387679) saying “loyal friend” Pam Bondi will transition to a yet-to-be-announced job in the private sector.

[CNN](https://www.cnn.com/2026/04/02/politics/pam-bondi-role-trump), the _[New York Times](https://www.nytimes.com/2026/04/02/us/politics/trump-fires-bondi-attorney-general.html?smid=url-share)_, and [Fox News](https://www.foxnews.com/politics/pam-bondi-already-fired-attorney-general-cabinet-official-teed-up-replacement-sources) all reported that Bondi, a figure in the [TikTok takeover](https://www.theverge.com/tiktok/697982/trump-tiktok-ban-apple-google-letters-pam-bondi), pressuring tech companies to [block ICE-tracking apps](https://www.theverge.com/news/840164/iceblock-joshua-aaron-sues-trump-administration-apple-app-store-removeal), and the [Epstein files release](https://www.theverge.com/policy/872092/jeffrey-epstein-files-doj-trump-musk-brin-gates), has been fired. Her departure closely follows the ouster of former Homeland Security secretary Kristi Noem [last month](https://www.theverge.com/policy/890196/kristi-noem-out-homeland-security), as deputy attorney general Todd Blanche will now serve as acting AG.

> [Donald Trump:](https://truthsocial.com/@realDonaldTrump/posts/116336247856387679)
> 
> Pam Bondi is a Great American Patriot and a loyal friend, who faithfully served as my Attorney General over the past year. Pam did a tremendous job overseeing a massive crackdown in Crime across our Country, with Murders plummeting to their lowest level since 1900.
> 
> We love Pam, and she will be transitioning to a much needed and important new job in the private sector, to be announced at a date in the near future, and our Deputy Attorney General, and a very talented and respected Legal Mind, Todd Blanche, will step in to serve as Acting Attorney General. Thank you for your attention to this matter!
