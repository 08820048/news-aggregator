$250

The Quest 3S can play the same games as the pricier [Meta Quest 3](https://www.theverge.com/23906313/meta-quest-3-review-vr-mixed-reality-headset), with _Batman: Arkham Shadow_ and _Maestro_ performing impressively well in our testing. Additionally, you can stream Xbox games with a Game Pass subscription and even wirelessly tether it to a gaming PC to play SteamVR games like _Half-Life: Alyx_. The Quest 3S is powered by the same Qualcomm Snapdragon XR2 Gen 2 processor that’s in the pricier Quest 3, although its display resolution and optics quality are on-par with the Quest 22. It features full-color passthrough, allowing you to see your surroundings without removing the headset. And, to top it off, it comes with the Quest 3’s Touch Plus controllers.
