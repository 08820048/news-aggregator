The creator economy is evolving fast, and ad revenue alone isn’t cutting it anymore. [YouTubers are launching product lines](https://techcrunch.com/2026/02/10/youtubers-arent-relying-on-ad-revenue-anymore-heres-how-some-are-diversifying/), acquiring startups, and building actual business empires. In fact, MrBeast’s company [bought fintech startup Step](https://techcrunch.com/2026/02/09/mrbeasts-company-buys-gen-z-focused-fintech-app-step/), and his chocolate business is outearning his media arm. This isn’t just one creator’s strategy. For many, it’s the new playbook. 

On this episode of TechCrunch’s [Equity](https://techcrunch.com/podcasts/equity/) podcast, hosts Kirsten Korosec, Anthony Ha, and Rebecca Bellan unpack how creators are diversifying beyond ads, whether their model can scale beyond the top 1%, everything happing at [India’s AI Impact Summit](https://techcrunch.com/2026/02/16/all-the-important-news-from-the-ongoing-india-ai-summit/), and more of the week’s headlines.
