Pre-Series A founders, this is your moment! If your startup is ready to be tested under real pressure, the battlefield is open.

This is your shot to step into the arena at [**TechCrunch Disrupt 2026**](https://techcrunch.com/events/tc-disrupt-2026/?utm_source=tc&utm_medium=post&utm_campaign=BF200&utm_content=sb200nominations&promo=post_sb200applicationsreminder_02202026&display=) and go head-to-head in front of world-class VCs and the full TechCrunch audience.

## Where breakout companies are forged

You’ll compete for **$100,000 in equity-free funding**, earn global exposure on the main stage at [**Disrupt**](https://techcrunch.com/events/tc-disrupt-2026/?utm_source=tc&utm_medium=post&utm_campaign=BF200&utm_content=sb200nominations&promo=post_sb200applicationsreminder_02202026&display=) in San Francisco, and get raw, unfiltered feedback from the investors who shape the future of tech. In addition, you’ll join an elite cohort of founders preparing to take on the biggest startup stage in the world.

Startup Battlefield alumni include Trello, Mint, Dropbox, Discord, and Fitbit — [and more than 1,500 startups](https://techcrunch.com/startup-battlefield/company/?status=all&pg=1) that once stood exactly where you are now: early, hungry, and ready to prove it.

Make your mark or get left behind. [**Nominate now.**](https://techcrunch.com/startup-battlefield/)

## What it takes to enter the arena

We’re looking for bold, early-stage founders with an MVP and a vision that challenges the status quo. If you’re bootstrapped, pre-seed, or seed-funded, this battlefield was built for you. Series A startups in capital-intensive industries may also qualify.

## Start the battle today

Nominations close on June 8, but champions don’t wait for the last call. Prepare early. Nominate early. [**Enter Startup Battlefield 200**](https://techcrunch.com/startup-battlefield/) and fight for your place on tech’s biggest stage.
