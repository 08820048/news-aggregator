Xbox is getting ready to launch its Gaming Copilot AI assistant on “current-generation consoles” this year, according to [a report from _GamesRadar_](https://www.gamesradar.com/games/xbox-just-revealed-gaming-copilot-is-coming-to-current-generation-consoles-later-this-year/). Sonali Yadav, Xbox’s product manager for gaming AI, revealed the news during a panel at the Game Developers Conference (GDC), adding that the company will also add the assistant to “more services that players are playing.”

Microsoft has been working on its [gaming-focused Copilot assistant](https://www.theverge.com/news/628666/microsoft-xbox-copilot-for-gaming) for months now, with the company launching it in beta on the Xbox mobile app, [Windows 11](https://www.theverge.com/news/719668/microsoft-xbox-gaming-copilot-windows-game-bar-beta-test), and [Xbox Ally handhelds](https://www.theverge.com/news/682011/microsoft-windows-xbox-pc-combination-features-rog-xbox-ally-devices). Players can use their voice to call upon the Gaming Copilot if they get stuck in a game, and the AI assistant is supposed to reply with suggestions about what to do next. It can also answer questions about a player’s gaming history, offer up tips or strategies, and provide game recommendations. In [examples shared by Xbox](https://go.skimresources.com/?id=1025X1701640&xs=1&url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.xbox.com%2Fen-US%2Fgaming-copilot), players can ask Gaming Copilot how to beat a particular boss or which materials they need to craft a sword in _Minecraft_.

Though Yadav didn’t confirm which consoles that Copilot is coming to, the company’s current lineup includes the Xbox Series X | S. It also [has a Project Helix Xbox in the pipeline](https://www.theverge.com/games/893119/xbox-project-helix-jason-ronald-gdc-2026) — the company’s first major launch in years without [former Xbox chief Phil Spencer at the helm](https://www.theverge.com/news/882241/microsoft-phil-spencer-xbox-leaving-retirement). Asha Sharma, Microsoft’s previous head of AI development for enterprise teams, took [over as CEO of Microsoft Gaming in February](https://www.theverge.com/games/882326/read-microsoft-gaming-ceo-asha-sharma-first-memo), while former Xbox president Sarah Bond also left. Project Helix isn’t expected to reach alpha until 2027 and [will play PC games, too](https://www.theverge.com/games/890194/microsoft-teases-its-next-xbox-says-project-helix-will-play-pc-games-too).
