Prediction platform Polymarket has been on a partnership spree — a trend that continued this week, with a new deal involving Major League Baseball.

MLB [announced Thursday](https://www.mlb.com/press-release/press-release-mlb-names-polymarket-exclusive-prediction-market-exchange-partner-and-signs-agreement-with-cftc-to-establish-iintegrity-framework) that Polymarket would be its official prediction market partner, a designation that will allow the site to gain exclusive access to official league data, as well as to use MLB logos in its products. The site will also receive “brand exposure” at games and through the league’s digital channels, the announcement notes.

At the same time, MLB also signed a memo of understanding with Commodity Futures Trading Commission (CFTC) chairman Michael Selig. The memo states that the agency, which is tasked with overseeing prediction market sites, and the league will share information with one another related to baseball and prediction markets.

Previously, MLB officials had expressed concern for how prediction markets might impact sports audiences. In [a letter](https://closingline.substack.com/p/mlb-statement-sports-betting-to-cftc) to the CFTC sent last year, the league noted the need for an integrity framework that could regulate how prediction markets impact the game. MLB officials have framed the new Polymarket partnership as a way to ensure the enforcement of integrity standards.

Once [banned from operation](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/14/how-prediction-markets-work.html) in the U.S., Polymarket has been on a hot streak [ever since September](https://www.reuters.com/sustainability/boards-policy-regulation/polymarket-receives-green-signal-cftc-us-return-2025-09-03/), when the CFTC eased previous regulatory roadblocks allowing the company to operate stateside. Since then, the company has announced major data partnerships with tech giants like [Google](https://www.nbcnews.com/business/consumer/google-kalshi-polymarket-prediction-markets-rcna242670) and [Palantir](https://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20260310736467/en/Polymarket-Partners-With-Palantir-and-TWG-AI-to-Build-Next-generation-Sports-Integrity-Platform); sports partnerships with [Major League Soccer](https://www.mlssoccer.com/news/mls-enters-multi-year-partnership-with-polymarket) and [Major League Hockey](https://www.nhl.com/news/nhl-announces-landmark-multiyear-partnerships-with-kalshi-polymarket#:~:text=NHL%20announces%20landmark%20multiyear%20partnerships%20with%20Kalshi%2C%20Polymarket,-By%20NHL%20Public&text=NEW%20YORK%20%E2%80%93%20The%20National%20Hockey,market%20partners%20of%20the%20NHL.); and media companies.
